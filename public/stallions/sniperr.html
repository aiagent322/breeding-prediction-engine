Full Profile

Sniperr -- 2019 Red Roan Stallion. By High Brow Cat (High Brow Hickory x Smart Little Kitty/Smart Little Lena/Doc's Hickory -- QData and NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Sire PE $97M+; deceased October 2019; ICSI only at Valley Equine; sire of Metallic Cat, Bet Hesa Cat, NRR Cat King Cole, Badboonarising and many others in this database; the foundation of the modern cutting horse industry through his sons and daughters) out of Gini One Time (One Time Pepto x -- One Time Pepto is by Peptoboonsmal x One Time Soon, a leading NCHA cutting sire; dam produce record for Gini One Time not further confirmed in available sources; the One Time Pepto connection adds the Peptoboonsmal/Peppy San Badger foundation to the High Brow Cat top line). Valley Equine (formerly Brazos Valley Stallion Station), Stephenville TX. Stud fee $3,500 confirmed Valley Equine 2026.

Source: https://valleyequinerepro.com/stallions/ LTE $216,536 confirmed valleyequinerepro.com. Confirmed show record: 2023 Abilene Spectacular Champion; 2023 NCHA Summer Spectacular Non-Pro Champion. The Abilene Spectacular is a premier NCHA regional event bringing together top cutting horses for open and non-pro competition across multiple go-rounds. Winning the championship at this level in 2023 establishes Sniperr as an active and competitive performer at the age when most stallions are just beginning their breeding careers, giving breeders the rare combination of a proven show record and elite pedigree depth.

The High Brow Cat x One Time Pepto cross pairs two of the most influential cutting horse sire lines of the modern era. High Brow Cat with PE $97M+ is the #2 All-Time Leading NCHA sire and the foundation of the Cat bloodline that dominates cutting horse breeding globally. One Time Pepto through Gini One Time brings the Peptoboonsmal and Peppy San Badger line that has produced elite cow horse and cutting talent for decades. The combination creates horses with exceptional cow sense from both sides of the pedigree -- High Brow Cat athleticism and natural cattle reading ability paired with the Peptoboonsmal line intelligence and competitive fire that has produced champions across all three phases of reined cow horse and cutting competition.

No offspring show data confirmed in available sources -- Sniperr is a 2019 stallion with first foal crops still young. His 2023 NCHA championship credentials combined with Valley Equine placement and exceptional pedigree depth establish him as a legitimate breeding prospect. Breeders seeking to access both the High Brow Cat and Peptoboonsmal pedigree lines simultaneously will find Sniperr an efficient cross on mares that lack either bloodline.
