Performance Summary

Hottish Metal 'Mike' -- 2018 Sorrel Stallion. By Hottish (Spots Hot x Stylish Play Lena/Docs Stylish Oak -- PE $23.8M+, 2025 QData #3 and 2024 NCHA #4 Leading Sire, NCHA Top 12 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, Equi-Stat Leading Cutting Junior Sire when first foals showed, died February 2020 -- full profile bvss005) out of Show Me The Rey (Metallic Cat x Nurse Moss/Dual Pep -- NCHA money-earner confirmed 7ranch.com; dam of Hottish Metal; Nurse Moss [Show Me The Rey's dam by Dual Pep] LTE $20,954: South Point Winter Limited Open Derby Reserve Champion, Rocky Mountain Non-Pro Derby Reserve Champion; Jay Moss [Nurse Moss granddam]: dam of 16 money-earners $681,028+ including High Brows Nurse $138,460 [NCHA Non-Pro Futurity Reserve Champion, NCHA Open Super Stakes Reserve Champion], Rey Dual $123,449, Dual Rey $105,038 [QData/NCHA #3 All-Time Leading Sire]). Valley Equine 2026 lineup describes Hottish as 'NCHA Reserve Horse of the Year Hottish Metal' confirming that distinction. NCHA Hall of Fame 2023. Owned by Jim Good. Open rider: Adan Banuelos. Non-Pro rider: Ali Good (Jim's daughter) -- QHN: 'I've been the only one to ride him since he was 2 years-old, as far as getting him ready for whoever to work. I've done that my whole life with every one of my horses.' Retired 2024 completely sound. ICSI Only. Stud fee $5,500. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $497,370 (Valley Equine 2026 -- most current confirmed). Became the highest-earning son of Hottish, surpassing Dual Reyish's previously held record. QHN retirement article (April 2024): Ali Good marked 225 at 2024 NCHA Super Stakes Non-Pro Classic finals, earning $20,417 and pushing LTE to $472,809 at that moment -- surpassing Dual Reyish by almost $20,000. Continued earning to reach $497,370 per Valley Equine 2026 listing. QHN: 'Their most recent goal has been to make Hottish Metal the highest earning son of the late $16 Million Sire Hottish. Dual Reyish has held that title for the seven years since Hottish's first foal crop. Now, after marking a 225 score in the finals and taking home a check for $20,417, Hottish Metal's total lifetime earnings came to $472,809. That is almost $20,000 over Dual Reyish's show record.' NCHA Reserve Horse of the Year. Stallion Register: 'His power, quickness and sensitivity to a cow set him apart.' Full confirmed show record: 2021 NCHA Open Futurity 3rd; 2022 Breeders Invitational Open Derby Champion; 2022 The Ike Open Derby Champion; 2022 NCHA Super Stakes Open Derby 3rd; 2022 PCCHA Open Derby 3rd; 2022 ICHA Open Derby Reserve Champion; Cattlemens Derby Open finalist; West Texas Derby Open finalist; Brazos Bash Open Derby Reserve Champion; Cotton Stakes Open Champion; Idaho Futurity Open Champion; 2024 NCHA Super Stakes Classic Non-Pro Champion (scored 225 with Ali Good, $20,417 -- retirement run). QHN: 'We weren't going to push him to do something that he wasn't capable or healthy enough for. And we weren't going to keep showing him until she's seven or eight years old to get that, it just worked out well enough that we were able to get it early on.' No confirmed PE yet -- first foals not yet of showing age as of 2026. Hottish Metal stands as one of the most decorated sons of the late Hottish and one of the most accomplished Hottish x Metallic Cat cross performers in the modern NCHA era, a pedigree combination that concentrates Spots Hot athleticism with Metallic Cat cow sense and intelligence.
