Performance Summary

Metallic Rey Mink -- 2016 Red Roan Stallion. By Metallic Cat (High Brow Cat x Chers Shadow/Peptoboonsmal -- LTE $637,711, QData/NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, PE $83.6M+ -- full profile in database) out of Dual Rey Mink (Dual Rey x -- 2019 NCHA Top 10 Leading Dam of Cutting Money-Earners confirmed Performance Horse Central; by Dual Rey, QData/NCHA #3 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire PE $57.9M+). Owned by Los Jaboncillos Ranch, Premont TX. Trained and shown by Austin Shepard, EquiStat $10M+ Rider. Joined Valley Equine for the 2025 breeding season -- confirmed Valley Equine announcement September 2024. Stud fee $4,000. Foals eligible: Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive, Breeders Invitational, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive, NCHA Super Stakes, PCCHA, Abilene Spectacular, Royal Crown Roping, Chrome Cash. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $205,224. #1 All-Age Money Earner in 2019 -- confirmed Los Jaboncillos Ranch. 2019 NCHA Open Futurity Champion -- scored 226, confirmed multiple sources. 2020 NCHA Senior Open Champion. 2020 West Texas Open Derby Finalist. 2020 Waco Derby Open Finalist. Austin Shepard: 'He was not only a great athlete, but he was as smart about a cow as any horse I have ever seen.' Valley Equine September 2024: 'Known for his impeccable natural cow sense and cutting style, this stallion is set to make a remarkable impact on the future of the industry.' First foal crop producing historic results. 2025 NCHA Open Futurity: Minkish (x Blackish/Metallic Rebel, for Jackpot Farm and Ellen Carter) scored 226 under Beau Galyean, earned $138,000 in Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive payouts -- confirmed Valley Equine December 2025. Valley Equine: 'His finals score matched the 226 his sire earned in his own Futurity year, a fitting milestone for the first crop.' One of Jackpot Farm's connections said: 'They always said Mink was special and so willing, even as a two year old, and watching that trait pass on has been so meaningful.' 2026 NCHA Super Stakes confirmed offspring champions: Red N Milds (Open Derby Champion, scored 223.5, $77,514.70 for Wheeler Dealer Cutting Horses LP with Michael Cooper -- led both go-rounds and the semifinals before winning the finals); Tessa Rey (Amateur Derby Champion, scored 222, $14,674.55 for Crow Performance Horses LLC with Trystan Crow); Mazeratis Mink (Limited Amateur Co-Champion, scored 212, $5,384.87 for Dwayne and Amber Haselip with Ashtyn Haselip); Matchbox (Limited Amateur Co-Reserve Champion with owner-rider Marcie Campbell). Valley Equine April 2026: 'Another title from Metallic Rey Mink's first crop made it clear that Red N Milds was not a one-off story.' Four champions from a single major event, all from the first foal crop -- one of the most dominant debut sire performances in recent NCHA history. The Metallic Cat x Dual Rey Mink cross stacks the two deepest sire lines in modern cutting -- #2 All-Time (Metallic Cat) on top and #3 All-Time (Dual Rey) on bottom.
