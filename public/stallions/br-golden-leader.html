BR Golden Leader Taxi — 2013 Gray. By Confederate Leader (LTE $22,381, BFA World Barrel Derby Champion, sire of Epic Leader $171K and Dashing Dillon $115K — Epic Leader passes speed, athleticism and mental edge to colts; Epic Leader top earner CP He Will Be Epic $629K) out of KN Docs Gray Gold Cricket (Groanies Gray Pony): EquiStat $127,675, 41 Barrel Points, BFA World Barrel Futurity 3rd, Old Fort Days Derby Reserve Champion, WPRA Sheridan Rodeo Reserve Champion, Lance Graves Pro Classic 1D Champion, consistently top 30 WPRA World Standings for many years, AQHA Open ROM. Cricket dam Docs Smooth Gold: $201,571 producer, 7 earners. LTE $58,000+, 16 AQHA points.

Owned by Baker Ranch, Garland Ray Baker Jr, Russellville AR. Ridden by Whitney Davison (barrels) and Cole Davison (roping). Show record: 2017 Ardmore Open Barrel Futurity Champion, 2018 4th High Point Barrel Stallion, WPRA Futurity Round Winner, American Rodeo Semi-Finals Contender, Finalist Diamonds and Dirt Futurity and Derby, BFA Futurity and Derby, OKC Derby, AQHA ROM. First foal crop: $300,000+ across team roping, barrel racing, breakaway and tie-down roping.

Top offspring: BR Golden Kat Olena (ARHFA Limited Heading Futurity World Champion + Intermediate Heading Reserve), BR Call A Uber (3rd ARHFA Open Heading Futurity), Saylors Packing Fame (top-5 NBHA Finals barrel racing). Offspring: remarkable versatility and easy-going attitude. Elite outcross for barrel racing, produces versatile winners across roping and timed-event disciplines. Stud fee $3,000.
