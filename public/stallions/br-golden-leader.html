Performance Summary

BR Golden Leader 'Taxi' -- 2013 Gray. AQHA registered. 6 Panel N/N. Color tested AA Ee N/G -- a consistent gray carrier passing the distinctive coloring alongside the speed genetics of his Special Effort SI 104 foundation. By Confederate Leader (Special Leader x Some Kinda Man/Southern Streaker -- BFA Derby Champion, confirmed leading barrel sire; sire of Epic Leader, LTE $171,080, No. 1 Money Earning Futurity Stallion 2012, 296 money earners, PE $7M+, 11th All-Time Leading Barrel Sire, ranked #6 among all sires of barrel money earners in 2025; Confederate Leader passes exceptional speed through the Special Effort SI 104 and Special Leader SI 103 line and documented athletic ability to his get across barrel racing and roping disciplines) out of KN Docs Gray Gold 'Cricket' (Groanies Gray Pony x Docs Smooth Gold/Smooth Herman -- EquiStat LTE $127,675, AQHA Open ROM, 41 AQHA barrel racing points; confirmed ProRodeo money-earner ranked consistently in the Top 30 WPRA World Standings; confirmed titles: BFA World Barrel Futurity 3rd, Frostbite Futurity Slot Race Reserve Champion, Old Fort Days Derby Reserve Champion, WPRA Sheridan Wyoming Rodeo Reserve Champion, Lance Graves Pro Classic 1D Champion; Docs Smooth Gold dam line confirmed multiple earners). Owned by Garland Ray Baker Jr. of Baker Ranch, Russellville, Arkansas. Shown in barrels by Whitney Davison; husband Cole Davison competes in team roping on BR Golden Leader offspring. Stud fee $3,000. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $58,000+. Confirmed show record: 2017 Ardmore Open Barrel Futurity Champion; 2018 4th High Point Barrel Stallion; WPRA Futurity Round Winner; American Rodeo Semi-Finals Contender; finalist in Diamonds and Dirt Futurity and Derby, BFA Futurity and Derby, and OKC Derby. Baker Ranch confirms he has won with multiple riders at the Futurity, Derby, and Open ranks. Still competing with Whitney Davison in 2026 in barrel racing while offspring by Cole Davison compete in team roping -- giving the stallion documented dual-arena credentials through the same family. Confederate Leader's sire line is confirmed elite through Epic Leader: bred by Kassie Mowry off Confederate Leader x Firewater Fiesta (WPRA Reserve World Champion, $850K+ LTE by Fire Water Flit), Epic Leader won $171,080, was the 2012 No. 1 Money Earning Futurity Stallion, and has since sired $7M+ in progeny earnings across 296 money earners -- ranking 11th All-Time Leading Barrel Sire and 6th in 2025 annual rankings. Team Roping Journal on BR Golden Leader: 'An elite outcross for barrel racing, BR Golden Leader produces versatile winners across roping and timed-event disciplines.' Baker Ranch: 'An outcross with proven barrel racing line on both sides of his pedigree, his genetics are a great addition to anyone's program.' The combination of Confederate Leader speed and KN Docs Gray Gold's ProRodeo barrel credentials creates a genuine outcross for the dominant barrel pedigrees -- Blazin Jetolena, Dash Ta Fame, Tres Seis, and Frenchmans Guy mares dominate the barrel industry and BR Golden Leader crosses on all of them without duplication. First foal crop confirmed results: total offspring earnings exceeded $300,000 across team roping, barrel racing, breakaway roping, and tie-down roping -- confirmed Team Roping Journal 2026. BR Golden Kat Olena: 2025 ARHFA Limited Heading Futurity World Champion AND Intermediate Heading Reserve Champion -- the ARHFA is the American Rope Horse Futurity Association, the premier judged roping futurity with Trevor Brazile and Kaleb Driggers among regular competitors. BR Call A Uber: 3rd ARHFA Open Heading Futurity (ridden by Kaleb Driggers, 15-time PRCA World Champion). Saylors Packing Fame: Top-5 NBHA Finals barrel racing finish. These three offspring alone produced $45,000+ in a single October weekend at the ARHFA Finals -- confirmed by Team Roping Journal. Whitney Davison: 'I'm really looking forward to the future with a bunch of his babies hitting the arena.' Enrolled in Ruby Buckle, Select Stallion, Colorado Classic, Breeder's Challenge, Blue Collar, Future Fortunes, Gold Buckle, Royal Crown, Triple Crown 100, Valley Girls Stallion Incentive, and Young Guns League.
