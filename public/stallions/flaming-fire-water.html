Performance Summary

Flaming Fire Water -- 2001 Palomino Stallion. By Fire Water Flit (Flit Bar x Slash J Harletta -- Nation's Leading Maternal Barrel Sire of the Decade; PE $2.4M+ barrel progeny; leading barrel horse sire with many years on leading sire lists; standing at Firewater Creek Ranch, Collinsville TX) out of My Easter Flame (Flaming Jet x -- #6 Producing Broodmare of the Decade confirmed Future Fortunes; grand dam Otoe Glo Miss is producer of multiple NFR Qualifiers). Owned by Harriett Martin Upton. Stud fee $2,500 confirmed Future Fortunes listing at Valley Equine. Foals eligible: Breeders Challenge, Ruby Buckle, Future Fortunes, Select Stallion Stakes, Blue Collar Breeders, Royal Crown, Triple Crown 100, TQHA, Colorado Classic, Tomorrows Legends, Legends of the South, Top Shelf Breeders, VGBRA, Diamond Classic. NOT part of cutting Super Stallion Incentive. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Primary discipline: barrel racing exclusively. LTE $14,315 and 100 AQHA Points (Valley Equine 2026 confirmed). PE $769,696 (Valley Equine 2026 confirmed). Confirmed show record from Future Fortunes listing: AQHA Superior Barrel Horse; six-time AQHA World Show Top Ten; 2 Performance ROMs; Futurity, Derby and Open Money Earner. Top confirmed offspring: Chewingthehotwire -- proven performer at the professional level per Valley Equine 2026. Valley Equine 2026: 'Flaming Fire Water has become a consistent sire of competitive barrel horses. His offspring include standouts such as Chewingthehotwire, a proven performer at the professional level, along with additional futurity and open earners competing across multiple levels of the sport. His influence spans major futurities, slot races, and open competition, with performers known for speed, rate, and consistency in the arena.' Fire Water Flit sire line is one of the foundational barrel racing dynasties in the industry -- the Flit Bar / Slash J Harletta cross produced what became known as a dynasty of barrel horse producers. My Easter Flame as #6 Producing Broodmare of the Decade adds strong maternal depth through Flaming Jet and grand dam Otoe Glo Miss whose NFR qualifier produce confirms the quality of the female line.
