Bet He Sparks -- 2016 Sorrel Stallion. By Bet Hesa Cat (High Brow Cat x Miss Silver Pistol/Freckles Playboy -- LTE $272,151; 2001 NCHA Open World Champion; NRCHA Million Dollar Sire; PE $25,000,000+; standing at Oswood Stallion Station as anchor sire of the station's cow horse program) out of Sparking Train (Shining Spark x -- LTE $93,487; PE $602,273 confirmed Gold Buckle Futurities; Shining Spark is a leading NRHA and NRCHA sire with PE $25M+; the Bet Hesa Cat x Shining Spark cross pairs two NRCHA Million Dollar Sire lines -- Bet Hesa Cat through High Brow Cat and Shining Spark through his foundational NRHA and NRCHA influence -- creating offspring bred to excel across the full spectrum of western performance disciplines). Owned by Shaleah Hester. Oswood Stallion Station, Weatherford TX. LTE $308,539 confirmed Oswood roster. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/bet-he-sparks/ Confirmed show record from Gold Buckle Futurities and Oswood records: 2024 American Performance Horseman Team Division Working Cow Horse Champion; 2024 American Performance Horseman Team Champion; 2023 World's Greatest Horseman Finalist; 2023 World's Greatest Horseman Preliminary Go-Round Champion Herd Work; 2023 World's Greatest Horseman Preliminary Go-Round Champion Rein Work; 2023 World's Greatest Horseman Preliminary Go-Round Champion Steer Stopping; 2022 NRCHA Derby Open Bridle Spectacular Champion; 2022 NRCHA Open Derby Champion; 2021 NRCHA Open Hackamore Classic Champion; 2020 NRCHA Derby Two-Rein Spectacular Open Champion; 2019 NRCHA Open Derby Champion; 2019 NRCHA Hackamore Classic Open Champion; 2018 NRCHA Derby Reserve Open Champion. Arena record holder in the steer stopping event of the World's Greatest Horseman. Bet He Sparks has built one of the deepest and most sustained competitive records of any stallion in the Oswood program. His titles span the snaffle, hackamore, two-rein, and bridle phases of reined cow horse competition, demonstrating the full-spectrum ability that separates truly elite horses from good performers. The World's Greatest Horseman Go-Round Championships in three separate phases -- herd work, rein work, and steer stopping -- on the same horse is an exceptional achievement reflecting complete mastery of all cow horse disciplines simultaneously. The 2024 American Performance Horseman Team Division Working Cow Horse Championship demonstrated his continued elite ability well into his aged-horse career. The Bet Hesa Cat x Shining Spark cross concentrates two of the most commercially important NRCHA sire lines available. Bet Hesa Cat (2001 NCHA Open World Champion; $25M+ PE; NRCHA Million Dollar Sire) provides the High Brow Cat cow sense foundation that has defined elite reined cow horse breeding for two decades. Sparking Train's sire Shining Spark ($25M+ PE; NRHA and NRCHA leader) adds the athleticism and natural collection required to compete in the rein work and fence work phases of the sport. Together these two sire lines in a single individual have produced one of the most versatile and decorated cow horse careers of his generation, with Bet He Sparks standing as the premier example of what this cross can achieve when both sides of the pedigree are represented by elite producers.
