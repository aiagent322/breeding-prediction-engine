Full Profile

Spots Hot -- 2001 Sorrel Stallion. By Chula Dual (Dual Pep x Smart Fancy Lena/Smart Little Lena -- LTE $171,134; 1998 NCHA Futurity 4th (tie) 3YO Open; 1999 Polo Ranch Classic 4YO Open Champion; 1999 Royal Santana Futurity 4YO Open Co-Champion; 1999 El Cid Futurity 4YO Open Reserve Champion; sire of offspring $2M+, Spots Hot his leading money-winner; by Dual Pep -- PE $26M+, three-quarter brother to Peptoboonsmal, both foundational sires of the modern cutting horse) out of Sweet Shorty Lena (Shorty Lena x -- LTE $114,276; 2000 Steamboat Springs Derby 4YO NP Reserve Champion; 2002 Wyoming Futurity 5/6YO NP Reserve Champion; dam of 11 money-earners, produce $1.1M+ including Spots Hot, Innovation Time $195,686, His Reyflection $134,652; Shorty Lena is a son of Doc O'Lena and sire of mares that have produced $13M+ including NCHA Futurity Open Champions Spots Hot, Oh Cay Felix, One Time Royalty, and Oh Miss Caroline -- all four sired by stallions out of Smart Little Lena mares, confirming a powerful linebreeding pattern to Doc O'Lena). NCHA Hall of Fame 2009. Owned by Bobby Patton, Rocking P Ranch, Weatherford TX -- same ownership as Metallic Cat.

Laid to rest 2022. ICSI only. Ranks top 20 of EquiStat's All-Time Cutting Horses. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX.

LTE $527,682. 2004 NCHA Open Futurity Champion. 7 Major Event Championships. NCHA: 'With his superior athleticism, he could toy with and trap a cow like no other, making him a force to be reckoned with in the show pen.' Full confirmed show record: 2004 NCHA Open Futurity 3YO Open Champion; 2004 NCHA Non-Pro Futurity 4th; 2005 South Point Futurity 4YO Open Champion; 2005 Memphis Futurity 4YO Open Champion; 2005 Great American Competition 4YO Open Champion; 2005 Augusta Futurity 4YO NP Reserve Champion; 2005 NCHA Summer Spectacular 3rd 4YO Open; 2005 PCCHA Futurity 4YO Open finalist; 2005 Abilene Spectacular 4YO NP finalist; 2005 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Open semifinalist; 2006 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5/6YO NP Champion; 2007 Memphis Futurity 5/6YO NP Champion; 2007 NCHA Super Stakes 5/6YO NP Reserve Champion; 2007 NCHA Summer Spectacular 3rd 5/6YO NP; 2007 Brazos Bash 5/6YO NP finalist.

The pedigree carries a significant linebreeding to Doc O'Lena through both Chula Dual (Smart Little Lena on dam side) and Sweet Shorty Lena (Shorty Lena, direct Doc O'Lena son). QHN In The Blood on Dual Reyish: 'This cross returns the blood of Doc O'Lena through the foal's dam and the sire's dam -- that increases the influence of the sire's dam on the foal.' PE $9,419,211 (Valley Equine 2026 -- most current). As sire: became an NCHA Futurity Open winning sire in 2016 when Second Spot won the title, giving him NCHA Futurity wins as both a performer and a sire. NCHA announcement at time of death: 'With just shy of seven million, eight hundred thousand dollars in progeny earnings, he sired household names such as Second Spot, Hottish, and Cinca Im Hot.' PE has grown posthumously to $9.4M+ as of 2025.

Top confirmed offspring: Hottish ($301,843, 2012 NCHA Reserve Horse of the Year, PE $23.8M+, 2025 QData #3 Leading Sire -- Spots Hot's greatest legacy); Second Spot ($449,607, 2016 NCHA Open Futurity Champion); Cinca Im Hot ($265,969); Tattoos On This Town ($194,130, 2013 Breeders Invitational 4YO NP Reserve Champion); Innovation Time ($195,686); Harley ($205,168, 2014 AQHA Amateur Cutting World Champion, 2010 NCHA Summer Spectacular 4YO NP Champion, 2010 Breeders Invitational 4YO NP Champion); Some Like It Hott ($183,223, 2010 NCHA Futurity Open Reserve Champion); High Classed Hottie ($158,412); His Reyflection ($134,652). Spots Hot's legacy lives most powerfully through Hottish, whose 26 offspring earnings of $100K+ and PE $23.8M+ are a direct extension of the athleticism, cow sense, and trainability that Spots Hot established as a performer and passed on as a sire.
