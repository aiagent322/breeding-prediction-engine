The 2015 AQHA World Championship in Junior Heading is one of the most prestigious annual awards in the team roping world, recognizing the top heading horse across all breeds and ages at the AQHA World Show in Oklahoma City.

Winning the Junior Heading World Championship requires consistent excellence across multiple go-rounds under different judges, demonstrating that CSR Dual Glo possesses the athleticism, trainability, cow sense, and competitive consistency to perform at the highest level of team roping competition. The combination of 371 AQHA points across multiple events further confirms his versatility across disciplines -- an unusual asset in a stallion that pairs the Dual Rey cutting and cow horse pedigree with genuine team roping world championship credentials on the performance record. The Dual Rey x Bobby Lewis dam cross is rare in the breeding world. Dual Rey with PE $57M+ brings the elite cutting and cow horse genetics that have dominated western performance horse breeding for two decades.

The Bobby Lewis dam side contributes the heading athleticism, ground-covering stride, and natural cow sense under the horn that defines an elite heading horse in team roping. The combination creates offspring with the versatility to compete across team roping, reined cow horse, ranch riding, and cutting -- making CSR Dual Glo an unusually flexible cross for breeders wanting a stallion that works across multiple western performance disciplines. At $1,500 stud fee he represents significant pedigree depth at an accessible price point for breeders seeking Dual Rey access without the premium fees commanded by his more famous full siblings and half-siblings.
