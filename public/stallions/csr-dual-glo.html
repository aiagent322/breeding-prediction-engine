Performance Summary

CSR Dual Glo -- Stallion. By Dual Rey (Peptoboonsmal x Nurse Rey/Nu Rey -- PE $57M+, QData #7 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire -- full profile bvss001) out of a mare shown by Bobby Lewis (LTE $61,802; 1,006 AQHA points; AQHA World Champion Heading; AQHA All-Around High Point Champion; AQHA High Point Junior Horse; Superior Tie-Down Roping; National High Point Senior Working Cow Horse, Junior Tie-Down, Heading and Heeling Champion; AQHA Performance Champion Superior Tie-Down, Working Cow Horse, Heading and Heeling; dam name not confirmed in available sources). Dam also produced 2017 AQHA World Champion Jr Heading J Lows Glo, 2017 AQHA World Champion Heeling Dual Patron, and CSR Lay Down Sally -- all full siblings confirmed Bobby Lewis Quarter Horses: 'All of these World Champions are full siblings.' Shown by Bobby Lewis. Stud fee $1,500 fresh contracts only confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup. Foals eligible: Riata Buckle Stallion, Pitzer Ranch HPI Eligible, IQHA Ranch Horse Program, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive. NOT part of the cutting Super Stallion Incentive program. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. CSR Dual Glo represents a distinct discipline profile within the Valley Equine roster -- primarily a team roping, ranch horse, and versatile performance stallion rather than a cutting or cow horse specialist. His Dual Rey sire line provides the athleticism and cow sense that crosses well into versatile ranch horse and roping programs. His dam family is exceptional in the AQHA versatile performance world, producing four world champions across heading and heeling -- a depth of production rarely seen from one mare. No confirmed dollar LTE -- his record is documented in AQHA points and championship titles. Confirmed show record from Valley Equine stallion page: 2015 AQHA World Champion Junior Heading; 371 AQHA points in multiple events; 2015/2016 Fort Worth Stock Show AQHA Heading Reserve Champion; 2015 AQHA High Point Stallion Open Heeling; 2015 AQHA High Point Stallion Open Ranch Riding; 2014 NRCHA Celebration of Champions Intermediate Open and Limited Open Derby World Champion; 2014 SRCHA Fall Derby Limited Open Champion and Open Reserve Champion; 2014 SRCHA RodetoReno Limited Open Derby Champion and Intermediate Open Reserve Champion. Bobby Lewis: 'In 2015 CSR Dual Glo and Bobby won the AQHA World Champion Jr Heading.' Offspring include Sevens Star Glo (Valley Equine stallion -- sired by CSR Dual Glo out of Sevens Tootsie Time) earning in roping and ranch horse competition per Valley Equine 2026 lineup.
