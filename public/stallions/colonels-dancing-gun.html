Performance Summary

Colonels Dancing Gun -- 2012 Gray Stallion. By Colonels Smoking Gun (Colonel Freckles x Smoke N Impressive/Impressive -- AQHA Hall of Fame; NRHA $1 Million Sire; also sire of Gunnatrashya osw001 and Americasnexttopgun osw004 in this database; one of the most prolific reining sire lines of the modern era) out of Dance Little Spook (Smart Spook x -- Smart Spook is a leading NRHA sire and NRHA Hall of Fame inductee with PE $12M+; dam line contributes elite NRHA athleticism and the renowned Smart Spook trainability to the cross). Owned by DAG Ventures LLC. Oswood Stallion Station, Weatherford TX. LTE $249,726 confirmed Oswood roster. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/colonels-dancing-gun/ Confirmed show record from Oswood records and NRHA results: 2024 NRHA Open World Champion; 2019 NRBC Open Classic/Challenge Co-Reserve Champion; 2018 NRHA European Futurity L4 Open Champion; 2018 NRHA Germany Breeders Futurity 3-Year-Old L4 Open Champion. LTE $249,726 confirmed. Colonels Dancing Gun's 2024 NRHA Open World Championship is the pinnacle achievement in reining -- the title awarded to the highest-scoring horse across all NRHA World Show open classes, representing the best reining horse in the world in competition that year. This title came well into his career as an aged horse, demonstrating the rare longevity and consistency required to remain at the top of the sport across multiple competitive seasons. His 2018 European Futurity and Germany Breeders Futurity championships as a 3-year-old established him as an elite performer from his first year of competition, and the 2024 World Championship confirmed that his ceiling extended far beyond his early futurity success. The Colonels Smoking Gun x Smart Spook cross pairs two of the most significant reining sire lines in modern history. Colonels Smoking Gun brings the Colonel Freckles foundation cow-sense and the Impressive-line athleticism that has dominated NRHA competition for decades. Smart Spook as dam sire adds the famous Smart Spook trainability, mental consistency, and fluid movement that has produced $12M+ in NRHA offspring. Together this cross creates offspring with the physical tools and mental makeup required to compete at the world championship level, as Colonels Dancing Gun himself proved across a career culminating in the 2024 NRHA World Championship. Standing at Oswood alongside Gunnatrashya (another Colonels Smoking Gun son) and Americasnexttopgun (another Colonels Smoking Gun son by a different dam), breeders can study the Colonels Smoking Gun cross on three distinct dam lines within the same stallion program.
