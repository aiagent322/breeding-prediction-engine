Full Profile

The Kingsmen -- 2018 Gray Stallion. By Ivory James (SI 103; Corona Cartel x -- LTE $220,026 confirmed NickelBar Ranch and High Point Performance; PE $26M+ in racing and barrel offspring confirmed Valley Equine 2026; #1 Living Son by #2 All-Time Corona Cartel per Future Fortunes; 2021 Leading Sire of 2-year-olds by money earned; top racing offspring include Instygator $811,691, La Vencedora $516,296, Ivan James $481,562, San Lorenzo Tay $392,482 winner of $926,500 Remington Park Oklahoma-Bred Futurity G1; stud fee $7,500 at Cox Stallion Station Kaplan LA) out of TR Dashing Badger 'Dolly' (Mr Illuminator x -- LTE $800,000+ confirmed Valley Equine 2026 and NickelBar Ranch; 4x NFR Qualifier confirmed multiple sources; 2x WPRA Reserve World Champion confirmed NickelBar Ranch and High Point Performance; full sister to multiple $50,000-$200,000+ earners; producer of $1M+ in progeny earnings confirmed NickelBar Ranch; 100% 1D producer with foals). The Kingsmen is the only cross of Ivory James x TR Dashing Badger confirmed NickelBar Ranch: 'THE ONLY CROSS TO EVER STAND BY IVORY JAMES AND OUT OF TR DASHING BADGER.' Previously standing at NickelBar Ranch and High Point Performance before Valley Equine. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX.

No confirmed dollar stud fee from Valley Equine -- contact Valley Equine directly. Foals eligible: Valley Girls Barrel Racing Association, Cowboy State, Colorado Classic, Legends of the South, WPRA PESI, Christi Durfey Benefit Auction, Southwest Desert Classic. NOT part of cutting Super Stallion Incentive. Pure barrel racing and speed event program.

No confirmed dollar LTE available from sources -- described as a junior stallion at Valley Equine. First foals started under saddle in 2024. NickelBar Ranch on first foal crop: 'fluid, light in the front end, have quick feet and are smart.' Valley Equine 2026: 'The Kingsmen is a young stallion backed by some of the most recognizable performance blood in the speed event industry. His sire, Ivory James SI 103, has produced more than $26 Million in earners, while his dam, TR Dashing Badger, is a four-time National Finals Rodeo qualifier with more than $800,000 in career earnings.' NickelBar Ranch: 'His offspring are already proving to be quiet, smart, quick to learn, and smooth moving.

Multiple repeat breeders come back each year in hopes of achieving the same great cross.' The combination of Ivory James racing dominance (PE $26M+, SI 103) with TR Dashing Badger's 4x NFR and 2x WPRA Reserve World Champion pedigree creates one of the highest-ceiling barrel racing pedigree crosses available in the industry today. TR Dashing Badger's $1M+ in progeny earnings from limited foals competing confirms the maternal line's ability to transmit elite barrel racing genetics across sire crosses.
