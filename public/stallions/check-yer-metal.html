Performance Summary

Check Yer Metal "Mike" — 2017 Bay Roan, 14.1H, 1100 lbs. AQHA 5856261, APHA 1106789. By Metallic Cat (High Brow Cat x Chers Shadow/Peptoboonsmal — LTE $637,711, 2008 NCHA Open Futurity Champion, 2009 NCHA Horse of the Year, NCHA Hall of Fame, NRCHA Hall of Fame, PE $83M+, QData and NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Sire, QData and NRCHA #1 All-Time Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire; the most decorated cutting and cow horse sire in the history of the industry) out of Checkers Live Oak (Im Countin Checks x N Yer Wildest Dreams/Peptoboonsmal — LTE $186,541, EquiStat 2023 #17 Cutting Dam). Owned and shown by Stuart Bozeman of Stephenville, Texas. 6 Panel N/N. Still competing as of 2025. Checkers Live Oak, affectionately called "Elanor" by the Bozeman family, was a decorated Non-Pro competitor in her own right. Her full confirmed show record includes: 2016 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5/6YO Non-Pro Champion, 2016 West Texas Futurity 5/6YO Limited Non-Pro Champion, 2016 El Rancho Mercuria Non-Pro Champion, 2016 NCHA World Championship Finals Non-Pro money earner, 2016 Brazos Bash 5/6YO Non-Pro Reserve Champion, 2016 El Rancho 5/6YO Non-Pro Reserve Champion, 2016 NCHA Super Stakes 5/6YO Non-Pro Semifinalist, and 2014 El Rancho 4YO Non-Pro Reserve Champion. Her dam N Yer Wildest Dreams (Peptoboonsmal) produced five Im Countin Checks offspring alone totaling $873,000: Deluxe Checks ($287K), Checkers Live Oak ($186K), Cat Countin Checks ($169K), Chexy Louella ($118K — 2018 NCHA Non-Pro Futurity Champion), and Countess Catrina ($112K) — one of the most productive single-mare/stallion combinations documented. Checkers Live Oak's maternal grandsire Im Countin Checks (Smart Lil Ricochet x Autumn Boon/Dual Pep — LTE $514,757) finished 2nd in the 2008 NCHA Horse of the Year race, is the sire of the 2017 NCHA Open World Champion and 2019 NCHA Non-Pro Horse of the Year, ranked Top 15 NCHA Sire six consecutive years, and carries PE $7M+. Described as having "the perfect outcross pedigree — a perfect cross on all High Brow Cat, Smooth As A Cat, Peptoboonsmal, Smart Little Lena, and Playboy bloodlines." Im Countin Checks was bred and raised at Tommy Manion Ranch alongside his sire Smart Lil Ricochet (leading siring son of Smart Little Lena at his 2007 death — won the 1997 NCHA Futurity first go-round scoring 223 before career-ending injury; offspring PE $3.9M+) and his grandsire Smart Little Lena himself (LTE $743,275 in 8 shows, only NCHA Triple Crown Champion, #2 All-Time NCHA Sire with PE $39M+, sired 17 World Champions, NCHA and AQHA Hall of Fame). LTE $449,312. Full confirmed show record: 2020 NCHA Futurity 3YO Non-Pro Semifinalist; 2021 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Intermediate Non-Pro Champion, Open Semifinalist, Non-Pro Finalist; 2021 Arbuckle Mountain 4YO Open Finalist, Ltd Non-Pro Champion, Non-Pro & 25K Non-Pro Reserve Champion; 2021 NCHA Summer Spectacular 4YO Non-Pro Finalist; 2021 Breeders Invitational 4YO Non-Pro Finalist; 2022 Brazos Bash 5/6YO Ltd Open Champion; 2022 Cattlemens 5/6YO Int Open Reserve Champion; 2022 NCHA Super Stakes 5/6YO Int Non-Pro 3rd (tie); 2023 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5/6YO Open Finalist, 4th Int Open, Non-Pro Finalist, 3rd Int Non-Pro; 2023 Arbuckle Mountain 5/6YO Int Open Reserve Champion; 2023 The Ike 5/6YO Non-Pro Reserve Champion; 2023 Idaho Cutting Classic 5/6YO Non-Pro Co-Champion; 2023 PCCHA Futurity 5/6YO Int Open Reserve Champion; 2024 Brazos Bash 7&Up Non-Pro Champion. EquiStat 2023 #15 Cutting Horse. #5 Leading Non-Pro Horse 2019–2024 — a five-year sustained ranking that reflects rare consistency. 45+ aged event finals with multiple championships across Open, Non-Pro, and Intermediate divisions. First foals 2-year-olds in 2025 — Valley Equine: "beginning to show their own competitiveness in futurity programs." Stud fee $3,750. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX.
