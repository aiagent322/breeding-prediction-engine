My Pistols Hott -- 2018 Bay Stallion. 14.2H, 1012 lbs. AQHA 5956396. 7-panel N/N. By Hottish (Spots Hot x Stylish Play Lena/Docs Stylish Oak -- 2004 NCHA Futurity Open Champion; PE $23,842,560 confirmed Valley Equine 2026; deceased, ICSI only at Valley Equine; one of the top producing sires of the modern cutting era; sire of Dual Reyish, Zigzagg, My Pistols Hott, Countin Hot Checks, Cool N Hot, Hottish Metal and many others in this database) out of Shes My Other Pistol (Playgun x Miss Freckles Hancock/Doc Bar -- Playgun is a leading NCHA and NRCHA sire by Mr Jess Perry; Miss Freckles Hancock carries Doc Bar and Three Bars blood; dam produce record for Shes My Other Pistol not further confirmed in available sources; Playgun influence adds reining athleticism to the Hottish cow sense base). Valley Equine (formerly Brazos Valley Stallion Station), Stephenville TX. Stud fee $3,500 confirmed Valley Equine 2026. Contact Valley Equine at valleyequinerepro.com. The NCHA Super Stakes is one of the three premier NCHA major events alongside the Futurity and the Derby, bringing together the top cutting horses aged 5 and 6 in the country to compete across Limited Open, Open, and Non-Pro divisions across multiple rounds. Winning the Open Classic at this level is a significant credential that establishes a horse as an elite open-level cutting competitor with the athleticism, cow sense, and training to succeed against the best competition in the country. The Hottish x Playgun cross pairs two distinct but complementary performance backgrounds. Hottish brings the Spots Hot x Stylish Play Lena combination -- a pedigree that has consistently produced horses with exceptional cow sense and natural athleticism that reads cattle instinctively. Playgun through Shes My Other Pistol adds reining-bred athleticism and the Doc Bar foundation that has underpinned elite performance horses for decades across cutting, reining, and cow horse. The combination creates offspring with versatility and depth of pedigree to compete across NCHA cutting and potentially NRCHA cow horse competition as foal crops mature. No offspring show data confirmed in available sources -- My Pistols Hott is a 2018 stallion with first foal crops just beginning to reach competition age. His NCHA Super Stakes Open Classic title at 6 years old establishes him as a legitimate open-level competitor whose position at Valley Equine reflects genuine competitive achievement. The 7-panel N/N genetic status confirms clean health genetics throughout the pedigree. Breeders crossing mares without Hottish or Playgun bloodlines will find the greatest genetic diversity benefit from this breeding.
