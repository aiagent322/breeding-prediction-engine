Full Profile

High Brow Cat 'Cat' -- 1988 Chestnut Stallion. By High Brow Hickory (1983 Dun, Doc's Hickory x Missy Copper x Poco Lena -- 1986 NCHA Futurity Reserve Champion; Bill Freeman who trained HBH said: 'whatever you did to that horse yesterday, it won't work tomorrow'; 347 money earners, avg offspring earnings $13,362) out of Smart Little Kitty (Smart Little Lena x Doc's Kitty/Doc Bar -- eye injured as yearling, never shown; Jack Waggoner: 'maybe the best Doc Bar daughter that there ever was'; produced 14 foals, 12 performers earning $336,257 including High Brow Cat, Smart Lil Highbrow $99,099, Smart Bronze $72,347, High Class Kitty $29,133). Bred by Hanes Chatham and Stewart Sewell. Purchased as 2-month-old colt by Jack Waggoner and Susan Ferguson.

Shown by Faron Hightower (NCHA Hall of Fame, $3M+ rider), then Jody Galyean, Olan Hightower, Jack Waggoner, and Bill Freeman. Sold in 2013 to Colt Ventures (Darren and Julie Blanton, Dallas TX) for reportedly just under $9 million. Partial co-ownership with Madison Smith, Beechfork Ranch, Weatherford TX from March 2019. Died October 2019.

ICSI only. HERDA N/H -- HERDA guarantee offered with proof of mare's test results. GBED, HYPP, MH, PSSM1, IMM/MYHM all N/N. AQHA Hall of Fame Class of 2023.

Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $110,784. Retired 1995. Show record: 1991 Will Rogers Futurity tied 13th (first check, shown by Faron Hightower); 1993 Augusta Futurity Classic Open Champion; multiple additional aged event placings through 1995.

Faron Hightower: 'Cat had a whole lot of cow about him, but he was a tough little son of a gun.' Jack Waggoner: 'Every once in a while a horse will come along that whatever you breed him to, it will cut.' From 1998 through 2010 never bred fewer than 100 mares in a single season -- bred 205 in 2003 and 208 in 2006. Estimated 3,100+ registered AQHA foals, two-thirds performers. In six years from first crop 1996 through 2002, placed at least one offspring in five NCHA Futurity Open Finals. Became a leading sire in 1997 with his second crop.

Top 10 leading cutting sire by 1999; #1 on the list by 2003. PE $97M+ (EquiStat Stallion Register, reported as of February 3, 2026 -- most current confirmed figure). AQHA All-Time Leading Sire in offspring earnings. EquiStat Elite All-Time Leading Sire.

AQHA/NCHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire 2011-2022. EquiStat #1 Leading Cutting Sire 2003-2022. EquiStat #1 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire 2009-2022. NRCHA All-Time Leading Sire.

Remained #1 or #2 on NCHA and Cutting Leading Sire lists since 2003 -- confirmed Valley Equine 2026. Record 8 NCHA Open Futurity Champions. 56 AQHA Champions across five disciplines. In 2021, 12 of the top 25 cutting horse sires were sons or grandsons of High Brow Cat.

#2 All-Time Broodmare Sire by performance money earned -- progeny earnings in cutting alone from his daughters' foals nearly $63 million. AQHA: 'On the all-time list of sires by performance money earned, Cat is joined in the top five by his sons Metallic Cat and Smooth As A Cat; their progeny alone have combined earnings of nearly $94 million.' Top confirmed offspring: Dont Look Twice ($843,096 LTE, 2011 NCHA HoY -- dam producing $950K+); Metallic Cat ($637,711 LTE, 2008 NCHA Futurity Champion, 2009 NCHA HoY, PE $83.6M+, NCHA/NRCHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire); Little Pepto Cat ($577,430); Boon San Kitty ($565,504); Smooth As A Cat (PE ~$43M+ as sire, NCHA leading sire in own right); Bet Hesa Cat ($272,151, 2011 NCHA World Champion, 2011 NCHA HoF); Classy CD Cat (2014 NCHA Open Futurity Champion); Shiftin Gears (2026 NCHA Super Stakes Amateur Champion, scored 223.5 with Blake Heid for Mike Heid, $15,605 -- confirmed Valley Equine April 2026, proving HBC offspring still winning at major events posthumously). Elite producing daughters: Dont Look Twice (dam of Dont Stopp Believin PE $5.9M+), Show Biz Kitty (dam of Badboonarising LTE $367K), Highbrow Supercat. Madison Smith (Beechfork Ranch): 'Metallic Cat is first to mind -- a Futurity champion, Horse of the Year, and now an incredible producing sire.

Several great daughters of High Brow Cat who have made an incredible mark on the industry include Dont Look Twice, Highbrow Supercat, and Show Biz Kitty.'.
