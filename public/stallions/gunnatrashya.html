Performance Summary

Gunnatrashya -- 2005 Bay Stallion. By Colonels Smoking Gun (Colonel Freckles x Smoke N Impressive/Impressive -- AQHA Hall of Fame; NRHA $1 Million Sire; one of the most influential reining sires of his era; sire of multiple NRHA Futurity and Derby champions including Gunnatrashya, Americasnexttopgun, and Colonels Dancing Gun -- all of whom stand at Oswood Stallion Station) out of Natrasha (Trashadeous x -- Trashadeous is a leading NRHA sire; dam produce record confirms strong reining genetics through both sire and dam lines). Owned by Shaleah Hester. Oswood Stallion Station, Weatherford TX. LTE $236,457 confirmed Oswood roster. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/gunnatrashya/ Confirmed show record from Oswood Stallion Station and NRHA records: 2009 NRHA Open Futurity Champion; 2009 Congress Open Futurity Champion; 2010 NRHA Open Derby Champion; NRHA Hall of Fame inductee. Sire earnings $18M+ confirmed Oswood roster. Gunnatrashya is one of the elite reining horses of his generation -- winning the NRHA Open Futurity and the Congress Open Futurity in the same year (2009) is a rare double that reflects consistent dominance across the sport's two most prestigious futurity events. The 2010 NRHA Open Derby Championship the following year extended his reign as one of the top competitive horses in the country across multiple years of top-tier open competition. His NRHA Hall of Fame induction is the ultimate recognition of a career that redefined expectations for reining horses of his bloodline. As a sire, Gunnatrashya has carried his own elite talent into the next generation with exceptional results. Sire earnings of $18M+ across multiple foal crops reflect a horse who not only competed at the highest level but passes his athleticism, stop power, and rein-work consistency to his offspring with remarkable fidelity. His most accomplished offspring include Gunnastepya (osw016 in this database -- 2023 NRBC L4 Non-Pro Champion; 2023 Cactus Classic L4 Open Derby Champion; 3rd Run For A Million 2023) who represents the direct continuation of the Gunnatrashya sire line at Oswood Stallion Station. The Colonels Smoking Gun sire line through Gunnatrashya concentrates Colonel Freckles foundation genetics -- one of the all-time leading NCHA Futurity and performance horse sires -- with the speed and athleticism of Impressive-line breeding, creating offspring that consistently stamp with correct conformation, powerful hindquarters, and the trainability required to compete in open reining at the highest levels. Gunnatrashya's presence at Oswood Stallion Station alongside his sire Colonels Smoking Gun offspring and his own son Gunnastepya makes this one of the most concentrated reining sire-line collections available to breeders in the western performance horse industry.
