Full Profile

Flexing Guns N Town -- 2019 Palomino Stallion. AQHA. 6-panel N/N. 7-panel N/N.

Color tested AA ee N/Cr -- palomino confirmed. By Hollywoodstinseltown (Hollywood Dun It x Miss Tinseltown -- PE $4M+; NRHA $4 Million Sire announced June 2025; both Hollywood Dun It and Miss Tinseltown are NRHA Hall of Famers making Hollywoodstinseltown a son of two Hall of Famers; LTE $185,886; 2010 World Equestrian Games team gold medalist with Tim McQuay; 389 NRHA money-earning foals; a consistent global producer whose sons and daughters compete at the top of NRHA open competition across North America and Europe) out of Shesa Fashion Gun (Colonels Smoking Gun x -- Colonels Smoking Gun is NRHA Hall of Fame, NRHA $15 Million Sire, sire of Gunnatrashya osw001, Americasnexttopgun osw004, and Down Right Amazing osw013 in this database; Shesa Fashion Gun's produce record confirms Flexing Guns N Town as her highest-earning offspring with LTE $614,720). Frozen semen only, ICSI available. Stud fee $2,000 + $675 chute fee.

Subscribed to NRHA Sire & Dam, NRBC, Oswood Breeders Incentive. Source: https://oswoodstallionstation.com/flexing-guns-n-town/ LTE $614,720 confirmed Oswood Stallion Station. Confirmed career highlights: 2025 The Run For A Million Open Champion -- ridden by NRHA Professional Arnaud Girinon in what announcers called the greatest upset in reining history, beating a powerhouse international field including Andrea Fappani's 230-point score; 2024 NRHA European Level 4 Open Derby Champion (230.5 score); 2024 IRHA Derby 2nd place (230.5 score); 2024 100X European Classic Level 4 Open Derby Reserve Champion; NRHA Breeders Futurity Open 3rd; NRHA Italian Futurity Open 2nd; NRHA Austrian Futurity Open Finalist. The Run For A Million is the richest single reining event in the world, held in Las Vegas with over $1 million in added money.

Winning The Run For A Million Open Championship against a field assembled from the top qualifier horses in North America and Europe is the pinnacle of professional reining competition. Flexing Guns N Town's 2025 TRFAM win not only secured the championship for France for the first time in the event's history but established him as one of the most accomplished reining horses competing at the international open level in 2025. His consistent 230+ scores across the NRHA European circuit, the IRHA, and the 100X events in the season leading into the TRFAM win confirmed that the championship performance was the product of sustained excellence rather than a single outlier run. The Hollywoodstinseltown x Colonels Smoking Gun dam cross pairs two of the most commercially reliable reining pedigree lines of the modern era.

Hollywoodstinseltown (PE $4M+) produces consistent NRHA open performers across North America and Europe, while Colonels Smoking Gun ($15M+ sire) through Shesa Fashion Gun brings the competitive fire, stop power, and natural athleticism of the Gunner line. Together these two sire lines create horses built for the elite demands of international open reining competition -- exactly what Flexing Guns N Town has demonstrated across multiple continents and multiple premier events. First foal crops are currently developing and will be watched closely by the international reining community.
