Confirmed show record from 6666stallions.com career highlights: 2023 Arbuckle Mountain Senior Intermediate Amateur Classic Reserve Champion. Primary discipline: cutting and amateur cow horse competition, consistent with the Playgun sire line's strong Non-Pro and Amateur market history. Bowmans Gun represents the accessible amateur and grassroots tier of the 6666 Ranch stallion program. At $1,800 stud fee he is the lowest-priced offering on the 6666 Ranch cutting and cow horse roster, providing breeders entry-level access to the historic 6666 Ranch program with a young gray stallion that carries two proven sire lines. Playgun's Freckles Playboy sire line is one of the genuine foundational pillars of Western performance horse history -- Freckles Playboy (Jewel's Leo Bar x Gay Jay) sired Freckles Melody, Doc's Oak, Docs Keepin Time, and appeared in the pedigrees of NCHA, NRCHA, and PRCA champions across cutting, cow horse, team roping, and versatility ranch horse for three decades. The Miss Silver Pistol dam through Doc's Hickory adds the Doc Bar foundation that built the modern cutting horse industry, making Playgun a genuinely cross-discipline sire with cattle instincts running through both sides of his own pedigree. Bowmans Fancy Lena as dam by Smart Little Lena adds the QData All-Time Leading Cutting Sire whose PE of $15.4M+ spanned cutting, cow horse, and reining. Smart Little Lena daughters are among the most sought-after dam-line options in the cutting and cow horse industries due to consistent production of trainable, cowy offspring with the Doc O'Lena foundation that defines great cutting horse movement. The combination of Playgun's Freckles Playboy athleticism and Smart Little Lena's Doc O'Lena cutting polish creates a pedigree that suits amateur and intermediate competition particularly well -- exactly the market segment where the 2023 Arbuckle Mountain Senior Intermediate Amateur Classic Reserve Championship is the relevant credential. Bowmans Gun is owned by Circle Bar Ranch, confirming working ranch horse and practical performance horse program backing. No offspring show data confirmed yet -- first foal crops still young.
