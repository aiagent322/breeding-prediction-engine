By Playgun (Freckles Playboy x Miss Silver Pistol/Doc's Hickory -- gray 1992 stallion, deceased; LTE $168,408 confirmed allbreedpedigree; PE $10,000,000+ confirmed allbreedpedigree 'Offspring Earnings over $10,000,000 and growing'; finalist at 21 major events; NCHA Super Stakes Open and Non-Pro Champions sired; NCHA World Champion sired; RHAA World Champion sired; SCR Sporties Playgun was PRCA NFR Team Roping qualifier; foals consistently described as 'incredibly trainable, willing, and good minded'; stood at 6666 Ranch historically) out of Bowmans Fancy Lena (Smart Little Lena x Bowmans Fancy/Lenas Jewel Bars/Doc O'Lena -- Smart Little Lena is a QData All-Time Leading Cutting Sire with PE $15.4M+; sire of Colonel Freckles, Freckles Playboy, and the most influential Doc O'Lena crosses in performance horse history; dam name and produce record not further sourced in available records beyond the pedigree structure).

Primary discipline: cutting and amateur cow horse competition, consistent with the Playgun sire line's strong Non-Pro and Amateur market history. Bowmans Gun represents the accessible amateur and grassroots tier of the 6666 Ranch stallion program.

At $1,800 stud fee he is the lowest-priced offering on the 6666 Ranch cutting and cow horse roster, providing breeders entry-level access to the historic 6666 Ranch program with a young gray stallion that carries two proven sire lines. Playgun's Freckles Playboy sire line is one of the genuine foundational pillars of Western performance horse history -- Freckles Playboy (Jewel's Leo Bar x Gay Jay) sired Freckles Melody, Doc's Oak, Docs Keepin Time, and appeared in the pedigrees of NCHA, NRCHA, and PRCA champions across cutting, cow horse, team roping, and versatility ranch horse for three decades. The Miss Silver Pistol dam through Doc's Hickory adds the Doc Bar foundation that built the modern cutting horse industry, making Playgun a genuinely cross-discipline sire with cattle instincts running through both sides of his own pedigree. Bowmans Fancy Lena as dam by Smart Little Lena adds the QData All-Time Leading Cutting Sire whose PE of $15.4M+ spanned cutting, cow horse, and reining.

Smart Little Lena daughters are among the most sought-after dam-line options in the cutting and cow horse industries due to consistent production of trainable, cowy offspring with the Doc O'Lena foundation that defines great cutting horse movement. The combination of Playgun's Freckles Playboy athleticism and Smart Little Lena's Doc O'Lena cutting polish creates a pedigree that suits amateur and intermediate competition particularly well -- exactly the market segment where the 2023 Arbuckle Mountain Senior Intermediate Amateur Classic Reserve Championship is the relevant credential. Bowmans Gun is owned by Circle Bar Ranch, confirming working ranch horse and practical performance horse program backing. No offspring show data confirmed yet -- first foal crops still young.
