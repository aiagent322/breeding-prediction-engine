Full Profile

Dual Rey -- 1994 Sorrel Stallion, 14.2H. By Dual Pep (Peppy San Badger x Miss Dual Doc/Doc's Remedy -- PE $26,181,194 confirmed, all-time leading cutting sire; Dual Pep and Peptoboonsmal are three-quarter brothers in blood, two of the most influential sires in performance horse history; Dual Pep's sons include Dual Rey and his half-brothers by the same dam) out of Nurse Rey (Wyoming Doc x -- LTE $65,587; half-sister to Rey Lynx and Smartolena). Bred and owned by Linda Holmes, Holmes Cutting/Performance Horses, Longmont Colorado. Shown by Lloyd Cox (who also showed Nurse Rey and many of her offspring for Linda and sister Gail Holmes).

Euthanized May 2018 due to complications of age at approximately 24 years old. ICSI only. HERDA N/N confirmed. Owned by Linda Holmes.

Stud fee $5,625 ICSI. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $105,038. Show record: 1997 NCHA Futurity -- tied 6th in Open finals with Lloyd Cox.

Multiple additional aged event placings in Open and Non-Pro competition. Linda Holmes: 'Dual Rey wasn't a very big horse, about 14-2 hands. He had a deep heart girth. Dual Rey was really quick, he really wanted to be low-headed, have that freedom of head and neck when he came through turns.

He wasn't a mechanical horse. He really wanted to do it naturally. He was very sound and we never shod him. He went barefoot.

He had great feet. The other amazing thing about him is how smart he was.' AQHA Hall of Fame inductee. PE $57,893,606 (Valley Equine 2026). QData and NCHA #3 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire.

QData #7 and NRCHA #6 All-Time Leading Sire. 65 World or Reserve World Champions. 1,144 NCHA money earners with a near-record average of $31,292. 16 AQHA World Champions and 18 AQHA Reserve World Champions.

SallyHarrison.com: 'From his first crop (32 AQHA foals of 1999), Dual Rey lit the boards with TR Dual Rey -- from that crop on there was no stopping the Dual Reys, as performers and producers.' Top confirmed direct offspring: Special Nu Baby ($481,197); Rey Down Sally ($404,170); Dont Stopp Believin ($463,962 -- 2011 Sorrel, Dual Rey x Dont Look Twice/High Brow Cat, 2025 QData Top 12 Leading Cutting Sire, PE $5,941,502, 3 NCHA Non-Pro Horses of the Year, standing at Valley Equine); Reys Dual Badger ($312,179); TR Dual Rey ($291,111 -- NCHA Super Stakes Open Reserve Champion, NCHA Derby Reserve Champion, himself a sire with $3.7M+ in NCHA earners); A Smart Little Rey ($204,868); Cat A Rey ($230,360); Reys Desire ($400,000+). As maternal grandsire through elite daughters: Miss Ella Rey (Dual Rey daughter) produced 2015 NCHA Futurity Champion Stevie Rey Von ($420,559 LTE, PE $20M+, NRCHA $3M+ sire); Dual Rey Tag (Dual Rey daughter, LTE $57,251) produced 2018 NCHA World Champion Hashtags ($485,293 LTE, PE $5.3M+); A Little Reylena (Dual Rey daughter, LTE $126,608, QData/NCHA Top 15 All-Time Leading Dam) produced 2017 NCHA Open Futurity Champion Dual Reyish ($452,687 LTE, PE $2.5M+) and 2023 NCHA Open Futurity Champion Lone Woolf ($668,310 LTE). Dual Rey daughters' produce earnings confirmed at more than $10 million in the arena -- making him one of the most dominant maternal grandsires in NCHA history. He stands as the ultimate cross for mares across the board with championship titles spanning multiple disciplines including cutting, reined cow horse, reining, and roping.
