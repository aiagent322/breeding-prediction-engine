Full Profile

Custom Dreams -- 2020 Palomino Stallion. 15H. AQHA. By Magnum Chic Dream (Smart Chic Olena x Sailin Barbee -- NRHA Hall of Fame inductee 2016; PE $13M+; $13 Million Sire; the fifth sire in NRHA history to cross the $13 million mark; 900+ money-earning progeny with 30 offspring each surpassing $100,000 NRHA LTE; 2004 AQHA Senior Reining World Champion; one of the most dominant reining sire lines in NRHA history; his leading money-earner is Customize My Dream, the full sibling of Custom Dreams through the same dam) out of Gunna Be Custom (Colonels Smoking Gun x -- Colonels Smoking Gun is NRHA Hall of Fame, NRHA $13 Million Sire, sire of Gunnatrashya osw001 and Americasnexttopgun osw004 in this database; the Magnum Chic Dream x Colonels Smoking Gun dam cross pairs two Hall of Fame sire lines in a single pedigree, creating offspring with the athleticism and natural lope of the Magnum Chic Dream line combined with the competitive fire and stop of the Colonels Smoking Gun line).

Owned by Custom Dreams Brazil (Lincoln Malherios). Oswood Stallion Station, 1400 Old Garner Road, Weatherford TX 76088. Stud fee $3,000 + $675 chute fee. Frozen semen only.

ICSI available. Source: https://oswoodstallionstation.com/custom-dreams/ LTE $215,000+ confirmed NRHA (nearly $215,000 earned in 2024 alone per NRHA Million Sire milestone article). Confirmed show record: 2024 NRHA Derby Level 4 Open Reserve Champion ridden by NRHA $5 Million Rider Casey Deary; 2025 6666 NRHA Derby Level 4 Open leader with a 229.5 score -- the highest score in the L4 Open at that point in the competition. Ridden throughout his career by NRHA $5 Million Rider Casey Deary, one of the most accomplished reining professionals in the sport.

Winning the 2024 NRHA Derby L4 Open Reserve Championship is one of the top credentials available in professional reining competition. The NRHA Derby is one of the three major annual NRHA premier events alongside the Futurity and the NRBC, attracting the world's best five and six-year-old reining horses and most accomplished open riders. A Reserve Championship in the Level 4 Open places Custom Dreams among the very best reiners in the world at his age, with only one horse finishing above him in the most competitive division. His 2025 6666 NRHA Derby performance -- leading with a 229.5 score -- confirms that his 2024 credentials were not a one-time performance but a reflection of sustained elite ability across multiple seasons and premier events.

Custom Dreams is a full sibling to Customize My Dream (osw011 in this database), also by Magnum Chic Dream x Gunna Be Custom. Customize My Dream holds LTE $482,591 and won the 2022 NRHA Futurity Open Reserve Championship -- the sibling connection confirms that the Magnum Chic Dream x Gunna Be Custom cross consistently produces horses capable of performing at the elite NRHA Level 4 Open level. Two full siblings both performing at the top level of the most competitive division in reining is exceptional and confirms the cross as proven rather than coincidental. The Magnum Chic Dream x Colonels Smoking Gun dam cross is one of the most commercially significant reining breeding combinations of the modern era.

Both sires are NRHA Hall of Famers and $13 Million Sires, making Custom Dreams a horse with unmatched pedigree depth from both sides. His first foal crops are 2024 and are still developing. Breeders seeking to access this dual Hall of Fame cross at Oswood Stallion Station will find Custom Dreams a horse whose arena credentials at the highest NRHA level back up every aspect of his pedigree promise.
