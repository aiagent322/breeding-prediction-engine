Bet Hes Blu is part of a notable pairing within the Oswood Stallion Station program -- a full brother to Bet Hesa Boon (osw008) through the same sire Bet Hesa Cat and same dam Flo N Blu Boon.

Having two full brothers standing at the same station is unusual in the stallion industry and gives breeders the opportunity to study how the same genetic cross expresses across different individuals. While Bet Hesa Boon has compiled a more prominent open-level show record including the 2023 NRCHA Hackamore Classic Open Bridle Championship and World's Greatest Horseman credentials, Bet Hes Blu's futurity and hackamore finaling at the NRCHA level demonstrates that both brothers inherited the cow horse ability and competitive temperament of their parents. The Bet Hesa Cat x Flo N Blu Boon cross is proven -- Flo N Blu Boon's produce earnings of $607,000+ confirm her status as an elite producer, and both Bet Hes Blu and Bet Hesa Boon reflect the quality of that female family. Bet Hesa Cat as sire (2001 NCHA Open World Champion; NRCHA Million Dollar Sire) provides the athletic foundation and cow sense that defines the best Oswood stallions.

The NRCHA open-level finaling represents legitimate top-tier cow horse credentials. The broader Bet Hesa Cat x blue roan color line has proven consistently popular with breeders seeking the combination of High Brow Cat cow sense with the athleticism of the Pretty Boy Boon dam family, making Bet Hes Blu a complementary breeding option alongside his more decorated brother in the Oswood program.
