Performance Summary

One Fine Vintage 'Vinnie' -- 2009 Sorrel Stallion. 15.1 hands. 6-Panel N/N. By One Time Pepto (LTE $331,097; 2005 NCHA Super Stakes Open Champion; 2006 Augusta 5/6YO Open Classic Champion; 2006 Abilene Spectacular Open Classic Champion; PE $22M+; by Peptoboonsmal; owned by Jeffrey and Sheri Matthews, Weatherford TX; Team Roping Journal confirms '$22 million producer') out of Shiners Siena (Shining Spark -- Shining Spark is a foundational NRHA/NRCHA sire, NRHA Futurity Champion and NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity Champion, multiple NRHA world champions in produce). Owned by Robertson Ranches. Standing at Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Foals eligible: Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive, PCCHA, Royal Crown Roping, Riata Buckle, ARHFA Top Gun Stallion Incentive, Gold Buckle Futurities, The Diamond Classic. Enrolled: NRCHA Stakes, PCCHA Stakes. LTE $135,187. Competed successfully across seven or more disciplines at the AQHA World Show -- an exceptional demonstration of versatility and trainability. Confirmed full show record: 2015 NRCHA Supreme Reined Cow Horse; 2016 AQHA World Champion Senior Working Cow Horse; 2017 AQHA Bronze Champion Senior Working Cow Horse; 2017 NSHA World's Richest Stock Horse Reined Cow Horse Open Champion; 2018 World's Greatest Horseman Finalist; 2019 AQHA World Show Qualifier and Finalist in Working Cow Horse, Heading, Heeling, Ranch Riding, and Halter; AQHA High Point Open and Supreme Working Cow Horse; NRCHA Supreme Reined Cow Horse; 280.5 AQHA Points. Stallion Register: 'AQHA and NRCHA World Champion, AQHA High Point Open and Supreme Working Cow Horse and NRCHA Supreme Reined Cow Horse.' Team Roping Journal 2025: 'One Fine Vintage, the No. 11 team roping sire of 2024 according to QData, has offspring that have won nearly $500,000 in roping alone. Known as Vinnie, the stallion is himself an AQHA and NRCHA world champion, and the cornerstone of Robertson Ranches breeding program.' Jazlyn Rice (fit Robertson Ranches yearlings for sale): 'We have been so impressed by the consistency in their size, build and demeanor. They are equal parts smart and sweet and possess the athletic ability to excel at multiple events just like Vinnie did. He puts his stamp on them every time.' PE $792,879 (Valley Equine 2026 -- most current confirmed). No. 11 QData Team Roping Sire of 2024 -- confirmed Team Roping Journal February 2025. Offspring won nearly $500,000 in roping alone. Versatility across disciplines confirms the dam-line (Shining Spark) and sire-line (One Time Pepto x Peptoboonsmal) combination produces horses with multi-event athleticism and trainability. Valley Equine 2026: 'One Fine Vintage brings together a rare combination of cow horse excellence, stock horse versatility, and practical production. A World Champion across multiple associations, his own resume spans the highest levels of the AQHA and NRCHA, including elite Open Bridle and Hackamore accomplishments. That same adaptability is showing up in his offspring, with success across rope horse futurities on both the head and heel side, as well as continued presence in the cow horse and stock horse arenas.' Of all the stallions in this database, One Fine Vintage stands out as the most directly relevant to breeders targeting team roping performance alongside cow horse and cutting versatility.
