Full Profile

Walla Walla Whiz -- 2003 Chestnut Stallion. 15H. AQHA. 6-panel N/N.

By Topsail Whiz (Topsail Hickory x Jennas Boon -- NRHA Eleven Million Dollar Sire, PE $11M+; the leading NRHA sire of his era; sire of Whizkey N Diamonds and numerous elite reining performers; one of the most dominant reining sire lines in NRHA history; the Topsail Whiz line consistently produces horses with exceptional stop, speed control, and lope cadence) out of Shiney Walla Wanda (Shining Spark x -- Shining Spark is a leading NRHA and NRCHA sire with PE $25M+; the Shining Spark x Topsail Whiz cross is one of the most successful combinations in reining breeding producing horses with exceptional athleticism, speed, and trainability; Shiney Walla Wanda's produce record includes Walla Walla Whiz as the standout performer). Owned by Arcese Quarter Horses. Oswood Stallion Station, 1400 Old Garner Road, Weatherford TX 76088. 2026 Breeding Fee: $3,650.

Cooled and frozen semen available. ICSI available. Source: https://oswoodstallionstation.com/walla-walla-whiz/ LTE $187,820 confirmed NRHA. PE $3,254,144+ confirmed In Foal Partners 2026.

Confirmed career highlights: NRHA Open Futurity Champion; NRHA Open Derby Champion; NRBC Level 4 Open 3rd; Shadow Oak Plantation Derby Level 4 Open Reserve Champion. His son Americasnexttopgun (osw004 in this database) won the 2022 NRHA Level 4 Open Derby Championship and the 2020 NRBC Level 4 Open Derby Championship, confirming that Walla Walla Whiz consistently produces elite open-level NRHA performers across multiple generations. Winning both the NRHA Open Futurity AND the NRHA Open Derby as a performer places Walla Walla Whiz among a very short list of horses to have won at the highest level of NRHA competition across multiple major annual events. The NRHA Futurity and Derby each attract hundreds of the best reining horses and trainers in the world.

A horse that wins the Open division at both events demonstrates elite natural talent, exceptional trainability, and the competitive consistency required to perform under maximum pressure against the deepest fields in reining. The Topsail Whiz x Shining Spark dam cross is one of the most successful breeding combinations in NRHA reining history. Topsail Whiz with PE $11M+ was the leading reining sire of his era, and Shining Spark daughters as dams have repeatedly proven among the best crosses for Topsail Whiz sons. The combination creates horses with the stop, speed control, and athletic collection that defines the modern elite reining horse.

His ongoing offspring success at the Level 4 Open through Americasnexttopgun confirms that the Walla Walla Whiz sire line is producing the next generation of NRHA open-level champions.
