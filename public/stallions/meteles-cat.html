Meteles Cat 'Alvin' -- 2010 Bay Roan Stallion. AQHA. 14 hands. By Metallic Cat (High Brow Cat x Chers Shadow/Peptoboonsmal -- LTE $637,711, NCHA Open Futurity Champion 2008, NCHA Horse of the Year 2009, QData/NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, PE $83.6M+, NRCHA All-Time Leading Sire -- full profile in database) out of Teles Lies (Lenas Telesis -- LTE $93,206; Northwest Festival Open Derby Reserve Champion; San Diego Open Derby Reserve Champion; 3rd NCHA $10,000 Novice Top Ten; PCCHA $10,000 Novice Champion; 6th PCCHA Fall 4YO Open Stakes; finalist NCHA $10,000 Novice Finals).

5-Panel N/N, HERDA N/Hrd, N/My, EJSCA N/N. Trained and shown by Rock and Cody Hedlund, whose skill, dedication and love for the horse were instrumental in his rise to prominence per Solo Select Horses. NCHA Hall of Fame. Now standing at Solo Select Horses, Gainesville TX for 2026 breeding season at $3,200 ($2,500 standing fee plus $700 chute fee).

Previously stood at Brazos Valley Stallion Station / Valley Equine, Stephenville TX; foals from Valley Equine years remain eligible for Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive through the 2028 NCHA Futurity. Also eligible: Breeders Invitational, NCHA Super Stakes, NRCHA Stallion Stakes, PCCHA, Royal Crown Roping, Gold Buckle Futurities. LTE $437,016. NCHA Hall of Fame.
