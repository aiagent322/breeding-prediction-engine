Performance Summary

Meteles Cat 'Alvin' -- 2010 Bay Roan Stallion. AQHA. 14 hands. By Metallic Cat (High Brow Cat x Chers Shadow/Peptoboonsmal -- LTE $637,711, NCHA Open Futurity Champion 2008, NCHA Horse of the Year 2009, QData/NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, PE $83.6M+, NRCHA All-Time Leading Sire -- full profile in database) out of Teles Lies (Lenas Telesis -- LTE $93,206; Northwest Festival Open Derby Reserve Champion; San Diego Open Derby Reserve Champion; 3rd NCHA $10,000 Novice Top Ten; PCCHA $10,000 Novice Champion; 6th PCCHA Fall 4YO Open Stakes; finalist NCHA $10,000 Novice Finals). 5-Panel N/N, HERDA N/Hrd, N/My, EJSCA N/N. Trained and shown by Rock and Cody Hedlund, whose skill, dedication and love for the horse were instrumental in his rise to prominence per Solo Select Horses. NCHA Hall of Fame. Now standing at Solo Select Horses, Gainesville TX for 2026 breeding season at $3,200 ($2,500 standing fee plus $700 chute fee). Previously stood at Brazos Valley Stallion Station / Valley Equine, Stephenville TX; foals from Valley Equine years remain eligible for Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive through the 2028 NCHA Futurity. Also eligible: Breeders Invitational, NCHA Super Stakes, NRCHA Stallion Stakes, PCCHA, Royal Crown Roping, Gold Buckle Futurities. LTE $437,016. NCHA Hall of Fame. 48 finals appearances -- extraordinary consistency across a long career. 14 Championships, 8 Reserve Championships. 2 AQHA World Championships in Cutting. Confirmed titles: Breeders Invitational Non-Pro Derby Reserve Champion; NCHA Non-Pro Super Stakes Reserve Champion; Showdown in Cowtown Summer Slot Cutting 4/6YO Non-Pro Derby Champion; Breeders Invitational Non-Pro Classic Champion; The Ike Open Classic Reserve Champion; NCHA Non-Pro Classic/Challenge Reserve Champion; Bonanza Non-Pro Classic Champion. NCHA Non-Pro Futurity finalist. AQHA Open Preperformance ROM, earning 12 Cutting (Level 3) points in open events. Solo Select Horses: 'Throughout his illustrious career, Meteles Cat made 48 finals appearances, captured 14 Championships, and secured 8 Reserve Championships, solidifying his reputation as a top performer.' His 14 hands frame belies an athlete of remarkable power and footwork whose ability to read and rate cattle proved elite across nearly a decade of competition. PE $3,262,972 as of January 6, 2026 (confirmed gallopauction.com -- most current). 576 AQHA foals registered, 183 money-earners, average earnings per money-earner $17,830.45. Foal earnings discipline breakdown: Cutting $2,725,778.90; Reined Cow Horse $383,220.41; Roping (all events) $139,594.77; Barrel $12,893.00; Ranch $5,706.87; Other $7,065.61. Versatility across disciplines confirms Meteles Cat passes athleticism, cow sense, and trainability broadly across his foal crops. Solo Select Horses: 'Meteles Cat has become a $3.2+ Million-Dollar Sire, with his offspring continuing to excel in multiple disciplines.' In-foal partners (earlier listing): 'Meteles Cat's offspring are proving to be very versatile. Producing NRCHA and NCHA Champions that have earned over $500,000 in earnings with very limited foal crops.' The Metallic Cat x Teles Lies (Lenas Telesis) cross creates a pairing of elite cutting athleticism on top with a non-pro-proven, trainable mare family underneath -- producing offspring that have proven accessible across a range of competitive levels from NCHA open to non-pro, NRCHA, and roping.
