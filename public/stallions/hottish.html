Performance Summary

Hottish -- 2008 Sorrel Stallion. By Spots Hot (2004 NCHA Futurity Open Champion -- confirmed from QHN; Spots Hot standing at Valley Equine, his offspring produce confirmed in the cutting horse industry) out of Stylish Play Lena (Docs Stylish Oak x -- LTE $289,623; QData #4 All-Time Leading Cutting Dam -- confirmed Valley Equine 2026; produce earnings $1.6M+ including Hottish as leading earner, Smooth Talkin Style, and Pepto Boom; dam page confirms exceptional production capacity across multiple elite performers). Bred by Double Dove Ranch. Owned by Dustin and Deena Adams, Springtown TX. Passed away 2020. ICSI only. NRCHA Million Dollar Sire (milestone July 2025 -- confirmed NRCHA announcement). Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $301,843. 2012 NCHA Reserve Horse of the Year. Confirmed full show record: 2011 NCHA Open Futurity finalist; 2012 NCHA Open Derby Champion; 2012 Breeders Invitational 4YO Open Champion; 2012 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Non-Pro Champion; 2012 NCHA Summer Spectacular 4YO Open Champion; 2012 South Point Futurity 4YO Open Champion; 2012 Idaho Futurity 4YO Open Reserve Champion; 2012 Wrangler Futurity 4YO Open Reserve Champion; 2012 Calgary Stampede Wrangler Open Derby Reserve Champion; 2012 NCHA Reserve Horse of the Year; 2013 Bonanza Open Classic finalist; 2014 NCHA Non-Pro Classic/Challenge Champion; 2014 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5/6YO Non-Pro Champion; 2014 Bonanza Open Classic top 10; 2014 NCHA Super Stakes Open Classic finalist. Dustin Adams (owner): 'He is the most trainable horse that I have ever ridden. I see that in his babies. A lot of different styles of trainers are getting along with him. Everyone talked about how much of a freak athlete Hottish was, and he absolutely was, but my favorite part about that horse was the fact that he was like a big teddy bear. He was a lover. You could hurt his feelings, and he loved his job. He had charisma on a cow, and I see that in some of his babies a lot.' Deena Adams (NRCHA Million Dollar Sire milestone): 'Hottish becoming an NRCHA Million Dollar Sire is huge for us. It's very rewarding to watch his legacy grow. We're grateful for the advances in science that allow us to continue his breeding career, and we love how Hottie continues to bless our family and so many others.' PE $23,842,560 (Valley Equine Hottish page -- most current confirmed). 2025 QData #3 Leading Sire. 2024 NCHA #4 Leading Sire. QData and NCHA Top 12 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire. NRCHA Million Dollar Sire milestone confirmed July 2025. At time of death in 2020, ranked #4 among leading cutting sires behind only Metallic Cat, Dual Rey, and High Brow Cat. Top confirmed offspring: Kittennish (million-dollar mare, NCHA Horse of the Year -- confirmed Valley Equine Hottish page as leading female earner); Phil N Dangerous (x Lil Maddy Rey/Dual Smart Rey, LTE $575,979, NCHA Open Futurity Champion, 2025 NCHA Open Classic/Challenge Co-Champion, standing Valley Equine 2026); Hottish Metal (x Show Me The Rey/Metallic Cat, LTE $497,370, NCHA Horse of the Year, NCHA Hall of Fame, standing Valley Equine 2026); Dual Reyish (x A Little Reylena/Dual Rey, LTE $452,687, 2017 NCHA Open Futurity Champion, NCHA Hall of Fame, standing Valley Equine 2026); Cool N Hot (x Donas Cool Cat/High Brow Cat, LTE $435,069, 2018 NCHA Horse of the Year, NCHA Hall of Fame, standing Valley Equine 2026); Countin Hot Checks (x Cat Countin Checks/Im Countin Checks, LTE $296,780, 2023 NCHA Open World Champion, standing Valley Equine 2026); Hott Rod (NRCHA World's Greatest Horseman Champion -- confirmed Valley Equine Hottish page); Blackish (x SS Blacks Lil Kitty/Bayou Boon, 4YO Open Champion at The Ike with Lloyd Cox -- confirmed QHN 2018); Swish (2026 NCHA Super Stakes 5/6YO Open Champion, scored 221.5 with Clayton Carter for Carol Anderson Ward, $12,132 -- confirmed Valley Equine April 2026); Sneakish (x Smooth Sneakin/Smooth As A Cat, 2017 NCHA Futurity Limited Non-Pro Co-Champion -- confirmed QHN 2018). QHN: 'Mare owners can really breed anything to him' -- confirmed as a genuine outcross for the dominant bloodlines of the era.
