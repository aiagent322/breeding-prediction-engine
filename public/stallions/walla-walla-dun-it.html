Full Profile

Walla Walla Dun It -- 2004 Stallion. AQHA. By Hollywood Dun It (Hollywood Jac 86 x Blossom Berry -- NRHA Hall of Fame inductee 2000; deceased 2005 at age 22; NRHA $6 Million Sire -- the second stallion in NRHA history to reach the $6 million mark in offspring earnings; NRHA Futurity Reserve Champion; NRHA Derby Champion; NRHA Superstakes Champion; 1,209 registered AQHA foals; his sons Hollywoodstinseltown -- sire of Flexing Guns N Town osw020 -- and Reminic N Dunit stand as the most commercially active Hollywood Dun It sons in the current market; offspring renowned for their attractive looks, signature big stop, and extreme trainability; retired to stud 1988 and became the first stallion in NRHA history to reach the $4 million sire milestone) out of Walla Walla Wanda (Taris Catalyst x Miss Walla Cactus -- LTE $39,000+ and 44 AQHA points; AQHA World Championship Senior Reining top 15 finalist; dam of 7 money-earners and point-earners with combined NRHA offspring earnings of $53,946+; Taris Catalyst is a leading NRHA sire whose daughters are well represented in the pedigrees of multiple Oswood stallions including Walla Walla Whiz osw002 whose dam Shiney Walla Wanda shares the same maternal line). Stud fee $1,000.

Contact Oswood Stallion Station at (817) 599-4560 or Toyon Ranch LLC for availability. Source: https://oswoodstallionstation.com/walla-walla-dun-it/ LTE $4,500 NRHA. Walla Walla Dun It is primarily a heritage breeding stallion offering direct access to Hollywood Dun It genetics -- the NRHA $6 Million Sire and Hall of Fame inductee who defined the modern big-stop reining style -- at an accessible $1,000 stud fee that makes his bloodline available to breeders who cannot access Hollywood Dun It through the frozen ICSI programs of other stations. Hollywood Dun It's influence on American reining is foundational.

His signature drop-to-the-basement stop revolutionized what breeders expected from reining horses and established the standard for NRHA competition from the 1990s forward. Hollywoodstinseltown (Flexing Guns N Town's sire, osw020 in this database) is Hollywood Dun It's most commercially prominent son and has produced the 2025 Run For A Million Champion through Flexing Guns N Town -- confirming that Hollywood Dun It genetics continue to produce elite performers two and three generations removed from the foundation sire. Walla Walla Dun It provides breeders the opportunity to access that Hollywood Jac 86 x Blossom Berry foundation directly through a 2004 son whose dam Walla Walla Wanda contributes Taris Catalyst athleticism and the Walla bloodline that connects to multiple Oswood breeding stallions. For breeders seeking to outcross heavily from the Gunnatrashya and Colonels Smoking Gun bloodlines that dominate the current Oswood roster, Walla Walla Dun It's Hollywood Jac 86 foundation genetics offer a complementary cross at the lowest stud fee on the station's roster.
