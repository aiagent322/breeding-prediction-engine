NRCHA SBF Open Finalist confirmed. The NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity Open is one of the premier NRCHA annual events, bringing together the top reined cow horse prospects to compete across three scored phases. Placing as an Open Finalist confirms that Metallic Masterpiece demonstrated the cattle handling ability, rein work precision, and athletic collection required to compete against the best NRCHA futurity prospects in the country. His APHA dual registration confirms he is solid paint-bred on one side, adding color genetic possibilities for breeders seeking roan or paint offspring.

The Metallic Cat x Smart Little Lena dam cross pairs the #2 All-Time NCHA sire with the #5 All-Time NCHA sire through Kings Masterpiece. This double infusion of elite NCHA cutting genetics creates offspring with exceptional natural cow sense, athletic ability, and the trainability demanded at the NRCHA futurity level. Metallic Cat brings the High Brow Cat x Chers Shadow pedigree that has produced $83M+ in offspring earnings, while Smart Little Lena through Kings Masterpiece adds the consistent cutting horse foundation that has been producing champions since the 1980s. At 14.1H and 1050 lbs Metallic Masterpiece is a compact cutting horse type whose bone density and athletic proportions suit both cutting and cow horse competition.

No offspring data separately confirmed in available sources.
