Performance Summary

Metallic Masterpiece -- 2011 Sorrel Stallion. 14.1H, 1050 lbs. AQHA 5424611 / APHA 1105085. By Metallic Cat (High Brow Cat x Chers Shadow/Peptoboonsmal -- PE $83,631,404, QData #1 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire and NRCHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire -- full profile add001; ICSI only) out of Kings Masterpiece (Peppy San Badger x -- Peppy San Badger sired $25M+ in offspring from 2,325 AQHA foals; NCHA Futurity 1977 Champion; AQHA Hall of Fame 2008; deceased 2005; Kings Masterpiece produce record not further confirmed in available sources). Owned by Silver Spur Ranches. 6666 Ranch, Guthrie TX. LTE $73,749 confirmed 6666stallions.com roster. Stud fee not confirmed in available sources -- contact 6666 Ranch at (806) 386-1911. Transported cooled semen available. First used for breeding 2016 confirmed Silver Spur Ranches. Silver Spur Ranches: 'He possesses the athletic ability of a champion combined with a trainable mind. We are fortunate that this stallion has been used in branding and ranch work, as well as being an open finalist in the prestigious NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity.' Source: https://www.6666ranch.com/quarter-horses/stallions/metallic-masterpiece/ Confirmed show record from Silver Spur Ranches stallion page: NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity Open Finalist; NRCHA Intermediate Open Derby Reserve Champion; Colorado RCHA Mid America Open Two-Rein Champion; Colorado AQHA Ranching Heritage 5/6-Year-Old Open Champion; NRCHA Futurity AQHA Ranching Heritage 4-Year-Old Working Ranch Horse Open Champion; NRCHA Futurity AQHA Ranching Heritage 5/6-Year-Old Working Ranch Horse Open Champion; Southwest RCHA Fall Open Derby Co-Reserve Champion. NRCHA personal earnings of $64,989 confirmed Quarter Horse News NRCHA Million Dollar Sire article (listed among top Metallic Cat NRCHA offspring). Confirmed offspring from Silver Spur Ranches: Spark Master $99,516 NRCHA Open Futurity Reserve Champion; Genuine Masterpiece $91,199 AQHA World Champion Junior Working Cow Horse; Master Merada $74,498 NCHA Open Super Stakes Co-Champion. Silver Spur Ranches is one of the largest cattle operations in the US encompassing Wyoming, New Mexico, Nebraska, and Colorado and is an AQHA Legacy Breeder and 2017 Zoetis AQHA Best Remuda Award winner -- Metallic Masterpiece serves as a genuine working ranch horse sire with validated offspring results across NRCHA, AQHA, and NCHA disciplines.
