Performance Summary

Smooth Talkin Style 'Talker' -- 2011 Sorrel Stallion. AQHA/APHA. By Smooth As A Cat (LTE $474,325; PE $36,594,820; QData and NCHA #5 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire; NCHA Horse of the Year; 'the only NCHA Horse of the Year to sire not only one but two NCHA Open Horses of the Year' per in-foal partners; one of only three stallions in NCHA history to sire both an NCHA HoY and an NCHA World Champion Open or Non-Pro horse, alongside Smart Little Lena and High Brow Cat) out of Stylish Play Lena (Docs Stylish Oak x Play Lena/Freckles Playboy -- LTE $264,473; QData and NCHA #4 All-Time Leading Cutting Dam confirmed Valley Equine 2026 and multiple sources; PE $2,392,989; 2001 South Point Futurity 4YO Open Reserve Champion; 2003 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5/6YO Open Champion; 2003 Abilene Spectacular 5/6YO Open Champion; dam of Hottish $301,843 PE $23.8M+ 2025 QData #3, Smooth Talkin Style, Pepto Boom $316,324, Areybe $160,250 currently showing; produce earnings $2M+ with grandget $9M+). 5-Panel N/N. Bred by Double Dove Ranch. Sold to Teton Ridge Ranch 2021 (with NCHA Futurity Champion All Spice and 2020 NCHA Open HoY Twice In Santiago); sold again at 2025 Teton Ridge Sale September 29, 2025 at Western Bloodstock. Now owned by XIT Ranch and Bosque Ranch Performance Horses. Previously stood at Brazos Valley Stallion Station / Valley Equine; no longer at Valley Equine but foals remain eligible for Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive through 2029 NCHA Futurity per Valley Equine Super Stallion page. LTE $305,284. 2015 NCHA Open Horse of the Year -- five major titles in his first nine months of aged event competition. Confirmed show record: 2014 NCHA Open Futurity 3YO Reserve Champion (with Lloyd Cox); 2015 Breeders Invitational 4YO Open Champion; 2015 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Open Co-Champion; 2015 Arbuckle Mountain Futurity 4YO Open Co-Champion; multiple other 2015 limited age event finals and placings. QHN: 'a campaign that included Derby Open Championships at the NCHA Super Stakes, Breeders Invitational and the Arbuckle Mountain Futurity.' Jeremy Barwick: 'We were very interested in Smooth Talkin Style because of his ability to sire not only cutting, but also cow horse winners. He also has some very promising foals in the roping and barrel horse industry. We were looking for a sire for as many disciplines as possible which we feel is important in today's market.' PE $9,390,419 (Western Bloodstock September 2025 -- most current confirmed figure). Leading Freshman Sire of 2020. 2023 QData and NCHA Top 12 Leading Cutting Sire. 2023 QData Top 12 Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire. QData #6 Leading Cow Horse Sire. Top confirmed offspring: Storyteler ($383,249 -- 2024 American Performance Horseman Open Cutting Champion, split 3rd NCHA Super Stakes Open Derby, 3rd NCHA Super Stakes Open Classic, split 3rd Run For A Million Cutting Challenge; Adan Banuelos: 'he shows up to work as if it's his personal playground'); A Shoot N Star ($312,326 -- NCHA Super Stakes Limited Non-Pro Classic Champion, NCHA Open Futurity finalist, swept NCHA Intermediate Open and Limited Open Futurities; Colt Moore: 'I've never felt a horse go that fast and be that smart in that big of a moment'); Reys Talkin ($290,791 -- 3rd NCHA Limited Non-Pro Futurity, Cascades Cutting Non-Pro Classic Reserve Champion); SJR Talkin Diamonds ($203,712 -- 2024 American Performance Horseman Open Working Cow Horse Champion); Hez Talkin Smack ($124,821 -- 2025 RFA Tie-Down Roping High Stakes Slot Open Champion); WhatUTalkinAbout ($102,159 -- NSHA Open Futurity Reserve Champion); Gingersnaps ($128,717 -- PCCHA Derby Open Champion). Cross-discipline offspring earnings confirm exceptional versatility: cutting, reined cow horse, roping, and barrel racing all represented among top earners.
