Performance Summary

Metallic Cat 'Denver' -- 2005 Red Roan Stallion. By High Brow Cat (High Brow Hickory x Smart Little Kitty/Smart Little Lena -- LTE $110,784; AQHA/NCHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire 2011-2020; EquiStat #1 Leading Cutting Sire 2003-2020; EquiStat #1 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire 2009-2020; NRCHA All-Time Leading Sire; PE $97M+; sire of a record 8 NCHA Open Futurity Champions and 56 AQHA Champions across five disciplines; inducted into AQHA Quarter Horse Hall of Fame 2023) out of Chers Shadow (Peptoboonsmal x Shesa Smarty Lena/Smart Little Lena -- LTE $1,675, Abilene Spectacular Open Derby finalist). Peptoboonsmal (Chers Shadow's sire): LTE $180,487, 1995 NCHA Futurity Open Champion, PE $20M+, sire of Once In A Blu Boon and Badboonarising's sire. Shesa Smarty Lena (Chers Shadow's dam, by Smart Little Lena -- NCHA Triple Crown Champion and #2 All-Time NCHA Sire) produced Patera Cat ($87,440 -- Breeders Invitational Non-Pro Classic Champion, NCHA Super Stakes Non-Pro Classic Co-Champion), Cherrey by Dual Rey ($27,646), and Dual Cher by Dual Pep ($27,038). Bred by the Roan Rangers (Alvin and Becky Fults, Fults Ranch). Shown by Beau Galyean (NCHA Hall of Fame earner, $3M+ rider) who piloted Denver to every title. Purchased by Bobby Patton, Rocking P Ranch, Weatherford TX in 2017. Presenting sponsor of the NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity. Owned and managed through Rocking P Ranch with semen available at Valley Equine, Stephenville TX -- ICSI and frozen only. LTE $637,711 -- highest money earner of all High Brow Cat offspring and the highest money-earning aged event stallion in a 32-year history at time of documentation. Made exactly 12 trips to the show pen -- never missed a finals, never lost a cow in the cutting pen. Confirmed show record: 2008 NCHA Open Futurity Champion; 2009 NCHA Open Horse of the Year; NCHA Open Super Stakes Reserve Champion; NCHA Hall of Fame inducted 2010. PE $83,631,404 (Valley Equine confirmed 2026). Led NCHA sire standings every year 2016 through 2024 -- 9 consecutive years. Led NRCHA sire standings 2018 through 2024 -- 7 consecutive years. 2024 QData #1 Leading Cutting Sire. QData and NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire. QData and NRCHA #1 All-Time Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire. 2016, 2017, 2019 NRCHA Sire of the Year; 2016 and 2017 NCHA Sire of the Year. 720+ money-earning offspring holding 2,350+ event championships and 1,852+ reserve championships across 14 disciplines (2018 figure -- substantially higher in 2026). Offspring span cutting, reined cow horse, reining, barrel racing, halter, ranch horse, and roping. Top confirmed offspring: Metallic Rebel (Sweet Abra/Abrakadabracre, LTE $438K+, 2017 NCHA HoY, NCHA HoF, PE $16.6M+, NRCHA Million Dollar Sire -- sire of 2023 NCHA Futurity Champion Lone Woolf LTE $668,310); Stevie Rey Von (Miss Ella Rey/Dual Rey, LTE $420,559, 2015 NCHA Futurity Champion, NCHA HoF, PE $20M+, #1 QData Leading Sire 2025); Check Yer Metal (Checkers Live Oak/Im Countin Checks, LTE $449,312, standing at Valley Equine); Swing Wide (Dually Noted/Dual Pep, LTE $353K+, standing at Valley Equine); Fiddle And Steel (Lil Bit Wreckless/CD Royal, LTE $203K+, 2020 NCHA Futurity Non-Pro Champion, 2021 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Non-Pro Champion scored 225.5); Audeycious (2026 NCHA Super Stakes Non-Pro Co-Reserve Champion, scored 218 with Kristen Galyean); Aristotallic (2026 NCHA Super Stakes Limited Amateur Co-Reserve Champion, scored 214). NRHA top performers include Call Me Mitch, Cats Picasso, Ricato Suave, Hazardouz Material, and Moonshineandtwoadvil -- each with over $100,000 in NRCHA earnings. Clayton Edsall (NRCHA Million Dollar Rider): 'They're attractive but the biggest thing is that you have a trainable mind. One of the reasons you see so many Metallic Cats in the show pen, the winner's circle and the sale is that they have the ability to be trained and be successful. I've found that even if one doesn't make a cutting horse, it makes a cow horse or a rope horse. He checks a lot of boxes as a breeder -- color, size and bone, athleticism and mind.' The depth of the Metallic Cat legacy is confirmed by the successor generation -- Stevie Rey Von, Metallic Rebel, Rollz Royce, Swing Wide, and Fiddle And Steel are all Metallic Cat sons now standing as leading sires -- an unprecedented generational depth in Western performance horse history.
