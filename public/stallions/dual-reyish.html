By Hottish (Spots Hot x Shes Hott/Smart Little Lena -- LTE $301,843; 2012 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Non-Pro Champion; 2012 Breeders Invitational 4YO Open Champion; 2012 NCHA Summer Spectacular 4YO Open Champion; 2012 South Point Futurity 4YO Open Champion; 2012 Wrangler Futurity 4YO Open Reserve Champion; 2012 Idaho Futurity 4YO Open Reserve Champion; 2014 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5/6YO Non-Pro Champion; PE $4.1M+ at time of death; ranked #4 among 2020s leading cutting sires at time of death -- behind only Metallic Cat, Dual Rey, and High Brow Cat; humanely euthanized February 7 at Springtown TX ranch of Dustin and Deena Adams) out of A Little Reylena (Dual Rey x Katie Little Lena/Smart Little Lena -- LTE $126,608; confirmed show record: 2006 Utah Futurity 3rd 3YO Open, 2006 Idaho Futurity 3YO Open finalist, 2008 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5/6YO Non-Pro 50K Champion, 2008 Utah Futurity 5/6YO Non-Pro Co-Champion, 2009 NCHA Super Stakes 5/6YO Non-Pro Reserve Champion, 2009 The Mane Event 5/6YO Non-Pro Reserve Champion; QData and NCHA Top 15 All-Time Leading Dam; confirmed produce earnings $1.5M+ including Dual Reyish $452K, Lone Woolf $668,310 2023 NCHA Open Futurity Champion and NCHA Horse of the Year, Hay U $200,245 multiple Non-Pro champion, Rey To Sly $103,208). Dual Rey (A Little Reylena's sire): LTE $105,038, AQHA Hall of Fame, QData and NCHA #3 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, PE $57.9M+, 65 World or Reserve World Champion offspring. 6-Panel N/N. Owned by Billy Wolf, Wolf Livestock, Whitesboro TX. ICSI Only. Highest money-earning son of Hottish. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $452,687. 2017 NCHA Open Futurity Champion. NCHA Hall of Fame. Described as the perfect outcross for conformation, athleticism, cow smarts, great mind, and perfect disposition. Multiple Aged Event Champion. Confirmed full show record: 2017 NCHA Open Futurity Champion; 2018 Cotton Stakes Open Derby Reserve Champion; 2018 Southern Futurity Open Derby Reserve Champion; 2018 AQHA Congress Open Derby Champion; 2018 Brazos Bash Open Derby Top 5 finalist; 2018 NCHA Super Stakes Open Derby finalist; 2018 Augusta Futurity Open Derby finalist; 2018 Bonanza Cutting Open Derby finalist; 2019 NCHA Eastern Nationals Classic Open Champion; 2019 NCHA Summer Spectacular Open Classic finalist; 2019 Cotton Stakes Open Classic finalist. PE $2,517,756 (Valley Equine 2026). 2025 QData Top 20 Leading Cutting Sire. Top confirmed offspring: LBR Hangem (2025 NCHA Open Classic/Challenge Co-Champion, scored 228 with Kenny Platt for Solo Select Horses, NCHA Hall of Fame inductee at this event, $32,696 earned -- confirmed Valley Equine April 2026 Super Stakes recap); Weezy F Baby (2025 NCHA Non-Pro Futurity Champion -- confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup); Xxplosive (2026 NCHA Super Stakes Non-Pro Co-Reserve Champion, scored 218 with Kristen Galyean for Glover/Galyean Partnership LLC, $38,937). The combination of Hottish speed and athleticism over the deep Dual Rey dam line -- A Little Reylena is a confirmed QData/NCHA Top 15 All-Time Leading Dam -- creates a cross with two generations of elite NCHA titleholders on both sides. Dual Reyish's sire Hottish and his dam's sire Dual Rey were both leading NCHA sires in their own right, making this a convergence of two of the most productive cutting bloodlines of the modern era. His own production is accelerating: LBR Hangem's 228 score at the 2026 Super Stakes placed him among the elite performances of the year and added an NCHA Hall of Fame induction to Dual Reyish's sire report.
