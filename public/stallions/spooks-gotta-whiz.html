Performance Summary

Spooks Gotta Whiz -- 2006 Blue Roan Stallion. By Spooks Gotta Gun (Colonels Smoking Gun x Miss Silver Spook/Smart Spook -- LTE $130,694; NRHA $1 Million Sire; leading reining sire; Colonels Smoking Gun sire line also produced Gunnatrashya osw001, Americasnexttopgun osw004, and Colonels Dancing Gun osw009 in this database -- the Colonels Smoking Gun dynasty is the backbone of the Oswood Stallion Station reining program) out of Prettywhizprettydoes (Topsail Whiz x -- Topsail Whiz is a foundational NRHA sire and Hall of Fame inductee with PE $5M+; dam of Spooks Gotta Whiz contributes elite Topsail Whiz athleticism, stop power, and the famous Whiz-line trainability to the pedigree, creating a dual sire-line pedigree that combines the Colonel Freckles/Colonels Smoking Gun foundation with the Topsail Whiz movement and athleticism). Owned by Ariat International. Oswood Stallion Station, Weatherford TX. LTE $346,715 confirmed Oswood roster. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/spooks-gotta-whiz/ Confirmed show record from Oswood Stallion Station and NRHA records: 2010 NRHA Open Futurity Champion; 2012 NRBC Open Derby Champion; 2012 NRHA Open Derby Champion; World Equestrian Games Triple Crown Champion; NRHA Hall of Fame inductee. Sire earnings $14M+ confirmed Oswood roster. Spooks Gotta Whiz compiled one of the most decorated show records in modern reining history. The 2010 NRHA Open Futurity Championship established him as an elite performer from the start of his competitive career. His 2012 season was exceptional -- winning both the NRBC Open Derby and the NRHA Open Derby in the same year, then capping the performance with the WEG Triple Crown Championship. The World Equestrian Games Triple Crown is one of the most prestigious achievements in international reining, requiring a horse to win all three phases of competition against the top reining horses in the world. His NRHA Hall of Fame induction recognizes a career that placed him among the sport's all-time elite performers. As a sire, Spooks Gotta Whiz has built a significant produce record with $14M+ in offspring earnings spanning reining, reined cow horse, and ranch horse disciplines. The cross of Spooks Gotta Gun's Colonels Smoking Gun foundation with Topsail Whiz athleticism through Prettywhizprettydoes creates a pedigree that stamps offspring with powerful stops, fluid spins, and the mental trainability that defines elite reining bloodlines. His offspring compete at the highest levels of the NRHA and NRCHA, carrying forward the dual sire-line legacy of both Colonels Smoking Gun (through Spooks Gotta Gun) and Topsail Whiz (through the dam) -- two of the most influential reining sire lines of the modern era. Standing at Oswood Stallion Station alongside multiple Colonels Smoking Gun descendants makes this one of the premier reining stallion rosters in the industry. The consistent production of $14M+ in offspring earnings across multiple disciplines including reining, cow horse, and ranch horse confirms that the Spooks Gotta Whiz cross is a proven, commercially successful option for breeders seeking elite western performance genetics.
