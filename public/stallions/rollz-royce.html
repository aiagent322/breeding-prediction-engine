Rollz Royce -- 2014 Sorrel Stallion, 14.1H. By Dual Smart Rey (Dual Rey x The Smart Look -- LTE $330,436; 2006 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Open Champion; 2006 Bonanza 4YO Open Champion; 2006 AQHA World Champion Junior Cutting Horse; 2005 NCHA Futurity 3YO Open semifinalist; 2007 Abilene Spectacular 5/6YO Open Reserve Champion; 2007 Augusta Futurity 5/6YO Open Reserve Champion; PE $11M+ by 2022, confirmed NCHA 2022 announcement; sire of Rollz Royce, Dual Smart Kitty $317,758 NCHA Open Futurity Champion, Carolena Reyn $275,587 NCHA Non-Pro Super Stakes Co-Champion) out of Show Biz Kitty (High Brow Cat x Show Biz Sandy/Son Ofa Doc -- confirmed QData/NCHA #1 All-Time Leading Dam -- Valley Equine 2026 lineup states 'Show Biz Kitty, the industry's #1 All-Time Leading Dam'; suffered serious hock injury in 2005 before planned 2006 NCHA Futurity debut, never shown; purchased by Royce Stallcup in 2013 after seeing her potential as a broodmare; confirmed produce: Kittennish $729,112 NCHA HoY by Hottish, Rollz Royce by Dual Smart Rey, Badboonarising $367,101 NCHA Open Futurity Reserve Champion by Once In A Blu Boon, Storyteller $381,604 by Smooth Talkin Style, SS Blacks Lil Kitty $188,241 by Blue Bayou Boon; dam produce earnings $970,000+ at earlier count, now substantially higher). Show Biz Sandy (Show Biz Kitty's dam by Son Ofa Doc): LTE $91,889; produce $370,000+; grandget $1.7M+. 4-Panel N/N, GBED N/G. Bred by Starkey Smith. Owned by Tom and Lisa Guinn, Philadelphia MS -- same ownership as Metallic Rebel. Retired to stallion barn 2021 after claiming full books since first standing. Stud fee $7,500, booked full for 2026. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $354,032. In-foal partners: 'Rollz Royce could dance in the show pen like no other, his ability to mirror a cow left his fans in awe.' EquiStat Leading Cutting Freshman Sire. PE $4,979,851 (Valley Equine 2026). 2025 QData #6 Leading Cutting Sire. Top confirmed offspring: Rollz Eh (x This Cats Smilen/WR This Cats Smart, LTE $720,000+, 2025 NCHA Open Horse of the Year, NCHA Open Futurity Reserve Champion, NCHA Super Stakes Open Derby Champion, PCCHA Open Derby Co-Champion, shown by Austin Shepard for owner Billy Wolf -- confirmed Valley Equine Dec 2025); Nu Rollz Royce (2026 NCHA Super Stakes Amateur Reserve Champion, scored 221.5 with Elle Elizabeth Martin for Nancy and Steve Martin, $14,065 -- confirmed Valley Equine April 2026); Rollz (2026 NCHA Super Stakes 5/6YO Open Reserve Champion, scored 216 with Paul Wasyln for Harold Bailey, $6,680 -- confirmed Valley Equine April 2026); NCHA Non-Pro Futurity Champion (confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup).
