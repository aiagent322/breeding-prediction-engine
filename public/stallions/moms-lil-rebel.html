Performance Summary

Moms Lil Rebel -- 2019 Bay Stallion. By Metallic Rebel (Metallic Cat x Sweet Abra/Abrakadabracre -- LTE $438,315, NCHA Open Horse of the Year 2017, NCHA Hall of Fame, NCHA World Champion Stallion, PE $16.6M+, 2025 QData #2 Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire, NRCHA Million Dollar Sire -- full profile bvss004) out of SDP Moms CD (High Brow CD x -- LTE $133,129; producer of $526,000+ confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup; from SDP Buffalo Ranch). High Brow CD (SDP Moms CD's sire, High Brow Cat x Sweet Little CD/CD Olena): LTE $542,101; 2007 NCHA Open Futurity Champion; 2008 NCHA Horse of the Year; 2011 NCHA Champions Cup Champion; NCHA Hall of Fame; PE $8,644,760; described as 'the only stallion in history to win the NCHA Futurity and then 5 major events at age 4.' The pedigree of Moms Lil Rebel creates a significant concentration of High Brow Cat influence: Metallic Rebel is by Metallic Cat (son of High Brow Cat) and SDP Moms CD is by High Brow CD (son of High Brow Cat), meaning High Brow Cat appears twice in the first three generations -- a pedigree structure that consistently produces elite cow sense and athletic ability. Stud fee $3,500. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Foals eligible: Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive, Breeders Invitational, NCHA Super Stakes. Semen availability: GREEN designation on Valley Equine Breeding Calendar. LTE $422,270 (Valley Equine 2026 confirmed). Valley Equine 2026 lineup: 'Moms Lil Rebel built his record through consistent finals appearances across multiple major aged events, earning at a level that reflects durability and competitiveness in strong company.' Confirmed title: 2022 NCHA Futurity Brazos Bash Valley Super Stallions Open Incentive Champion -- confirmed Valley Equine stallion page. Still actively showing in 2025 per Valley Equine page (2025 Show Schedule listed). No foal crop data yet; first foals approaching show age. Metallic Rebel's influence as a sire makes this stallion an attractive early entry: the sire of Moms Lil Rebel is the 2025 #2 Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire and continues to produce at the top of multiple disciplines. Valley Equine 2026 on Metallic Rebel: 'Metallic Rebel, the 2025 #2 Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire, and out of SDP Moms CD, an earner of $133,129 who has gone on to produce more than $526,000.' Breeders targeting Metallic Rebel genetics with a High Brow Cat-line dam cross have a compelling option in Moms Lil Rebel. The double High Brow Cat concentration in this pedigree combined with proven Metallic Cat sire-line production makes him a strong candidate for cutting and cow horse programs alike.
