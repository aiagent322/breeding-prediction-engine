Full Profile

Bet Hesa Cat — 2006 Red Roan, 14.2H, 1050 lbs. By High Brow Cat (High Brow Hickory x Smart Little Kitty/Smart Little Lena — NCHA and AQHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire with PE $98.7M+, sire of a record 8 NCHA Open Futurity Champions, QData All-Time #1 Cutting Sire, also #1 Leading Sire of Sires and #1 Leading Sire of Dams — inducted into the AQHA Quarter Horse Hall of Fame 2023; High Brow Cat's offspring are described as producing horses with elite cow sense, athleticism, and pure trainability and intelligence across all western performance disciplines) out of Bet Yer Blue Boons (Freckles Playboy x Royal Blue Boon/Boon Bar — LTE $350,615, 2000 NCHA Open World Champion, 1997 NCHA Open Reserve World Champion, NCHA Hall of Fame, dam of foals earning nearly $6M). Bet Yer Blue Boons is a half-sibling to Im Countin Checks ($514,757, PE $5.6M+, NCHA Hall of Fame) making Autumn Boon's Boon Bar female family one of the most productive in cutting history. LTE $272,151.

2011 NCHA Open World Champion, shown by Austin Shepard (NCHA Hall of Fame earner $8.4M — nine-time NCHA Eastern Nationals Open Champion and one of the most decorated cutting horse riders of his generation). NCHA Hall of Fame. The only NCHA World Champion Stallion in history whose dam is also an NCHA World Champion. The first horse to win the NCHA Open World Championship Stallion title at age 5.

NRCHA Million Dollar Sire. One of only four stallions in history to sire an NRCHA Open Snaffle Bit Futurity Champion and Reserve Champion in the same year. PE $4M+. Top offspring: Bet He Sparks (2024 American Performance Horseman Team Champion Working Cow Horse, 2023 World's Greatest Horseman, NRCHA SBF Intermediate Open Reserve Champion, LTE $298K+), Plain Catty (2017 NRCHA SBF Open Champion, LTE $183K), Bet Hes Black (2017 NRCHA SBF Open Reserve Champion), Bet Hesa Boon (2025 NRCHA World's Greatest Horseman Herd Work Champion), Bet Hes Blu (NSHA RCH Open Futurity Finalist, NRCHA Open Futurity Finalist).

High Brow Cat passes elite cow sense, athleticism, and trainable intelligence — his offspring are consistent across all western performance disciplines from cutting to reined cow horse to ranch versatility. Bet Yer Blue Boons adds world-champion competitive drive and the proven Boon Bar/Royal Blue Boon dam-line athleticism. The combination of High Brow Cat's dominant gene pool with a World Champion dam who is herself a proven producer creates offspring with exceptional range across cutting, cow horse, and ranch disciplines. Described at 6666 Ranch as an all-around type stallion equally suited to cutting and cow horse programs.

6-Panel Negative — all genetic tests clean. An ideal cross for High Brow Cat and Metallic Cat mares seeking Peptoboonsmal-line genetics without HERDA risk. Stud fee $4,000. Standing at 6666 Ranch, Guthrie TX.
