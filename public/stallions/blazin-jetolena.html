Full Profile

Blazin Jetolena — 1999 Sorrel. By Lenas Sugar Daddy (Doc O'Lena son born 1978 — purchased from Shorty Freeman, Doc O'Lena's trainer; injured as a 2-year-old which limited his show career; "offspring have won numerous roping, barrel, cutting and reining titles") out of Blazin Jennie Jet (Jet Of Honor) — a gifted barrel and gymkhana mare who set local arena records in the Pacific Northwest, earned $20,000+ at open jackpots and amateur rodeos, and won a go-round at the Puyallup Pro Rodeo under PRCA conditions. Lenas Sugar Daddy carries Doc O'Lena (Doc Bar x Poco Lena — AQHA/NCHA Hall of Fame, described as "a thinking, cowy, smart horse with a connection with the cow" who passed intelligence and trainability to his get; sired Smart Little Lena, first NCHA Triple Crown winner). Blazin Jetolena is described as: "The speed of Jet Deck, the mind of Doc O'Lena, and the ability of both these legendary sires." LTE $95,201 (EquiStat) — $190,000 total lifetime barrel earnings (AQHA).

PE $12,209,452. Deceased January 17, 2023 — sudden cardiac event at 24 years old. ICSI only at Valley Equine. Bred by Randy and Sue Rist, owned by Busby Quarter Horses (Jeff and Andrea Busby) from 2013 until death.

He was the No. 1 Barrel Futurity Horse in the nation in 2003 under trainer Ryan Lovendahl — winning seven futurities and earning $76,208 in his 4-year-old year alone. Qualified for the 2005 Wrangler National Finals Rodeo with Melanie Southard. AQHA Register of Merit in barrel racing and pole bending.

Stood as a leading barrel sire for 15 consecutive years. 394 money-earning offspring. No. 15 all-time leading maternal sire for barrel horses with $2.8M in grandget earnings.

Top offspring: Jets Top Gun "Benny" (2024 NFR Average Champion — set the record for fastest aggregate time across all 10 NFR runs, 2023 WPRA World Championship, LTE $1M+), Famous Lil Jet "Rollo" (4th highest earning barrel horse of all time at $1.3M, 2022 WPRA World Champion with Jordon Briggs — by Dash Ta Fame out of Blazin Black Beauty by Blazin Jetolena), Blingolena (2024 NFR qualifier, LTE $417K+), Sheza Blazin Move (3x NFR qualifier, LTE $384,900). Son Blazin Honor earns in both barrels and team roping, and his offspring cross well on High Brow Cat, Metallic Cat, and Peptoboonsmal daughters with documented "exceptional trainability and adaptability." Offspring described: "quick footed, want to work, and have great minds which make them incredibly trainable." Valley Equine (ICSI), Stephenville TX.
