Full Profile

Blazin Jetolena -- 1999 Sorrel, 14.3H. AQHA 4459515. By Lenas Sugar Daddy (1978 Doc O'Lena son -- born at Shorty Freeman's operation, the trainer who showed Doc O'Lena to the 1970 NCHA Futurity Championship; purchased from Freeman by Wayland and Elaine Heatley of Marana, Arizona; injured as a 2-year-old which limited his show career; offspring won roping, barrel, cutting, and reining titles across multiple disciplines) out of Blazin Jennie Jet (Jet Of Honor -- a local Pacific Northwest stallion with documented NFR-qualifying get; purchased as a 3-year-old by Randy Rist in 1986 in Snohomish, Washington; shown by Sue Rist; set multiple local arena records in barrel racing and gymkhana; earned over $20,000 at open jackpots and amateur rodeos; won a go-round at the Puyallup Pro Rodeo under PRCA conditions; died 2001). Bred and raised by Randy and Sue Rist.

Acquired by Jeff and Andrea Busby of Busby Quarter Horses, Millsap, Texas in 2013 -- their cornerstone stallion. Died January 17, 2023 of a sudden cardiac event at age 24. ICSI only. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX.

LTE $95,201 EquiStat -- $190,000 total lifetime barrel earnings (AQHA). AQHA Register of Merit in barrel racing and pole bending. No. 1 Barrel Futurity Horse in the nation in 2003 under trainer Ryan Lovendahl -- winning seven futurities and $76,208 in his 4-year-old year alone.

Qualified for the 2005 Wrangler National Finals Rodeo with Melanie Southard. Andrea Busby: 'I felt like Blazin Jetolena took us on a magic carpet ride I never even dreamed we'd go on. Blazin Jetolena legitimately changed the course of my life, and not many people get to say a horse did that.' PE $10,415,538 (EquiStat All-Time Barrel Racing Leaders -- 395 money-earning offspring, $26,368 average). Lenas Sugar Daddy (his sire line) ranks identically with $10,416,869 confirming the full genetic contribution.

Blazin Jetolena ranked No. 1 sire in 2024 EquiStat barrel racing leaders with $675,752 in a single year from 33 offspring -- posthumously. Top confirmed offspring: Jets Top Gun 'Benny' ($1,084,294 -- 2024 NFR Average Champion, set the all-time record for fastest aggregate time across all 10 NFR runs; 2023 WPRA World Champion with Brittany Pozzi Tonozzi; 2023 Calgary Stampede, Salt Lake City, Reno Rodeo, and Dodge City Round-Up Champion; 2025 WNFR Average Champion; owned and bred by Busby Quarter Horses); Blingolena ($554,644 -- 2024 NFR Reserve Champion or Co-Reserve Champion of 5 barrel rounds, Patriot Vegas World Open 1D Finals Champion, Old Fort Days Champion, multiple NBHA and rodeo titles, 2024 Ruby Buckle East Open 1D Reserve Champion); Sheza Blazin Move ($385,029 -- 3x NFR qualifier, 3rd and 5th NFR barrel rounds, AQHA Reserve World Champion Junior Barrel Horse, All American Finals Reserve Champion); Jets Letters Ta Abby ($277,915); JR Naughty Jet ($250,266 -- Cheyenne Frontier Days and Pendleton short round qualifiers); Jets Heart N Soul ($235,880); Blazin Babe Olena ($204,970); KG Jukebox Hero ($195,304); Blazin Sugar Daddy ($190,752). As maternal grandsire: Famous Lil Jet by Dash Ta Fame out of Blazin Black Beauty (Blazin Jetolena daughter) -- LTE $1,324,495, 2022 WPRA World Champion with Jordon Briggs, 4th highest-earning barrel horse of all time.

No. 15 all-time leading maternal grandsire for barrel horses at $2.8M+ in grandget earnings. Described in his own promotional material: 'The speed of Jet Deck, the mind of Doc O'Lena, and the ability of both these legendary sires have come together in Blazin Jetolena to achieve the ultimate barrel horse.' Offspring described as 'quick footed, want to work, and have great minds which make them incredibly trainable.' ICSI dose available at Valley Equine.
