Full Profile

One Time Pepto -- 2001 Red Roan Stallion. By Peptoboonsmal (Peppy San Badger x Royal Blue Boon -- LTE $180,487; 2000 NCHA Open Futurity Champion; NCHA Hall of Fame; PE $24,000,000+; QData All-Time Leading Cutting Sire; one of the most dominant sire lines in modern cutting horse history; standing at Oswood Stallion Station -- an anchor of the station's cutting horse pedigree depth) out of One Time Soon (Smart Little Lena x -- Smart Little Lena is a QData All-Time Leading Cutting Sire with PE $15.4M+; dam produce includes One Time Pepto as top offspring confirming elite producer status). Owned by Shaleah Hester / Oswood Stallion Station. Weatherford TX.

LTE $331,097 confirmed Oswood roster and QData. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Sire earnings $24,000,000+ confirmed Oswood roster and multiple industry sources. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/one-time-pepto/ Confirmed show record from NCHA records and Oswood: 2005 NCHA Open Super Stakes Champion; Augusta Open Classic Champion; Abilene Spectacular Open Classic Champion; NCHA Silver Award.

LTE $331,097 confirmed. One Time Pepto is among the most commercially significant stallions standing at Oswood Stallion Station. The 2005 NCHA Open Super Stakes Championship established him at the top of the cutting horse world in his aged-event season, and his $331,097 LTE reflects sustained championship-level performance across multiple events. The Augusta Open Classic Championship and Abilene Spectacular Open Classic Championship confirm that his Super Stakes title was backed by broad competitive consistency rather than a single standout result.

The pedigree is exceptional. Peptoboonsmal as sire ($180,487 LTE; 2000 NCHA Open Futurity Champion; $24M+ PE) is one of the top five all-time sires in cutting horse history and the source of the foundational Pepto cow sense that defined an era of cutting horse breeding. Smart Little Lena as dam sire (QData All-Time Leading Cutting Sire; PE $15.4M+) adds the legendary Doc O'Lena-line athleticism and trainability. One Time Pepto concentrates two of the most elite cutting horse sire lines in existence on both sides of his pedigree, creating offspring with exceptional dual-line depth.

As a sire with $24M+ in offspring earnings, One Time Pepto has more than validated his breeding career as a continuation of the Peptoboonsmal line. His offspring excel across cutting, reined cow horse, and roping disciplines, confirming the cross-discipline versatility that the Peptoboonsmal x Smart Little Lena combination was expected to produce. The 2025 team roping statistics confirm One Time Pepto remains a leading sire across disciplines with 13 individual money earners on the year in rope horse competition alone, led by Nu One Time Blues ($351,128 heeling futurity earnings). This breadth of offspring success reflects the genetic diversity and adaptability built into his pedigree from both elite cutting sire lines.
