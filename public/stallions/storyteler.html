Performance Summary

Storyteler -- 2019 Sorrel Stallion, AQHA/APHA double-registered. By Smooth Talkin Style (bvss035 -- 2015 NCHA Open Horse of the Year, PE $9,390,419, QData #6 Leading Cow Horse Sire, Leading Freshman Sire 2020 -- full profile bvss035) out of Show Biz Kitty (High Brow Cat x -- NCHA Leading Dam 2023, 2020, and 2018; 3rd Leading Dam 2022; producer of $2M+ confirmed QHN March 2024; 'anticipating becoming the all-time leading Western performance horse dam in the near future' per QHN). Bred by Royce Stallcup. Owned by Double Dove Ranch, Fort Worth TX. Shown by Adan Banuelos (NCHA Riders Hall of Fame, NCHA Open Futurity Champion, NCHA Leading Rider 2018/2020/2021/2022, lifetime earnings $7M+). Stud fee $5,000. Foals eligible: Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive, NCHA Super Stakes, Breeders Invitational, PCCHA, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive, Chrome Cash. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Show Biz Kitty is the most accomplished active producing dam in Western performance -- also dam of: All Spice (Once In A Blu Boon x Show Biz Kitty -- 2020 NCHA Open Futurity Champion, NCHA Open Horse of the Year 2020, sold for $5M at 2025 Teton Ridge Sale); Kittennish (Hottish x Show Biz Kitty -- LTE $549,190+, 2025 NCHA Open Horse of the Year confirmed Valley Equine HoY article); Badboonarising (Once In A Blu Boon x Show Biz Kitty -- PE $3,811,012, 2025 QData #7 Leading Cutting Sire -- full profile bvss014). Banuelos at 2024 APH: 'I owe everything to the horse today... My hats off to their mama and what she has done.' Storyteler is a half-sibling to All Spice (2023 APH cutting champion with Banuelos) and Kittennish. The depth of Show Biz Kitty's produce record is unparalleled in the modern era of Western performance horse breeding. LTE $383,249 (Western Bloodstock September 2025 -- most current). Confirmed full show record: 2024 American Performance Horseman Open Cutting Champion -- scored 230 with Adan Banuelos at Globe Life Field in Fort Worth TX, March 2024; confirmed AQHA, APHA, QHN, Western Horseman, Horse Illustrated, multiple sources. Won for a second consecutive year for Banuelos (who won 2023 APH cutting on half-sibling All Spice). Storyteler also named 2024 AQHA Fan Favorite Horse of the Event, voted by those in attendance. NCHA Super Stakes Intermediate Open Derby Co-Reserve Champion -- confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup. Split 3rd Run For A Million Cutting Challenge -- confirmed Western Bloodstock Sept 2025. Split 3rd NCHA Super Stakes Open Derby -- confirmed Western Bloodstock. 3rd NCHA Super Stakes Open Classic -- confirmed Western Bloodstock. Valley Equine 2026: 'Storyteler built his reputation in the spotlight, earning a Co-Reserve Championship at the NCHA Super Stakes Intermediate Open Derby and capturing the Open Cutting title at The American Performance Horseman with a 230. Along the way he became a two-time AQHA Fan Favorite, reflecting both his presence in the pen and his consistency against top-level competition. His show record established him as the highest-earning son of Show Biz Kitty.' Banuelos: 'This horse is phenomenal. He comes to work every day enjoying what he does; he thinks it's a game, and I want to keep it that way as long as I can. I can't tell you how proud of this horse I am. He loves what he does, and so do I.' First foals approaching show age in 2025-2026 -- no offspring data yet.
