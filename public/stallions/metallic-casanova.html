Metallic Casanova -- 2018 Stallion. AQHA. 14.2H. By Metallic Cat (High Brow Cat x Chers Shadow by Peptoboonsmal -- PE $83M+; NCHA Hall of Fame; 2008 NCHA Open Futurity Champion; 2009 NCHA Horse of the Year; NRCHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire; NRCHA Million Dollar Sire; LTE $637,711; the dominant sire in cutting and cow horse history; sire of Fiddle And Steel f6r006, Hashtags bvss027, Metallic Royal Mate f6r011, Metallic Masterpiece f6r010, Ripp Tide f6r013 and many others in this database) out of Dark And Sultry (Spots Hot x -- Spots Hot is by High Brow Cat and is a major NCHA cutting stallion with PE $10M+ standing at Rocking P Ranch; Metallic Casanova is double-bred to High Brow Cat through both his sire Metallic Cat and his dam sire Spots Hot -- both are sons of High Brow Cat, making High Brow Cat Metallic Casanova's grandsire on both sides of his pedigree; this linebreeding concentrates the highest volume of High Brow Cat genetics available in any single breeding individual in the industry today; Dark And Sultry's produce record confirms Metallic Casanova as her standout performer).

Confirmed career highlights: 2023 Champion of Champions Tres Oso Open Derby Champion; 2023 Champion of Champions Western Open Derby Reserve Champion; 4th place Teton Ridge 2023 Open Stallion Stakes Derby; 4th place 2023 Hackamore Event Tulsa; active show career continuing into 2025. Sold as a yearling in 2019 per Pitzer Ranch records. The 2023 Champion of Champions Tres Oso Open Derby Championship is a significant credential.

The Champion of Champions and Tres Oso events are premier Texas cutting horse events that attract elite open professionals and proven horses in the aged-event divisions. A Derby Championship at this level confirms Metallic Casanova as a competitive elite open horse at ages five and six -- the prime demonstration years for cutting horse stallion prospects. His 4th place finish at the Teton Ridge Open Stallion Stakes Derby adds NRCHA cow horse credentials alongside his NCHA cutting wins, confirming his versatility across both disciplines. The double High Brow Cat linebreeding through Metallic Cat (top) and Spots Hot (dam sire) is unique in the current stallion marketplace.

High Brow Cat is the NCHA #1 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire with PE $97M+ and offspring earnings in excess of that figure through eight generations of producers. Metallic Cat with PE $83M+ is the leading son of High Brow Cat and the dominant sire in both NCHA and NRCHA competition. Spots Hot is another major High Brow Cat son with PE $10M+. Breeding both the top sire (Metallic Cat) and the dam sire (Spots Hot) from the same foundation stallion (High Brow Cat) concentrates genetic potential for cow sense, natural athletic collection, and trainability at a level that no other single breeding individual in the industry currently matches.

Breeders crossing Dual Rey daughters or Peptoboonsmal daughters on Metallic Casanova access the double High Brow Cat concentration without the inbreeding concerns that would arise from a closer cross, since Metallic Cat and Spots Hot are half-siblings rather than the same horse. His first foal crops are developing and will be closely watched given the depth of his pedigree credentials and the proven transmission rate of the High Brow Cat x Peptoboonsmal genetic combination across multiple generations of producers.
