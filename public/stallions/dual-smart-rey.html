Dual Smart Rey 'Lloyd' -- 2002 Sorrel Stallion. By Dual Rey (LTE $105,038; PE $46M+; AQHA Hall of Fame; EquiStat 2nd All-Time Leading Cutting Sire; full profile bvss001) out of The Smart Look (Smart Little Lena x -- LTE $48,723; producer of $1.9M+ confirmed Issuu; granddam of performers exceeding $23M confirmed Issuu; also dam of WR This Cats Smart LTE $236,514 2017 EquiStat Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire PE $13.5M+, One Smart Lookin Cat LTE $226,310 2003 NCHA Futurity 3YO Open Champion, SVR Reyl Smart LTE $276,623 2011 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Open Co-Reserve Champion). 4-Panel N/N, GBED N/G. Owned by Strawn Valley Ranch, Don and Michelle Horton, Strawn TX. Valley Equine (formerly Brazos Valley Stallion Station), Stephenville TX. Foals eligible: Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive, Breeders Invitational, NCHA Super Stakes, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive. LTE $330,436 (confirmed multiple sources). PE $16M+ (Stallion Register Directory most current: 'offspring earnings well over $16 Million'). Confirmed full show record from Issuu black type: 2005 NCHA Open Futurity 3YO Open Semi-Finalist; 2006 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Open Champion; 2006 Bonanza 4YO Open Champion; 2006 AQHA World Show Junior Cutting Champion; 2006 Super Stallion Incentive Fund 4YO Open Champion; 2007 Abilene Spectacular 5/6YO Open Reserve Champion; 2007 Augusta Futurity 5/6YO Open Reserve Champion. 2021 NRCHA Million Dollar Sire -- only the 22nd sire in NRCHA history to achieve this milestone confirmed NRCHA.com: 'The NRCHA Million Dollar Sire accolade has only been earned by 22 sires since the association's inception in 1949.' 2017 NCHA Top 10 Leading Sire and 2017 NRCHA Leading Sire confirmed Performance Horse Central. One of only six stallions in 49 years of NCHA history to sire an NCHA Open Futurity Champion, an NCHA Futurity Non-Pro Champion, and an NRCHA Open Snaffle Bit Futurity Champion in three separate events -- confirmed RCHTO and NCHA. In 2019 the only stallion in 39 years to sire the Open and Non-Pro champions in both age divisions of the same NCHA Super Stakes in one year -- confirmed RCHTO. Top offspring: Rollz Royce (Dual Smart Rey x Show Biz Kitty -- LTE $354,032; bvss007 full profile); Hiss N Vinegar ($391,588 -- Stallion Register Directory #1 earner); Dual Smart Kitty ($317,758 -- 2013 NCHA Open Futurity Champion); Duals Lucky Charm ($273,753 -- 2016 NRCHA Open Snaffle Bit Futurity Champion and 2020 NRCHA World's Greatest Horseman Champion); Stuffin My Pockets (2016 NCHA Non-Pro Futurity Champion); Hit Tha Flo (major earner). NCHA 2022: 'offspring earnings surpassing 11 million dollars in 2022... He has produced champions at all levels in cutting, reining and reined cowhorse, including Rollz Royce, Dual Smart Kitty, Hit Tha Flo and Duals Lucky Charm.' Michelle Horton: 'We were counting it down! It's not easy to be a reined cow horse million-dollar stud.'
