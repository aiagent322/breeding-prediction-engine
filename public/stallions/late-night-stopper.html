Confirmed championship record: 2016 NRHA Intermediate Open Derby Reserve Champion; 2016 NRHA Open Derby Top 10; 2016 NRBC Open Derby Finalist; 2015 Cactus Reining Classic Open Derby Finalist, 4th; 2014 NRHA Open Futurity Finalist. The 2016 NRHA Intermediate Open Derby Reserve Championship is a significant credential confirming elite reining ability in one of the most competitive divisions of the NRHA Derby. The Intermediate Open is a high-level division that attracts horses and riders competing just below the Level 4 Open, and a Reserve Championship in this division at the NRHA Derby places Late Night Stopper among the top intermediate open performers in the country for that year.

His concurrent placements in the Level 4 Open Top 10 and the NRBC Open Derby Finalist in the same season confirm that his Intermediate Open performance was backed by genuine elite-level ability across the full spectrum of NRHA Derby competition. The Jacs Electric Spark x Custom Chrome cross pairs two complementary reining sire lines. Jacs Electric Spark (PE $10.3M+) contributes the Shining Spark athleticism and Hollywood Jac 86 competitive fire that has produced NRHA champions across multiple decades. Custom Chrome (1993 NRHA Futurity Champion) through Best Stop adds the stop-power and natural collection that Custom Chrome consistently transmits through his daughters and sons.

Together these two lines create a horse with the balanced package of lope, stop, and competitive consistency that defines a successful NRHA derby performer. At 14.2H with his distinctive smoky black color confirmed by his AQHA papers, Late Night Stopper presents as an athletically correct reining stallion with genetic diversity suitable for crossing on a wide range of mare bloodlines.
