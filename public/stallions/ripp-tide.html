Performance Summary

Ripp Tide -- 2019 Sorrel Stallion. 15H, 1250 lbs. AQHA 6047704 / APHA 1115658. By Metallic Rebel (Metallic Cat x Sweet Abra/Abrakadabracre -- LTE $438,315; 2017 NCHA Open Horse of the Year; NCHA World Champion Stallion; NCHA Hall of Fame; NRCHA Million Dollar Sire; PE $16,600,000+ confirmed Valley Equine 2026; sired Lone Woolf bvss in this database; top NRCHA offspring include A Reyl Rebel $185,981 and ARA Rebel King osw005 in this database; offspring have excelled in cutting, cow horse, roping and ranch versatility) out of Laguna Girl (CD Royal x -- LTE $116,000+ confirmed Metallic Rebel website: 'who won $116,000-plus with the Westfall family'; CD Royal by CD Olena/Doc O'Lena -- CD Olena is a leading NRCHA and NCHA sire PE $17.7M+; Ripp Tide is the first foal from Laguna Girl confirmed Metallic Rebel website). Bred by Russ and Janet Westfall -- the same Westfall family also bred Fiddle And Steel f6r006 and Like I Reckon So who also stand at 6666 Ranch, confirmed Metallic Rebel website. Ridden by Brandon Westfall (multiple NCHA Futurity Non-Pro Champion). Owned by Ripp Tide LLC. 6666 Ranch, Guthrie TX. LTE over $281,000 confirmed 6666stallions.com roster. Stud fee not confirmed in available sources -- contact 6666 Ranch at (806) 386-1911. Transported cooled semen available. Metallic Rebel website: 'He's just been an unreal horse.' The 228.5 score in the Futurity Non-Pro finals is elite -- Futurity Non-Pro finalists are amateur riders, making this score a testament to Ripp Tide's exceptional rideability, trainability, and consistency under pressure. The CD Royal dam line through Laguna Girl adds proven cutting horse genetics from CD Olena, creating a Metallic Cat-family pedigree on both sides through Metallic Rebel (by Metallic Cat) and CD Royal (a proven cutting/cow horse sire). Ripp Tide is connected to other horses in this database through both the Metallic Rebel sire line (ARA Rebel King osw005, Lone Woolf bvss from this database) and the Westfall family breeding program (Fiddle And Steel f6r006 also bred by Russ and Janet Westfall).
