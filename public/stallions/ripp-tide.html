Full Profile

Ripp Tide -- 2019 Sorrel Stallion. 15H, 1250 lbs. AQHA 6047704 / APHA 1115658. By Metallic Rebel (Metallic Cat x Sweet Abra/Abrakadabracre -- LTE $438,315; 2017 NCHA Open Horse of the Year; NCHA World Champion Stallion; NCHA Hall of Fame; NRCHA Million Dollar Sire; PE $16,600,000+ confirmed Valley Equine 2026; sired Lone Woolf bvss in this database; top NRCHA offspring include A Reyl Rebel $185,981 and ARA Rebel King osw005 in this database; offspring have excelled in cutting, cow horse, roping and ranch versatility) out of Laguna Girl (CD Royal x -- LTE $116,000+ confirmed Metallic Rebel website: 'who won $116,000-plus with the Westfall family'; CD Royal by CD Olena/Doc O'Lena -- CD Olena is a leading NRCHA and NCHA sire PE $17.7M+; Ripp Tide is the first foal from Laguna Girl confirmed Metallic Rebel website).

Bred by Russ and Janet Westfall -- the same Westfall family also bred Fiddle And Steel f6r006 and Like I Reckon So who also stand at 6666 Ranch, confirmed Metallic Rebel website. Ridden by Brandon Westfall (multiple NCHA Futurity Non-Pro Champion). Owned by Ripp Tide LLC. 6666 Ranch, Guthrie TX.

LTE over $281,000 confirmed 6666stallions.com roster. Stud fee not confirmed in available sources -- contact 6666 Ranch at (806) 386-1911. Transported cooled semen available.
