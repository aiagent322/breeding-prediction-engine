Full Profile

Badboonarising 'BBR' -- 2015 Black Stallion, 15H, 1,024 lbs. AQHA/APHA. By Once In A Blu Boon (Peptoboonsmal x Autumn Boon/Dual Pep -- LTE $316,564; bred by legendary horseman Bill Freeman who passed just months after the horse's birth, never seeing him show; shown by James Payne; 14 major limited age event championship titles, 2 reserve titles, 29 trips to finals; confirmed titles: 2012 Arbuckle Futurity 4YO Open Champion, 2012 The Non-Pro 4YO Open Champion, 2012 West Texas Futurity 4YO Open Reserve Champion, 2013 Cattlemens Derby 5/6YO Open Champion, 2013 Arbuckle Futurity 5/6YO Open Champion, 2014 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5/6YO Open Champion, 2014 West Texas Futurity 5/6YO Open Champion, 2014 Cattlemens Derby 5/6YO Open Champion, 2014 The Non-Pro 5/6YO Open Champion; PE $12M+, 2025 QHN Stallion Register Cover Stallion; confirmed top offspring: All Spice $556,049 [2021 NCHA Open Horse of the Year], Baylenciaga $307,683 [NCHA Futurity Open finalist], Twice In Santiago $276,587 [2020 NCHA Open Horse of the Year], Here Comes The Boon $250,069 [2019 NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity Open and Intermediate Open Champion], Badboonarising $367,101 [2018 NCHA Futurity Open Reserve Champion]) out of Show Biz Kitty (High Brow Cat x Show Biz Sandy/Son Ofa Doc -- confirmed QData and NCHA #1 All-Time Leading Dam; suffered serious hock injury in 2005 before planned 2006 NCHA Futurity, never shown; produce includes Kittennish $729,112 NCHA Horse of the Year by Hottish, Rollz Royce by Dual Smart Rey, Badboonarising, Storyteller $381,604 by Smooth Talkin Style; dam also of SS Blacks Lil Kitty $187,137). Autumn Boon (Once In A Blu Boon's dam, by Dual Pep): daughter of Royal Blue Boon -- confirmed all-time leading dam in cutting AND leading EquiStat Western performance horse dam; Autumn Boon's produce includes Im Countin Checks $514,757, Once In A Blu Boon $316,564, A Tale Of The Cat $123,818.

Royal Blue Boon is one of the most influential dams in cutting horse history. 6-Panel N/N confirmed. Bred by Plantation Farms LLC. Owned by Plantation Farms LLC.

Shown by Adan Banuelos. Full book for 2026 breeding season. Jill Freeman: 'He was blessed with a great breeder, which was my husband, Bill Freeman. He was blessed with a royal pedigree and he was blessed with a great trainer, James Payne.' Valley Equine, Stephenville TX.

LTE $367,101. 2018 NCHA Open Futurity 3YO Reserve Champion. Confirmed show record: 2018 NCHA Open Futurity Reserve Champion; 2019 Idaho CHA 4YO Open Champion; 2019 West Texas Futurity 4YO Open Champion; 2020 West Texas Futurity Open Classic Champion; 2020 NCHA Summer Spectacular Open Classic 5th. In-foal partners and Gold Buckle on BBR: 'This young stallion exudes sheer athleticism, natural intent on a cow and the force to run and stop.
