Full Profile

Phil N Dangerous 'Phil' -- 2019 Sorrel Stallion. By Hottish (Spots Hot x Stylish Play Lena/Docs Stylish Oak -- PE $23.8M+, 2025 QData #3 and 2024 NCHA #4 Leading Cutting Sire -- full profile bvss005) out of Lil Maddy Rey (Dual Smart Rey x -- LTE $240,000+ confirmed Valley Equine 2026; producer of $680,000+ in earning offspring confirmed Valley Equine 2026; Dual Smart Rey sire: LTE $330,436, 2006 NCHA Super Stakes 4YO Open Champion, PE $10M+; Phil N Dangerous was the first money-winning performer in EquiStat for Lil Maddy Rey -- confirmed QHN December 2022). Owned by Misty Greeson. Shown by Lindy Thorn.

Stud fee $5,125 (confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup). Foals eligible: Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive, NCHA Super Stakes, PCCHA, Breeders Invitational, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Semen availability: GREY designation on Valley Equine Breeding Calendar.

LTE $575,979 (Valley Equine 2026 confirmed). 2022 NCHA Open Futurity Champion -- scored 228 with Lindy Thorn on December 10, 2022, at Will Rogers Coliseum in Fort Worth TX; won $300,061 plus incentives from a field of over 500 horses. The win made Lindy Thorn the first woman to win the NCHA Futurity Open Championship since Kathy Daughn in 2000 -- a 22-year milestone confirmed QHN and NCHA. Only the third woman to win the NCHA Futurity Open ever (Lindy Burch in 1981, Kathy Daughn in 1985 and 2000).

Thorn: 'When I saw the score, I thought my heart was gonna fall out of my chest.' Phil N Dangerous is the second NCHA Open Futurity Champion sired by Hottish, who also produced 2017 NCHA Open Futurity Champion Dual Reyish -- QHN: 'the first of a 1-2 punch for EquiStat Elite $10 Million Sire Hottish, who also sired Open Reserve Champion Kittenish' in the same 2022 finals. Thorn on Phil N Dangerous: 'He's super sensitive. He's super careful. He's very willing, he doesn't like to get in trouble -- he's just like his mother.' 2025 NCHA Open Classic/Challenge Co-Champion -- confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup.

Multiple aged-event finals appearances across his 5 and 6YO seasons. Valley Equine 2026: 'Phil N Dangerous proved himself early and often against the best competition of his generation. He captured the NCHA Open Futurity Championship, backed it up with multiple aged-event finals, and added a Co-Champion title in the 2025 NCHA Open Classic/Challenge. Along the way he was a finalist in multiple premier events.' Hottish sire page confirms Phil N Dangerous among Hottish's named top offspring alongside Kittennish, Dual Reyish, Hottish Metal, Cool N Hot, Hott Rod, and Blackish.
