Lil Catbaloo -- 2009 Sorrel Stallion. AQHA. 14.1H. 5-panel N/N.

HERDA (N/HRD). In Memoriam. By High Brow Cat (High Brow Hickory x Smart Little Kitty -- NCHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire; PE $97M+; eight NCHA Open Futurity Champions; #1 AQHA/NCHA Leading Sire 2009-2022; #1 Equi-Stat All-Time Leading Cutting Sire 2009-2022; NRCHA All-Time Leading Sire; LTE $110,784; his sons include Metallic Cat bvss001/add004 in this database, the $83M+ cutting sire; High Brow Cat's influence defines the modern cutting and cow horse world more completely than any other stallion in history) out of Sweet Lil Boo (Peptoboonsmal x -- Peptoboonsmal is NCHA Hall of Fame, NCHA Futurity Open Champion, PE $26M+; the Peptoboonsmal foundation is the most dominant cow sense bloodline in the NCHA cutting world; Sweet Lil Boo produced Lil Catbaloo as her standout offspring combining the two most dominant NCHA sire lines of the modern era -- High Brow Cat and Peptoboonsmal -- in a single individual). ICSI only.

Stud fee $1,500 ICSI. Contact Oswood Stallion Station at (817) 599-4560. Subscribed to NRCHA Stakes, NCHA Super Stakes, Breeders Invitational, PCCHA Stakes, The Bonanza Cutting. Source: https://oswoodstallionstation.com/lilcatbaloo/ LTE $238,871 confirmed NCHA and NRCHA.

2018 Leading NCHA Freshman Sire confirmed -- his first foal crop to perform in the show pen led all freshman sires in NCHA earnings for the 2018 season, a credential that confirms his genetic transmission is elite rather than a reflection of his own arena credentials alone. Confirmed career highlights: 2012 NCHA Open Futurity Finalist 4th (#2 Top Horse 4-Year-Old Open, #4 Top Open Horse All Ages, #6 Top Horse All Divisions); 2013 Breeders Invitational Open Derby Champion; 2013 Arbuckle Mountain 4-Year-Old Open Futurity Reserve Champion; 2013 Bonanza Open Derby Finalist 6th; 2013 West Texas Open Derby Finalist; 2013 Abilene Spectacular Open Derby Finalist; Sire & Dam Incentive Winner at 2013 Breeders Invitational Derby. The High Brow Cat x Peptoboonsmal dam cross is the most commercially significant breeding combination in NCHA cutting history. High Brow Cat ($97M+ PE) is the #1 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire; Peptoboonsmal ($26M+ PE) is one of the top five all-time NCHA cutting sires.

Lil Catbaloo places both sire lines within a single individual, creating offspring with the maximum concentration of cutting horse genetics available in any single breeding combination. His 4th place finish at the 2012 NCHA Open Futurity confirms he competed at the elite open level in the world's most competitive cutting horse event, and his 2013 Breeders Invitational Open Derby Championship adds a premier aged-event title that confirms his talent extended beyond the futurity year. The 2018 Leading NCHA Freshman Sire credential arrived before his death confirmed that Lil Catbaloo was transmitting his elite genetics to the next generation at a level that led all freshmen sires in NCHA earnings. Breeders crossing Metallic Cat daughters or Dual Rey daughters on Lil Catbaloo ICSI access the High Brow Cat x Peptoboonsmal foundation at a $1,500 ICSI price point that is among the most accessible entry points to this level of cutting horse pedigree available anywhere in the industry today.
