Performance Summary

Jesses Topaz -- 2011 Buckskin Stallion. 15.1H, 1227 lbs. AQHA 5374475. By Mr Jess Perry (SI 113 -- LTE $687,184; Texas Classic Futurity G1 National Track Record; Texas Classic Derby G1; AQHA Hall of Fame 2019; PE $53,900,000+ confirmed AQHA; one of only three stallions in history to sire earners of more than $50 million alongside First Down Dash and Corona Cartel confirmed AQHA; all-time 3rd leading broodmare sire of money earners; stood at 6666 Ranch from 1999 until euthanized November 15, 2017 due to complications of age and laminitis; buried on 6666 Ranch beside Special Effort and Ocean Runaway; 1,399 starters, 882 winners, 143 stakes winners) out of Paddys Topaz (Paddys Irish Whiskey x -- Paddys Irish Whiskey is QData Top 20 All-Time Leading Ranch Riding Sire confirmed 6666 Ranch PDF; NRCHA All-Time Leading Sire with $1,600,000+ in offspring confirmed 6666 Ranch Paddys Irish Whiskey page; sire of Smart Whiskey Doc 214 AQHA points and 2x AQHA World Champion Versatility Ranch Horse; female family connects to Frenchmans Guy, French Flash Hawk and PC Frenchmans Hayday barrel racing line confirmed QStallions). Owned by Burnett Ranches LLC / 6666 Ranch. 6666 Ranch, Guthrie TX. Stud fee $2,500 plus chute fee confirmed 6666stallions.com 2026. Jesses Topaz represents a unique pedigree cross within the 6666 Ranch stallion program: the combination of Mr Jess Perry's elite racing genetics (SI 113, $53.9M+ PE) with Paddys Irish Whiskey's proven cow-sense and ranch horse production creates a speed-plus-athleticism cross well-suited for barrel racing and versatile performance horse programs. Both Mr Jess Perry and Paddys Irish Whiskey stood at the 6666 Ranch -- this stallion carries the legacy of two 6666 Ranch foundation stallions on both sides of his pedigree, making him a uniquely authentic expression of the Four Sixes breeding program's historic depth.
