Reyzin The Limit -- 2021 Red Roan Stallion. By Reyzin The Cash (Dual Rey x Spookys Cash/Miss N Cash -- LTE $278,000+ confirmed 6666 Ranch; 2015 NCHA Open Derby Champion; 2016 NCHA Open Classic/Challenge Champion; PE $11,000,000+ confirmed 6666 Ranch press release: 'offspring have earned more than $11 million, excelling across cutting, reined cow horse, roping, ranch versatility, and even barrel racing'; now also standing at 6666 Ranch for 2026 -- a rare father-son pair at the same station; Dual Rey bvss001 grand-sire connection) out of Metallistic (Metallic Cat x -- Metallic Cat is QData #1 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire PE $83.6M+ -- full profile add001; Metallistic dam produce not further confirmed in available sources). Owned by Katie and Cookie Banuelos. Ridden by Adan Banuelos ($6M+ NCHA earnings, multi-time NCHA Open Horse of the Year rider).

6666 Ranch, Guthrie TX. NCHA Hall of Fame inductee -- confirmed 6666stallions.com sidebar: 'Reyzin The Limit Inducted into the National Cutting Horse Association Hall of Fame.' Highest earning offspring of Reyzin The Cash confirmed 6666 Ranch press release. Confirmed show record: split 5th 2024 NCHA Open Futurity confirmed Valley Equine Reyzin The Cash black type PDF ($400,109 at time of PDF); 2025 NCHA Super Stakes Open Derby Reserve Champion confirmed Valley Equine black type and 6666 Ranch press release; 2025 Cattlemens Derby Reserve Champion confirmed Valley Equine black type; West Texas Futurity Open Derby Champion confirmed 6666 Ranch press release; 3rd 2025 American Performance Horseman confirmed 6666 Ranch press release; 3rd 2025 Run For A Million confirmed 6666 Ranch press release. Reyzin The Limit and his sire Reyzin The Cash both standing at 6666 Ranch for 2026 -- a father-son pair at the same station, mirroring the Dual Pep (bvss022) and Dual Rey (bvss001) father-son relationship at Valley Equine. At only 4-5 years old in his show career, Reyzin The Limit's $512K+ LTE and NCHA Hall of Fame status represent one of the most compelling young stallion debuts in recent memory -- the combination of Reyzin The Cash's proven sire record ($11M+ in offspring) with Metallic Cat dam genetics through Metallistic creates a pedigree with elite cow-sense concentration on both sides.
