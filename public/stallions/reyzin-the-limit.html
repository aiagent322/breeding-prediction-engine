At only 4-5 years old in his show career, Reyzin The Limit's $512K+ LTE and NCHA Hall of Fame status represent one of the most compelling young stallion debuts in recent memory -- the combination of Reyzin The Cash's proven sire record ($11M+ in offspring) with Metallic Cat dam genetics through Metallistic creates a pedigree with elite cow-sense concentration on both sides.
