Full Profile

Metallic Rebel -- 2012 Red Roan Stallion. By Metallic Cat (QData/NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, NRCHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire, PE $83.6M+, 2008 NCHA Futurity Champion, 2009 NCHA HoY, NCHA HoF -- full profile bvss002) out of Sweet Abra (Abrakadabracre x The Better Choice/SR Instant Choice -- LTE $120,151; Battle In The Saddle World Series Open Champion; NCHA Super Stakes Open Classic finalist; AQHA World Amateur Cutting 3rd; Breeders Invitational Amateur Derby 4th; AQHA ROM 18.5 points; dam produce $1,048,799+ including Metallic Rebel $438K, Magistic Moon $177,729, Sweet Metallic $157,607, Magic Metallic $139,204, Magic Metalman $57,375, Abra Cat $31,554). Abrakadabracre (Sweet Abra's sire): notable daughter My Little Abra ($64,220) produced Ichis My Choice -- LTE $402,186, 2015 NCHA Horse of the Year, 2016 Breeders Invitational Open Derby Champion, 2016 NCHA Open Super Stakes Reserve Champion -- confirming the Abrakadabracre maternal line's elite production capacity. Bred by Fults Ranch Ltd, Amarillo TX.

Owned by Thomas and Lisa Guinn, Philadelphia MS. 13 Championship titles and 3 Reserve Championships in show career per metallicrebel.com. Stud fee $10,000. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX.

LTE $438,315. 2017 NCHA Open Horse of the Year. NCHA World Champion Stallion. NCHA Hall of Fame inductee.

Confirmed show titles include 2016 PCCHA Open Derby Champion. Total career record: 13 Championships and 3 Reserve Championships. metallicrebel.com: 'He has an amazing mind and is so cowy.' First son of Metallic Cat to reach EquiStat Elite $1 Million Sire status -- hit the milestone in April of his offspring's 4-year-old year, one month faster than his own sire Metallic Cat achieved it. First crop: 162 AQHA-registered foals born 2018.

2022 NCHA Leading Freshman Sire. PE $16,638,605 (Valley Equine 2026 -- most current confirmed figure). 2024 QData and NCHA #3 Leading Sire. 2024 NRCHA #4 Leading Sire.

QData #2 Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire (confirmed Valley Equine Armour announcement Dec 2025). Average $44,000 per performer. QHN 5-Year Stats (2019-2024): 222 money earners, $6,540,069. Top confirmed offspring: Lone Woolf (x A Little Reylena/Dual Rey, LTE $668,310, 2023 NCHA Open Futurity Champion, NCHA Reserve Horse of the Year, NCHA Hall of Fame -- confirmed highest-earning Metallic Rebel offspring); Armour (x Coureygous/Dual Rey, LTE $379,775, Breeders Invitational Open Derby Champion, standing Valley Equine 2026); Third Edge (x Lil Rattler/Dual Rey, $342,253, 2022 NCHA Super Stakes Open Co-Champion scored 229); Reybels Cause (x Twice As Reycy/Dual Rey, 2022 NCHA Super Stakes Open Co-Champion scored 229, $220,659); A Reyl Rebel (x Never Reylinquish, $185,981 NRCHA, 2024 NRCHA Western Derby Open Champion -- defended title back-to-back with Nick Dowers); Spanish Nights (NRCHA Stallion Stakes Open Champion with Veronica Swales -- confirmed metallicrebel.com/news); Poccahontas (x Daisy Poochanan/Smooth As A Cat, 2025 NCHA Reserve Amateur Horse of the Year, 2024 NCHA Amateur Futurity Champion and Intermediate Amateur Futurity Champion, owned/shown by Marcos Chacron-Arocha); Midnight In Harlem (2026 NCHA Super Stakes Non-Pro Champion, scored 219 with Ashley Galyean for Beau and Ashley Galyean, $8,453 -- confirmed Valley Equine April 2026); Phantomm (2026 NCHA Super Stakes Amateur Reserve Champion, scored 218.5 with German Lugo, $8,695).

Valley Equine Armour release: 'Metallic Rebel is currently the QData #2 Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire. His progeny earnings already exceed $15 million.' The Metallic Rebel cross on Dual Rey daughters has proven exceptionally productive -- Third Edge, Reybels Cause, Lone Woolf, Reybels Cause all carry Dual Rey on the dam side.
