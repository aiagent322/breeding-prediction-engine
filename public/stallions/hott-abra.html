Performance Summary

Hott Abra -- 2021 Bay Stallion. By Hottish (Spots Hot x Stylish Play Lena/Docs Stylish Oak -- PE $23.8M+, died February 2020, 2025 QData #3 and 2024 NCHA #4 Leading Sire -- full profile bvss005) out of Metallilac (Metallic Cat x Sweet Abra/Abrakadabracre -- Metallilac LTE $14,577; producer of 5 money-earners confirmed Valley Equine announcement December 2025; a full sister to Metallic Rebel $438,315 NCHA Open Horse of the Year 2017, PE $16.6M+, bvss004 -- both by Metallic Cat out of Sweet Abra). Sweet Abra (Metallilac's dam by Abrakadabracre): LTE $120,151; NCHA Super Stakes Open Classic finalist; Battle In The Saddle World Series Open Champion; 3rd AQHA World Amateur Cutting; 2022 NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Dam; 24 money-earners producing $2,448,352+ including Metallic Rebel $438,315 NCHA HoY, Pipe Bombs Dream $289,461 [2022 Breeders Invitational Open Cutting Classic Champion, 3rd 2022 NCHA Open Classic/Challenge], Warlock $127,615, The Dewd $124,866 [Southern Open Derby Champion], Magistic Moon $187,799 [Arbuckle Mtn 4YO Open Futurity Co-Champion], Magic Metallic $164,052, Sweet Metallic $157,607, Metabra $123,630, Maggic Mike $109,029, Broken Haloes $94,856; Sweet Abra was NCHA's leading dam in 2016, 2017, and 2019. Abrakadabracre (Sweet Abra's sire): LTE $112,566; AQHA World Champion Junior Cutting Horse; NCHA Limited Open Futurity Champion; Equi-Stat Elite $1 Million Sire. Metallilac is also the dam of Laser (by Hottish, 2020 gelding LTE $58,359, in training with Kolby Moore) and Lets Rollz (by Rollz Royce, 2020 stallion) -- both Hottish and Rollz Royce sons, confirming this family's proven compatibility with premier cutting stallions. 7 Panel N/N. Owned by Wendell and Wes Reeder, War Ranch, Deport TX. Shown by Colt Moore, continuing to show through 2026. Stud fee $4,500. Joined Valley Equine for 2026 breeding season announced December 30, 2025. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $84,347. 2025 Brazos Bash Open Derby Champion. 2025 Southern Cutting Open Derby Co-Champion. Non-Pro Cutting Novice Horse Derby Champion. 2nd The Outback Cutting Day One Open Derby. 4th Non-Pro Cutting Open Derby. Finalist in multiple premier events per Valley Equine 2026 lineup. Continuing to show through 2026 breeding season due to active show schedule. Foals first eligible starting at the 2026 NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity. Own incentive program: Hott Abra Incentive returns money to foals competing at the NCHA Futurity in Open, Non-Pro, and Amateur divisions. Also eligible for Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive and NCHA Super Stakes. Andrew Waters, Stallion Manager, Valley Equine: 'Hott Abra checks the boxes. He has already proven himself in the Open, he comes from a deep and reliable female line, and he has the mind and consistency breeders should look for when planning for the future. We are extremely excited to welcome him to Valley Equine.' The combination of Hottish (Spots Hot / Doc O'Lena lineage) on the top and Metallic Cat / Sweet Abra depth on the bottom creates a cross of extraordinary genetic concentration. Metallilac is a full sister to Metallic Rebel, one of the top-producing sires in the industry -- breeders who cross Hott Abra on Metallic Cat or High Brow Cat mares are stacking proven elite cutting genetics from both sides of the cross.
