Performance Summary

Countin Hot Checks -- 2016 Sorrel Stallion. By Hottish (Spots Hot x Stylish Play Lena/Docs Stylish Oak -- PE $23.8M+, died February 2020, 2025 QData #3 Leading Sire -- full profile bvss005) out of Cat Countin Checks (Im Countin Checks x A Cat Above/High Brow Cat -- LTE $169,104; 2014 NCHA Super Stakes Classic/Challenge Limited Non-Pro Co-Champion; shown by Jamie Hill/Jamie Goertz-Hill, Bastrop TX; producer of $359,000+ per Valley Equine 2026; Countin Hot Checks was her first money-earner per 2020 NCHA Brazos Bash article). Im Countin Checks (Cat Countin Checks' sire, by Smart Lil Ricochet x Autumn Boon/Dual Pep): LTE $514,757; NCHA Hall of Fame; Top 15 NCHA Sire 6 consecutive years; offspring earnings $5.6M+; 2005 NCHA Futurity Open 3rd; 2006 Breeders Invitational Derby Open Reserve Champion; 2008 NCHA Horse of the Year runner-up; sire of 2017 NCHA Open World Champion and 2019 NCHA Non-Pro Horse of the Year. Autumn Boon (Im Countin Checks' dam by Dual Pep): LTE $259,685; NCHA Super Stakes Champion; Augusta Futurity Champion; produce $1.9M+, grandget $13M+. QHN In The Blood on Catolena Cashin In confirms Countin Hot Checks carries a 4x4x4 linebreeding to Dual Pep -- through Hottish's sire side (Spots Hot by Chula Dual, a Dual Pep son) and through Cat Countin Checks' sire Im Countin Checks (out of Autumn Boon, a direct Dual Pep daughter), creating one of the densest Dual Pep pedigree concentrations among current young stallions. Bred and owned by Jamie Hill, Bastrop TX. Stud fee $6,500. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. LTE $296,780 (Valley Equine 2026). Valley Equine 2026: 'Countin Hot Checks built his reputation through success at the highest levels of Open aged-event competition, culminating in a World Championship and a Classic/Challenge title.' Confirmed titles from Valley Equine stallion page: NCHA Open Classic/Challenge Champion; Bonanza Open Classic Champion; 2020 Brazos Bash Open Derby Co-Champion (tied 228 points with Dual Clay under Grant Setnicka, ridden by Jaime Snider -- confirmed SallyHarrison.com September 2020; Sanctus with Rodrigo Taboga placed 3rd at 225.5); Abilene Spectacular Open Classic Reserve Champion. Additional: West Texas Derby Open Intermediate Champion; Arbuckle Mountain Open Novice Champion. Stallion Register: 'With athleticism, cow, and heart, Countin Hot Checks is showing success in the major aged events and there is no doubt he will have a future as a successful sire.' Valley Equine 2026: 'Sired by Hottish and out of Cat Countin Checks, a proven earner and producer of over $359,000, he represents a cross of two strong cutting families.' First foals showing in 2025, earning in both cutting and cow horse. Top confirmed offspring at the 2026 NCHA Super Stakes (Valley Equine April 2026): SCR Ima Hottish Pep by Countin Hot Checks and Jasper Patrick topped the Non-Pro Derby field with a 222 for owner Lloyd Ested, earning $8,106.30; a separate 4-year-old by Countin Hot Checks won the 4-Year-Old Non-Pro Derby, described by Valley Equine as 'separated themselves with the highest score of the final and gave Countin Hot Checks a Super Stakes Derby champion.' Two champions in the same Super Stakes event from a first foal crop is a significant debut statement for the stallion's sire career. The Hottish x Im Countin Checks cross creates a complementary pairing of cow horse athleticism (Hottish line) with cutting-specific foot speed and trainability (Im Countin Checks line), both underpinned by the dense Dual Pep concentration that has consistently produced elite performers in the NCHA.
