Third dam Lenas Lucinda (Doc O'Lena): LTE $161,760, 4th NCHA Open Futurity, dam of 15 money-earners earning $901,963 including SPL Altisimo ($192K — granddam of NCHA HoY LHR Smooth Jamie May $259K). LTE $436,150. NCHA Hall of Fame (inducted 2023). 2X Reyzin The Cash Incentive Champion. Trained and shown by Tarin Rice (EquiStat Elite $3M Rider). Owner/Breeder: Joel Colgrove Sr, Boligee, Alabama. Critical pedigree note: carries confirmed 2x3 linebreeding pattern to Dual Pep — same pattern behind NCHA champions Twice In Santiago (2020 HoY), Dual Reyish (2017 Futurity Champion), CR Gotcha Covered (2019 Open World Champion), and Countin Hot Checks. Show record: 2020 NCHA Open Futurity Finalist (scored 224 on opening day — high score that day); 2021 NCHA Super Stakes 4-Year-Old Open Champion (scored 230 — second highest score in Super Stakes history, behind only Sunettes Dually 231 in 2001); 2021 The Ike Open Derby Champion; 2021 West Texas Open Derby Champion; 2021 The Non-Pro Open Derby Champion; 2021 Arbuckle Mountain Open Derby Reserve Champion; 2021 Brazos Bash Open Derby 3rd; 2021 Abilene Spectacular Open Derby Finalist; 2022 Brazos Bash Open Classic Champion (retirement title); 2022 PCCHA 5/6 YO Open Reserve Champion; 2022 PCCHA Fall Open Classic/Challenge Reserve Champion; 2022 PCCHA Open Classic/Challenge Co-Reserve Champion. Retired after 2022 Brazos Bash Classic Championship. Described: "unparalleled strength, confidence and unique moves put him in a class of his own" and "unique way of moving, superior strength, and athleticism." Tarin Rice described first offspring as horses he was "more than excited to be riding." First foal crop born 2023 — 2-year-olds in 2025. No offspring championship results yet. Stud fee $4,500.
