Performance Summary

Eddie Stinson -- 2003 Sorrel Stallion. By Dash Ta Fame (First Down Dash x -- dominant barrel racing sire dynasty; leading paternal and maternal grandsire of all barrel horses per published statistics) out of Casino Lights (Sixarun x Ease On Down/Easy Jet -- graded stakes winner on track; Sixarun is multiple stakes winner and sire of leading barrel horse and racehorse sire Tres Seis). Owned by Cody and Luann Johnson, Twisted J Ranch, Dublin TX. Stud fee $6,000 confirmed Future Fortunes and Ruby Buckle. Foals eligible: Ruby Buckle, Breeders Challenge, Royal Crown, Future Fortunes, Select Stallion Stakes, Triple Crown 100, Blue Collar Breeders, Colorado Classic, TQHA, Tomorrow's Legends, Top Shelf Breeders, Legends of The South, VGBRA, Diamond Classic. NOT part of the cutting Super Stallion Incentive program. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Primary discipline: barrel racing and speed events exclusively. Race LTE $200,016+ confirmed in-foal partners. Speed Index 111 -- the fastest son of Dash Ta Fame confirmed Stallion Register Directory, Future Fortunes, in-foal partners, multiple sources. Luann Johnson (owner): 'As the fastest son of Dash Ta Fame with a 111 speed index, you would expect him to bring more of a hot, race horse temperament in his offspring, but he is the exact opposite. Out of all the Dash Ta Fame sons that stand and send progeny to the barrel pen, I believe he sends the best disposition and mind of them all. His colts are all super smart, athletic, very willing and trainable and wicked fast. He crosses with absolutely everything. There are offspring currently winning out of mares with straight running bloodlines like Ivory James and Corona Cartel as well as mares from foundation working bloodlines.' PE $9,940,964 confirmed Stallion Register Directory. Listed on QData Leading Barrel Sire lists for 9 consecutive years 2013-2021 -- confirmed in-foal partners. 2020 and 2021 QData #4 Leading Sire of Barrel and Speed Event Money-Earners. Sire of NFR qualifiers confirmed 2022 NFR pedigree article. Top confirmed offspring per Future Fortunes: CFour Tibbie Stinson LTE $371,400 -- 2016 AQHA/WPRA/PRCA Barrel Horse of the Year, 2017 NFR Qualifier, 1st The American Rodeo Semi Finals, 3-Time Futurity Champion; BT Buddy Stinson LTE $118,637 -- Multiple 1D, Derby and Super States Champion; Foxie Stinson LTE $68,682 -- Multiple 1D/2D Champion and American Rodeo Qualifier; Eddies Hotmoon Bug LTE $67,246 -- 4-Time Futurity Slot Race Finalist; VF Famous Duck LTE $60,410 -- 2017 NFR Qualifier; Dirty Dan Stinson -- 2017 APHA World Champion Sweepstakes. Eddie Stinson is one of Dash Ta Fame's leading siring sons in the barrel horse world with cross-discipline versatility noted across running bloodlines and foundation working lines.
