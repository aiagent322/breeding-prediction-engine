LTE $326,900 confirmed. ZAK 34's 2021 NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity Open Championship is the premier title in reined cow horse competition -- the equivalent of the NCHA Futurity for the cow horse world, awarded annually to the best 3-year-old reined cow horse in open competition. This is the title that elevated Woody Be Tuff to NRCHA Million Dollar Sire status, as ZAK 34's championship earnings were the milestone that pushed Woody Be Tuff over $1,000,000 in NRCHA offspring earnings confirmed NRCHA.com. ZAK 34 therefore carries singular historical significance in the Woody Be Tuff sire legacy -- he is the horse that made Woody Be Tuff an NRCHA Million Dollar Sire. His 2025 NRCHA Western Derby Two-Rein Spectacular Open Reserve Championship demonstrates continued elite competitiveness well into his aged-horse career, confirming that his Snaffle Bit Futurity Championship was not a peak result but rather the beginning of a sustained career at the top of the sport. The Woody Be Tuff x High Brow Cat cross is one of the industry's most celebrated proven combinations -- Solo Select Horses identifies daughters of High Brow Cat as one of Woody Be Tuff's top recommended crosses, and ZAK 34 is the most decorated product of that exact combination. He is a half-sibling to CR Tuff Pep (f6r003 at 6666 Ranch in this database) through their shared sire Woody Be Tuff, representing the Woody Be Tuff line across two different stallion stations in this database.
