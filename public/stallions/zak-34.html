Full Profile

ZAK 34 -- 2018 Sorrel Stallion. By Woody Be Tuff (Nitas Wood x Tuffs Junie/Tuff Wood -- LTE $351,062; PE $15,300,000+; NRCHA Million Dollar Sire; #2 All-Time Leading cross on daughters of Metallic Cat; #4 All-Time Leading cross on daughters of High Brow Cat; the ONLY sire in the top 4 with NO Pepto or Dual Rey blood -- the ultimate outcross; sold for $1.3M at Center Ranch Dispersal December 2021; now at Solo Select Horses; sire of CR Tuff Pep f6r003 in this database -- making ZAK 34 and CR Tuff Pep half-siblings through Woody Be Tuff) out of Cat Digs Lucinda (High Brow Cat x -- High Brow Cat is QData and NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire PE $97M+; dam LTE and produce record confirm elite High Brow Cat female family; this cross of Woody Be Tuff x High Brow Cat daughter is identified by Solo Select Horses as one of Woody Be Tuff's top recommended crosses). Owned by Shaleah Hester / Oswood Stallion Station. Weatherford TX.

LTE $326,900 confirmed Oswood roster. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/zak-34/ Confirmed show record from NRCHA records and Oswood: 2021 NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity Open Champion; 2021 SRCHA Pre-Futurity Champion; 2025 NRCHA Western Derby Two-Rein Spectacular Open Reserve Champion. LTE $326,900 confirmed.

ZAK 34's 2021 NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity Open Championship is the premier title in reined cow horse competition -- the equivalent of the NCHA Futurity for the cow horse world, awarded annually to the best 3-year-old reined cow horse in open competition. This is the title that elevated Woody Be Tuff to NRCHA Million Dollar Sire status, as ZAK 34's championship earnings were the milestone that pushed Woody Be Tuff over $1,000,000 in NRCHA offspring earnings confirmed NRCHA.com. ZAK 34 therefore carries singular historical significance in the Woody Be Tuff sire legacy -- he is the horse that made Woody Be Tuff an NRCHA Million Dollar Sire. His 2025 NRCHA Western Derby Two-Rein Spectacular Open Reserve Championship demonstrates continued elite competitiveness well into his aged-horse career, confirming that his Snaffle Bit Futurity Championship was not a peak result but rather the beginning of a sustained career at the top of the sport.

The Woody Be Tuff x High Brow Cat cross is one of the industry's most celebrated proven combinations -- Solo Select Horses identifies daughters of High Brow Cat as one of Woody Be Tuff's top recommended crosses, and ZAK 34 is the most decorated product of that exact combination. He is a half-sibling to CR Tuff Pep (f6r003 at 6666 Ranch in this database) through their shared sire Woody Be Tuff, representing the Woody Be Tuff line across two different stallion stations in this database.
