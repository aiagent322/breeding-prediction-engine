Full Profile

The Midnight Train -- AQHA Stallion. 14.1H. By Badboonarising (Once In A Blu Boon x Show Biz Kitty -- NCHA LTE $367,101; 2024 NCHA Freshman Sire of the Year; 2025 QData #7 Leading Cutting Sire with only two foal crops performing; 15H 1024 lbs; 6-panel N/N; his progeny dominated NCHA, NRCHA, and roping events in their first two seasons confirming the broadest multi-discipline utility of any young breeding stallion in the current market) out of Metallic Train (Metallic Cat x Sparking Train by Shining Spark -- 2018 NRCHA Stallion Stakes Open Champion; NRCHA earnings $172,250+; Metallic Cat's leading NRCHA money-earner; Sparking Train by Shining Spark confirms the Metallic Cat x Shining Spark cross that also produced Bet He Sparks osw006 in this database; Metallic Train's NRCHA Stallion Stakes Open Championship is the most prestigious single-event NRCHA title). Owned by Beverly Servi, Durango CO.

Oswood Stallion Station, 1400 Old Garner Road, Weatherford TX 76088. (817) 599-4560. Frozen semen only. ICSI available.

2026 Stud fee: $2,000 + $675 chute fee. Subscribed to Oswood Breeder Bonus, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive, NRHA Sire & Dam. Source: https://oswoodstallionstation.com/the-midnight-train/ LTE $133,837 confirmed NRCHA. 2024 NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity Open Finalist confirmed -- shown by Clayton Edsall for owner Beverly Servi; scored 214.5 in the herd work during the Open Finals.

The NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity is the premier NRCHA annual event held in Fort Worth, bringing together hundreds of the best young reined cow horses from the North American professional community. Qualifying for and competing in the Open Finals places The Midnight Train among the 15-20 best horses in the most competitive division of the most important NRCHA event of the year -- a credential that confirms elite-level talent at age three or four. The Badboonarising x Metallic Train cross is a pedigree of exceptional commercial depth. Badboonarising (2024 NCHA Freshman Sire, 2025 #7 QData Leading Cutting Sire) provides Once In A Blu Boon foundation genetics that have produced NCHA, NRCHA, and multi-discipline performers in his very first foal crop.

Metallic Train brings the Metallic Cat x Shining Spark cross through Sparking Train -- the same breeding that produced NRCHA champion Bet He Sparks (osw006 in this database), confirming that Oswood has seen this specific pedigree combination produce elite NRCHA performers before. Two generations of NRCHA championship genetics on both sides of this pedigree -- Badboonarising's explosive first-crop dominance and Metallic Train's NRCHA Stallion Stakes Open Championship -- create offspring bred to compete at the elite NRCHA open level from their first season. Breeders crossing Metallic Cat daughters or Dual Rey daughters on The Midnight Train access the Badboonarising x Metallic Cat second-generation combination at a $2,000 stud fee -- an accessible entry point to Beverly Servi's breeding program and a stallion whose NRCHA SBF Open Finalist credentials confirm competitive viability at the sport's highest annual event.
