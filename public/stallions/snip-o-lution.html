By Einsteins Revolution (Whizkey N Diamonds x -- LTE confirmed; leading NRHA sire with PE $5M+; Einsteins Revolution is an NRHA Million Dollar Sire whose offspring have excelled across reining, ranch horse, and versatility ranch horse disciplines at the highest competitive levels; standing at Oswood Stallion Station -- full profile ss018) out of Snip O Chex (Nu Chex To Cash x -- Nu Chex To Cash is a legendary NRHA sire and AQHA Hall of Fame inductee whose offspring have earned $30M+ across reining, cutting, and versatility disciplines; dam line contributes elite Nu Chex To Cash athleticism and consistency to the pedigree). Owned by Shaleah Hester. Oswood Stallion Station, Weatherford TX. LTE $109,050 confirmed Oswood roster. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Sire earnings $426,556+ confirmed Oswood roster. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/snip-o-lution/ Confirmed show record from Oswood records and NRHA results: 2018 Non-Pro World Champion; 2018 Equita Lyon Non-Pro Champion; 2016 NRHA Derby L4 Open Finalist; 2014 SWRHA Futurity Open Champion. Snip O Lution's 2018 NRHA Non-Pro World Championship is the defining achievement of his competitive career -- the title awarded annually to the top non-professional reining horse in the world across all NRHA World Show non-pro classes. This title carries exceptional significance for a breeding stallion because it demonstrates that Snip O Lution possesses the rideability, trainability, and consistency required to excel under an amateur rider at the world championship level -- qualities that translate directly into commercial breeding value, as the majority of performance horse buyers are non-professionals seeking horses with exactly these characteristics. His 2018 Equita Lyon Non-Pro Championship in France confirms international-level appeal across different competitive environments. The Einsteins Revolution x Nu Chex To Cash cross pairs two foundational NRHA sire lines -- Einsteins Revolution's Whizkey N Diamonds athleticism and Nu Chex To Cash's proven all-discipline versatility -- creating offspring suited for non-pro, amateur, and open competition at the highest levels. The dam sire Nu Chex To Cash is AQHA Hall of Fame recognized with $30M+ in PE across reining, cutting, and ranch horse, making Snip O Chex's female line a significant contributor to the pedigree's commercial depth. Sire earnings of $426,556+ from early foal crops confirm that Snip O Lution is passing his world championship rideability and trainability to offspring who are beginning to make their mark in competition -- a strong early validation of his genetic potential as a breeding stallion.
