By Dual Rey (QData and NCHA #3 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, AQHA Hall of Fame, PE $57.9M+, 1,642 money-earners at $51M+ per reyzinthecash.com pedigree page -- full profile bvss001) out of Spookys Cash (Miss N Cash -- LTE $284,410; 4th NCHA Open Futurity; NCHA Super Stakes Non-Pro Reserve Champion; Suncoast Fall 5/6YO Open Classic Reserve Champion; PCCHA Winter Roundup Open Derby Champion; split 3rd PCCHA 4-Year-Old Open and Non-Pro Stakes; 17+ money-earners earning $807,175-$910,465+; top produce beyond Reyzin The Cash: Spooky Smooth $100,100, Spookys Catchin Reys $92,759 Calgary Stampede W-Series Open Reserve Champion, Mean Bean $84,600 Cattlemens Non-Pro Classic Champion, Catamini $84,262 Utah CHA Open Futurity Champion). San Starlight (Spookys Cash's dam by Grays Starlight): NCHA Super Stakes finalist; dam of $1.5M+ in produce including Spookys Smarty Pants $258,027 NCHA Futurity Open Reserve Champion. NRCHA Million Dollar Sire. Owned by Iron Rose Ranch. Previously at Brazos Valley Stallion Station / Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Moved to 6666 Ranch, Guthrie TX for 2026 breeding season -- confirmed 6666 Ranch announcement September 8, 2025. 6666 Ranch, Guthrie TX. LTE $287,381. 2015 NCHA Open Derby Champion. 2016 NCHA Open Classic/Challenge Champion. QStallions confirms these as his two premier title wins. QHN (In The Blood on Catolena Cashin In) confirms Reyzin The Cash 'earned $287,381 in his career.' Reyzin The Cash carries a documented 2x3 linebreeding to Dual Pep -- his sire Dual Rey is by Dual Pep, and Spookys Cash has Dual Pep influence through the dam line -- a pattern QHN confirms is shared with NCHA Futurity winners Dual Reyish (4x3), Crey Zee (2x3), CR Gotcha Covered (3x4), Chexy Louella (3x3), and Countin Hot Checks (4x4x4). PE $11M+ (confirmed 6666 Ranch September 2025 announcement). NRCHA Million Dollar Sire. 6666 Ranch: 'His offspring have earned more than $11 million, excelling across cutting, reined cow horse, roping, ranch versatility, and even barrel racing competitions.' Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive: foals eligible through 2026 NCHA Summer Spectacular. Top confirmed offspring from Valley Equine black type PDF: Catolena Cashin In (x Dual Catolena/High Brow Cat, LTE $436,150, NCHA Super Stakes Open Derby Champion, The Ike Open Derby Champion, West Texas Open Derby Champion, Brazos Bash Open Classic Champion, NCHA Hall of Fame, 2x Reyzin The Cash Incentive Champion, standing Valley Equine 2026 at $4,500 stud fee); Reyzin The Limit (x Metallistic/Metallic Cat, LTE $400,109, split 5th 2024 NCHA Open Futurity, 2025 NCHA Super Stakes Open Derby Reserve Champion, 2025 Cattlemens Derby Reserve Champion); Reyckoning (x That Catomine/High Brow Cat, LTE $268,322, NCHA Super Stakes Open Classic Reserve Champion twice, Brazos Bash Open Derby Champion, Breeders Invitational $10,000 Non-Pro Derby Champion); Reyzin Jewels (x My Jewels Missin/Lenas Jewel Bars, LTE $237,436, 4th 2024 NCHA Super Stakes Open Derby, 2025 Augusta Open Classic Champion); Reydneck Woman (x Xquizit Cat/High Brow Cat, LTE $144,446, 3rd NCHA Super Stakes Non-Pro Derby); Cricket Reyzin Cash (listed in top offspring black type). 6666 Ranch: 'Among them is his standout son Reyzin The Limit, who has carried forward his sire's legacy in the show pen.'
