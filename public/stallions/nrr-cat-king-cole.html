NRR Cat King Cole 'Cole' -- 2007 Bay Stallion. By High Brow Cat (QData/NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, PE $97M+, AQHA Hall of Fame 2023 -- full profile bvss002) out of Uno Del Rey (Smart Little Uno/Smart Little Lena lineage -- bred at North Ridge Ranch, Pierre SD; dam produce $730,000+ confirmed Beechfork Ranch 2025; full brothers all High Brow Cat sons: Cats High Noon $146,240 multiple futurity champion, NRR Ollie Tom Cat $117,000 2016 Augusta Futurity Reserve Open Champion and multiple World Top Ten Open and Limited, NRR Al Cat Capone $110,000 multiple World Top Ten; The Cophagen Cat $48,844; Black Cadillac Cat $26,126). Smart Little Uno (Uno Del Rey's sire): son of Smart Little Lena, NCHA Triple Crown winner and #2 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire. 5-Panel N/N, HERDA N/N. Bred and owned by North Ridge Ranch (Greg and Cindy Ward), Pierre SD. Shown by Tracy Barton, NCHA Hall of Fame Rider. Retired to stud 2016 completely sound. NCHA Hall of Fame 2017. Stud fee $4,500. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Foals eligible starting at 2026 NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity. LTE $230,918 -- all earned in Open contests. First horse in history of NCHA to achieve all four simultaneously: Affiliate Open Champion, Area Open Champion, National Open Champion, AND World Open Champion -- confirmed multiple primary sources. 2011-2016 South Dakota Horse Association Open Champion. Confirmed full show record from Issuu black type and primary sources: 2011 Western States LAE 4YO Open finalist; 2011 State of Missouri Cutting Futurity 4YO Open finalist; 2012 Western States LAE 5/6YO Open Champion; 2012 CHAN Futurity 4th (tie) 5/6YO Open; 2012 Utah Futurity 5/6YO Open finalist; 2013 NCHA Western National Championships Open Champion (scored 225 with Tracy Barton in Reno Nevada, presented by 6666 Ranch); 2013 Western States LAE 5/6YO Open Champion; 2013 Iowa Breeders Futurity 5/6YO Open Champion; 2013 CHAN Futurity 5/6YO Open Co-Champion; 2013 Wyoming Futurity 4th 5/6YO Open; 2014 NCHA World Championship Finals Open money earner; 2014 NCHA Reserve World Champion Stallion (scored 232.5 Open run, Will Rogers Arena); 2016 NCHA World Champion Stallion; 2016 NCHA Reserve World Open Champion; 2016 NCHA Western Championships Open finalist. 21 Limited Age Event Open Championships across 10-state area (AZ, CO, ID, IA, MN, MT, NE, SD, UT, WY). Weatherford Equine: 'An extraordinary horse for his consistency, quick-footedness, range of movement, and great disposition.' His YouTube videos went viral with millions of views as testament to his unique showmanship and heart. PE $2,205,165 (Valley Equine 2026 -- most current confirmed). 2 of 24 finalists at 2021 NCHA Open Futurity. 2022 NCHA Australian Open Futurity Champion. 2022 Rope Horse Futurity Heading Champion. 2023 NCHA Amateur Futurity Reserve Champion. Top confirmed offspring: Cats Lightning Rey ($163,376 -- 2019 NCHA Western Championships NP Champion; 2019 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5/6YO NP Champion; 2018 Western States LAE 4YO Open Co-Champion; 2020 The Non-Pro 5/6YO NP Reserve Champion); NRR Coles Scootn Cat ($85,055 -- 2019 Augusta Futurity Int. Amateur Co-Reserve Champion; 2019 All American Congress Futurity 5/6YO UNL AM Reserve Champion). Valley Equine 2026 lineup: 'A true competitor, NRR Cat King Cole made his mark in the Open arena and now does the same in the breeding shed -- siring horses that compete and win at the highest levels, including Futurity finalists and international champions.
