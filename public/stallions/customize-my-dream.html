Full Profile

Customize My Dream -- 2016 Gray Stallion. By Magnum Chic Dream (Magnum Chics x A Little Dream/Impressive -- LTE $74,847; 2007 NRHA Open Futurity Co-Champion; NRHA $10 Million Sire; leading reining sire with multiple Futurity and Derby champion offspring across multiple countries; consistent top-10 NRHA leading sire) out of Gunna Be Custom (Gunnatrashya x -- Gunnatrashya osw001 in this database -- 2009 NRHA Open Futurity Champion; 2010 NRHA Open Derby Champion; NRHA Hall of Fame; sire earnings $18M+; dam's sire is the most decorated stallion at Oswood Stallion Station). Owned by DAG Ventures LLC. Oswood Stallion Station, Weatherford TX.

LTE $482,591 confirmed Oswood roster. Highest earning son of Magnum Chic Dream. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/customize-my-dream/ Confirmed show record from Oswood records and NRHA results: 2025 NRHA L4 Open Derby Champion with 233.5; 2025 Tulsa Ride And Slide Derby Champion; 2025 NRBC L4 Open Derby Finalist 3rd; 2025 The Run For A Million 4th; 2022 NRHA Futurity L4 Open Reserve Champion.

Customize My Dream's 2025 NRHA L4 Open Derby Championship with a 233.5 score is among the highest scores recorded in NRHA Derby competition -- a mark that reflects a run of near-perfect technical execution across all phases of the reining pattern. The 233.5 is particularly notable because it came in the L4 Open Derby, the highest and most competitive division at the NRHA Derby, where the world's best open reining horses compete. His 4th-place finish at The Run For A Million in 2025 -- one of the richest equestrian events in history with $1M+ in prize money -- confirms his standing among the very top reining horses in the world at his competitive peak. The pedigree is exceptional on both sides.

Magnum Chic Dream as sire ($10M+ PE; NRHA $10 Million Sire; 2007 NRHA Open Futurity Co-Champion) provides one of the most proven modern reining sire lines available. Gunna Be Custom's dam sire Gunnatrashya (osw001 -- 2009 NRHA Open Futurity Champion; 2010 NRHA Open Derby Champion; NRHA Hall of Fame; $18M+ sire earnings) creates a pedigree that concentrates two Hall of Fame-level reining sire lines -- Magnum Chic Dream's Magnum Chics foundation and Gunnatrashya's Colonels Smoking Gun/Colonel Freckles lineage -- in a single individual who has now validated both lines with an elite open derby championship. As highest earning son of Magnum Chic Dream, Customize My Dream stands as the proving ground for what that cross can produce at the absolute top of the sport.
