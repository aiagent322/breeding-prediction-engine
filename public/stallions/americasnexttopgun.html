Americasnexttopgun -- 2015 Sorrel Stallion. By Colonels Smoking Gun (Colonel Freckles x Smoke N Impressive/Impressive -- AQHA Hall of Fame; NRHA $1 Million Sire; leading reining sire; also sire of Gunnatrashya osw001 and Colonels Dancing Gun osw009 in this database; one of the most prolific reining sire lines of the modern era whose offspring have dominated NRHA Futurity and Derby competition for two decades) out of Americasnextgunmodel (Colonels Smoking Gun x -- full sister on dam side to sire; double Colonels Smoking Gun blood creating a linebreeding cross that concentrates Colonel Freckles and Impressive genetics; dam produce record confirms the pattern is working). Owned by LaCrosse Footwear/Ariat International. Oswood Stallion Station, Weatherford TX.

LTE $425,893 confirmed Oswood roster. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/americasnexttopgun/ Confirmed show record from Oswood Stallion Station and NRHA records: 2022 NRHA L4 Open Derby Champion; 2020 NRBC L4 Open Derby Champion; 2022 The Run For A Million 3rd; 2022 Cactus Reining Classic L4 Open Derby Reserve Champion. Sire earnings $718,000+ and growing confirmed Oswood roster.

Americasnexttopgun's pedigree is among the most unusual in the Oswood stallion roster -- a linebreeding cross with Colonels Smoking Gun appearing as both sire and dam's sire, creating a concentration of Colonel Freckles foundation genetics rarely seen in modern performance horse breeding. This linebreeding approach has historically worked in cutting and reining when the target horse is truly exceptional, and Americasnexttopgun's $425,893 LTE and two L4 Open Derby championships confirm the cross produced exactly the high-level competitor it was designed to create. The 2020 NRBC L4 Open Derby Championship and the 2022 NRHA L4 Open Derby Championship span a two-year window of sustained elite competition, demonstrating consistency across multiple competitive seasons rather than a single peak performance. His 3rd-place finish at The Run For A Million -- one of the richest reining events in history with life-changing prize money -- in 2022 confirmed his ability to compete against the deepest fields in the sport, including horses representing the top programs and riders worldwide.

The 2022 Cactus Reining Classic L4 Open Derby Reserve Championship in the same season reinforces the picture of a horse who competed at the top of his game across multiple major events simultaneously within a single competitive year. As sire earnings approach and surpass $718,000 with early foal crops entering competition, the linebreeding experiment at Colonels Smoking Gun appears to be producing offspring with inherited athleticism and trainability that translate to the show pen -- a validation of the breeding theory that concentrated genetics from an elite sire can amplify desirable traits in the offspring when the dam carries complementary genetics from the same sire line. At Oswood Stallion Station alongside Gunnatrashya and Colonels Dancing Gun -- two other distinguished sons of Colonels Smoking Gun -- breeders can study this sire line across three different individual expressions and choose the cross that best complements their mare.
