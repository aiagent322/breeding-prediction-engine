Full Profile

Areybe — 2020 Red Roan. By Stevie Rey Von (#1 QData Leading Sire 2025, NCHA Futurity Champion, PE $20M+, NRCHA $3M+ sire — described as: 'they are trainable and people like them, that's why there's such demand') out of Stylish Play Lena (Docs Stylish Oak x Play Lena/Freckles Playboy) — QData #4 All-Time Leading Cutting Dam, EquiStat Elite $2 Million Dam, PE $2M+, grandget $9M+. Stylish Play Lena was inducted into the National Cowgirl Museum Hall of Fame with a life-sized bronze sculpture. Her offspring include Hottish (LTE $301K, PE $23.8M, QData #3 Leading Cutting Sire 2025, NRCHA Million Dollar Sire), Smooth Talkin Style (LTE $305K, 2015 NCHA Open Horse of the Year, PE $2.68M), Pepto Boom ($316K), Copperish ($281K), and Halreycious ($271K).

LTE $160,250. Made the 2023 NCHA Futurity Open Finals — confirmed by QHN alongside Cat, both noted as offspring of Stylish Play Lena in the same Finals field. Valley Equine describes: 'consistent Open-level performance record and one of the strongest female families in the cutting industry' and 'presence and athleticism that make you take notice.' His first foal crops are not yet of showing age as of 2025. Enrolled in NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive and Chrome Cash.

Stud fee $3,500. Owned by Linda Holmes. Standing at Valley Equine, Stephenville TX.
