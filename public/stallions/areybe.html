Full Profile

Areybe — 2020 Red Roan. By Stevie Rey Von (#1 QData Leading Sire 2025, 2015 NCHA Futurity Champion, NCHA Hall of Fame, PE $20M+, NRCHA $3M+ sire, full book with waiting list into 2027 — his offspring consistently described as "incredibly gentle and effortless to handle," "requires minimal riding and training," "so accurate and never takes a foot out of place," "incredibly smart"; Beau Galyean on Stevie: "The reason there's such a demand for the horse is that he's trainable and people like them") out of Stylish Play Lena (Docs Stylish Oak x Play Lena/Freckles Playboy) — QData #4 All-Time Leading Cutting Dam and EquiStat Elite $2 Million Dam. Stylish Play Lena earned $289,624 in her own career: 2001 South Point Futurity 4-Year-Old Open Reserve Champion, 2003 NCHA Summer Spectacular 5&6YO Open Champion, 2003 Abilene Spectacular 5&6YO Open Champion. Her influence on the cutting horse industry is so significant that a life-sized bronze sculpture of Stylish Play Lena stands at the National Cowgirl Museum and Hall of Fame in Fort Worth — an honor shared by only the most exceptional mares in western performance history.

Her produce record defines elite production: Hottish (Spots Hot, LTE $301,843, 2012 NCHA Open Derby Champion, 2012 NCHA Reserve HoY, PE $23.8M+, 2025 QData #4 Leading Cutting Sire — ICSI only as deceased 2020; NRCHA Million Dollar Sire), Smooth Talkin Style (Smooth As A Cat, LTE $305,284, 2015 NCHA Open Horse of the Year, PE $2.68M; $96,698 earnings average per offspring — the highest per-offspring average of any dam at the time of reporting), Pepto Boom (Peptoboonsmal, $316,324 — multiple aged event champion), Copperish (Metallic Cat, $281,552 — multiple aged event champion), Halreycious (Dual Rey, $271K — sire of earners), and Areybe himself ($160K). The dam's grandget exceed $9M. LTE $160,250. Made the 2023 NCHA Futurity Open Finals alongside Cat — both are Stylish Play Lena offspring confirmed in the same Finals field by Quarter Horse News.

Valley Equine describes Areybe as offering breeders "a consistent Open-level performance record and one of the strongest female families in the cutting industry" and notes his "presence and athleticism that make you take notice" (Jeremy Barwick, Valley Equine owner). The cross of Stevie Rey Von on a Stylish Play Lena daughter is one of the most powerful combinations available in modern cutting — the #1 current sire on the #4 All-Time Leading Dam. Stylish Play Lena daughters are specifically mentioned by Stevie Rey Von management as among the most sought-after crosses for their stallion. First foal crops not yet of showing age as of 2025.

NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive and Chrome Cash enrolled. Stud fee $3,500. Owned by Linda Holmes. Standing at Valley Equine, Stephenville TX.
