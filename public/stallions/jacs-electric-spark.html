Full Profile

Jacs Electric Spark -- 1998 Sorrel Stallion. AQHA. By Shining Spark (Doc O'Lena x Jae Bar Ann -- NRHA Hall of Fame; NRHA $4 Million Sire; 1993 International Reining World Cup Open and Team Champion; AQHA Junior Reining World Champion; NRHA Derby Champion; Equi-Stat All-Time Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire ranking in top 10 of All-Time Leading Reining Sires; his sons and daughters have produced offspring earning in excess of $10.3 million; the Shining Spark line is one of the two most commercially dominant reining sire lines of the modern era alongside the Topsail Whiz and Gunner lines) out of Miss Hello Hollywood (Hollywood Jac 86 x Miss Doll Pine -- Hollywood Jac 86 is the NRHA Hall of Fame inductee and the first million dollar sire in NRHA history; Miss Hello Hollywood is a full sister to Chrome Plated Jac, the 1983 NRHA Non-Pro Reserve Champion, and to Jacs Little Pine with $35,000+ in NRHA earnings; her daughters and sons consistently produce NRHA money-earners confirming that the Hollywood Jac 86 x Miss Doll Pine dam combination carries exceptional genetic transmission for reining athleticism and competitive ability). Bred by Sally Brown, NRHA's first Dale Wilkinson Lifetime Achievement Award recipient.

Owned by Troy Heikes and Buck Hazledine. Oswood Stallion Station, 1400 Old Garner Road, Weatherford TX 76088. ICSI only. 2026 Stud fee: $5,000 ICSI.

International semen shipping available. Source: https://oswoodstallionstation.com/jacs-electric-spark/ LTE $85,907 confirmed NRHA. PE $10,300,000+ confirmed In Foal Partners -- producers with get earnings in excess of $16,000,000. NRHA $3 Million Sire milestone confirmed 2016 at the All American Quarter Horse Congress.

Confirmed show career highlights: 2001 NRHA Futurity Open finalist; 2002 NRBC Open finalist; 2002 NRHA Derby Open finalist; 2003 NRBC Open finalist; 2003 NRHA Derby Open finalist; 2004 NRBC Open finalist; 2004 NRHA Derby Open finalist. Consistent top-level open performer across seven consecutive futurity and derby seasons. Top offspring include Electric Code ($204,317 LTE; 2008 NRHA Futurity L3 Open Champion; No. 1 Reining Horse All Ages All Divisions at the 2008 NRHA Futurity; ridden by NRHA Professional Jared Leclair); REF Black Mamba ($121,754 LTE); Holier Then Thou ($111,027 LTE); Electric Cha Ching ($104,596 LTE); Steppin On Sparks ($102,207 LTE).

Electric Snow (osw012 in this database) is also among his money-earning offspring with LTE $122,000+ and PE $660,000+, confirming that Jacs Electric Spark's productive genetic line continues through multiple offspring who are themselves standing as breeding stallions today. The Shining Spark x Hollywood Jac 86 dam cross unites two of the foundational Hall of Fame sire lines in NRHA reining history. Shining Spark brought the athleticism, stop, and natural lope that defines elite reining competitors. Hollywood Jac 86, the first NRHA million dollar sire, brought the competitive fire, collection, and show intelligence that his descendants consistently transmit across generations.

Together these two Hall of Fame sire lines in a single individual produced $10.3M+ in offspring earnings across a broad base of performers, confirming that Jacs Electric Spark consistently transmitted elite genetics rather than relying on a small number of outlier performers. Breeders crossing Colonels Smoking Gun daughters or Gunnatrashya daughters on Jacs Electric Spark ICSI will access the Shining Spark x Hollywood Jac 86 foundation at a premium price point backed by one of the strongest PE records in the Oswood stallion roster.
