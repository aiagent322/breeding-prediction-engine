Full Profile

Down Right Amazing -- 2017 Stallion. AQHA & APHA. By Colonels Smoking Gun (Gunner -- NRHA Hall of Fame; NRHA $15 Million Sire; the only NRHA $15 Million Sire in history; sire of Gunnatrashya osw001 in this database and numerous other champions; his sons and daughters have dominated NRHA futurity, derby, and aged-event competition for over two decades; the Colonels Smoking Gun name is synonymous with elite NRHA competition at the highest level) out of Shesouttayourleague (dam of Down Right Amazing; her produce record confirms Down Right Amazing as her standout performer and one of the most accomplished reining horses in NRHA history). Bred and nominated by Curtis Performance Horses LLC.

Owned by DAG Ventures LLC. Oswood Stallion Station, 1400 Old Garner Road, Weatherford TX 76088. (817) 599-4560. Ridden throughout career by NRHA $5 Million Rider Casey Deary.

Source: https://oswoodstallionstation.com/down-right-amazing/ LTE $1,000,000+ confirmed -- the first reining horse in NRHA history to surpass $1 million in lifetime earnings. At the 2025 American Performance Horseman in Las Vegas, Down Right Amazing and Casey Deary delivered one of the most significant runs in the history of reining, scoring 229 to break the $1 million LTE barrier and make history as the sport's first million-dollar earner. Confirmed championship record: 2025 American Performance Horseman Reining Champion; 2024 NRBC Level 4 Open Classic Champion; 2024 American Performance Horseman Reining Champion; 2023 NRHA Derby Level 4 Open Champion; 2023 NRBC Level 4 Open Classic Champion; 2023 American Performance Horseman Reining Champion; 2022 The Run For A Million Reining Open Shootout Champion. The title of the first $1 million earner in NRHA history is not simply a financial milestone -- it is a definitive statement about the sustained excellence required to accumulate that level of earnings across multiple seasons and multiple premier events.

Down Right Amazing has won championships at the NRHA Derby, NRBC, The Run For A Million, and The American Performance Horseman -- four of the most prestigious and highest-paying events in professional reining. Winning the American Performance Horseman Reining Championship in three consecutive years (2022, 2023, 2024, 2025) is an achievement without precedent in the history of that event and reflects competitive consistency at a level that no other reining horse in history has matched across the same time period. His connection to Colonels Smoking Gun ($15 Million Sire) ensures that his offspring carry the stop, natural lope, and competitive athleticism that has defined the Gunner line for two decades. Colonels Smoking Gun's sons and daughters have won at every level of NRHA competition from novice horse to the Run For A Million open shootout, and Down Right Amazing represents the current peak of what that sire line can produce in a single individual.

His first foal crops are currently developing and will be watched closely by the entire reining world. Breeders at Oswood Stallion Station have access to the most accomplished active reining horse and unprecedented sire prospect in the sport today. There is no comparable stallion available at any price for breeders seeking the combination of elite pedigree, proven arena performance across multiple seasons, and NRHA historical significance that Down Right Amazing offers.
