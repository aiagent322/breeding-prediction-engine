Full Profile

Down Right Amazing -- 2017 Sorrel Stallion. AQHA & APHA. 14.2H. By Colonels Smoking Gun (Colonel Freckles x Smoke N Sparks -- NRHA Hall of Fame; #1 All-Time Reining Sire; PE $14,500,000+; sire of Gunnatrashya osw001 in this database, 2009 NRHA Futurity and Derby Champion; his sons and daughters have won virtually every major NRHA title available and have produced the next generation of champions across the world) out of Shesouttayourleague (Walla Walla Whiz x -- Walla Walla Whiz is osw002 in this database, NRHA Futurity AND Derby Open Champion; Shesouttayourleague earned $250,000+ NRHA LTE including the 2015 NRHA Futurity Open Championship and the 2017 NRHA Derby Championship with NRHA $5 Million Rider Casey Deary; the Colonels Smoking Gun x Walla Walla Whiz cross through Shesouttayourleague pairs the #1 All-Time Reining Sire with a mare who won the two most prestigious events in NRHA competition).

Bred by Curtis Performance Horses LLC. Owned by DAG Ventures LLC, Crowley TX. Oswood Stallion Station, 1400 Old Garner Road, Weatherford TX 76088. Stud fee $3,500.

ICSI fee $2,500 + $675 farm fee. Source: https://oswoodstallionstation.com/down-right-amazing/ LTE $1,050,409 confirmed -- the first reining horse in history to surpass $1 million in lifetime earnings. The historic milestone was achieved at the 2025 American Performance Horseman in Fort Worth, Texas, where Down Right Amazing scored a 229 with Casey Deary aboard to win the reining competition for the third consecutive year. Confirmed full show record: 2020 NRHA Futurity Open 3rd place (Deary rode while recovering from a broken neck); 2022 NRHA Derby Open 1st place; 2022 The Run For A Million Open Shootout Champion; 2022 NRBC Level 4 Open Preliminary Round Champion; 2023 NRHA Derby Level 4 Open Champion (231.5 score); 2023 NRBC Level 4 Open Classic Champion; 2023 American Performance Horseman Reining Champion; 2024 American Performance Horseman Reining Champion (back-to-back); 2024 NRBC Level 4 Open Champion; 2025 American Performance Horseman Reining Champion (three-peat, $1M milestone).

Down Right Amazing is the most decorated horse in the Oswood Stallion Station database and one of the most accomplished reiners in history. Three consecutive American Performance Horseman Reining Championships against the best horses and riders from all three western performance disciplines is without precedent in the modern era of the sport. The NRHA Derby Level 4 Open Championship with a score of 231.5 -- one of the highest scores ever recorded in Derby Open Finals history -- confirms the level of athletic ability and competitive consistency that defines elite reining at the highest possible standard. His dam Shesouttayourleague is one of the most accomplished mares in NRHA history, winning both the Futurity and Derby Open -- an achievement matched by very few horses.

Down Right Amazing inherits the Colonels Smoking Gun power and athleticism from the top side and the Walla Walla Whiz lope quality and technical precision from the bottom. The combination has produced a horse that performs in the 229-231 scoring range across multiple seasons, events, and venues -- a consistency that no other horse in modern reining history has matched. Breeding shed career just beginning after his 2025 retirement from competition. First foal crops are highly anticipated across the NRHA world.

Breeders accessing Down Right Amazing at Oswood Stallion Station are working with the most historically significant reining performer of his generation.
