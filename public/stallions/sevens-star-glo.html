Sevens Star Glo -- 2017 Buckskin Stallion. By CSR Dual Glo (bvss044 -- 2015 AQHA World Champion Junior Heading; 371 AQHA points in multiple events; 2015 AQHA High Point Stallion Open Heeling; 2015 AQHA High Point Stallion Open Ranch Riding; 2014 NRCHA Celebration of Champions Intermediate Open and Limited Open Derby World Champion -- full profile bvss044) out of Sevens Tootsie Time (Hes Dun His Time x -- confirmed producer whose offspring are earning in both roping and ranch horse competition per Valley Equine 2026). Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Stud fee $1,500 fresh contracts only confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup. Foals eligible: Riata Buckle, IQHA Ranch Horse Program, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive, Pitzer Ranch HPI Eligible. NOT part of cutting Super Stallion Incentive. First foals 2025 -- no offspring show data yet. Sevens Star Glo is a son of Valley Equine's own CSR Dual Glo (bvss044), making this a father-son pair standing at the same station -- a pedigree connection that gives breeders continuity in the CSR Dual Glo/Dual Rey athletic versatile performance horse line. LTE $144,834 and 234.5 AQHA Points confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup. The LTE combines dollar earnings from team roping, ARHFA, and NRCHA competition with AQHA point accumulation across multiple events. Confirmed show record from Valley Equine 2026 lineup: AQHA World Champion Senior Heading; Reserve Superhorse at the AQHA World Show; 2x ARHFA Heeling Championships. Valley Equine 2026: 'Sevens Star Glo built a versatile show record highlighted by an AQHA World Championship in Senior Heading and a Reserve Superhorse title at the AQHA World Show. His success across multiple events including 2x ARHFA Heeling Championships, reflects athleticism, and durability in the arena.' The Reserve Superhorse title at the AQHA World Show is a significant honor -- it requires a horse to accumulate points across multiple AQHA disciplines in a single show, demonstrating true all-around athletic ability rather than single-event specialization. Combined with his AQHA World Championship in Senior Heading and ARHFA heeling titles, Sevens Star Glo's record demonstrates exceptional cross-discipline athletic talent anchored by the Dual Rey bloodline through his sire CSR Dual Glo.
