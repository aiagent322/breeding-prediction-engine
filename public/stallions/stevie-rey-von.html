Performance Summary

Stevie Rey Von — 2012 Red Roan, 14.2H. By Metallic Cat (High Brow Cat x Chers Shadow/Peptoboonsmal — LTE $637,711, 2008 NCHA Open Futurity Champion, 2009 NCHA Horse of the Year, NCHA Hall of Fame, NRCHA Hall of Fame, PE $83M+, QData and NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Sire, QData and NRCHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire — led NCHA sire standings every year 2016-2024) out of Miss Ella Rey (Dual Rey x Huggs Olena/Smart Chic Olena). Miss Ella Rey was bred by LKC Ranch in Longmont, Colorado. She earned $209,491 in competition: 2011 NCHA Super Stakes Limited Non-Pro Champion, 2013 and 2014 AQHA Amateur Cutting World Champion. Huggs Olena, Miss Ella Rey's dam, is by Smart Chic Olena — the #1 All-Time Leading NRCHA Sire with PE $11M+. LTE $420,559. PE $20M+ (2025). 2015 NCHA Open Futurity Champion. NCHA Hall of Fame. #1 QData Leading Sire 2025 across all disciplines. The only living stallion in NCHA Open Futurity history to be an NCHA Open Futurity Champion, sired by an NCHA Open Futurity Champion, and to have sired an NCHA Open Futurity Champion. In 2022 was leading sire at both the NCHA Futurity AND the NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity in the same year — the only stallion other than Metallic Cat to accomplish this. NRCHA $3M+ sire. Offspring PE average $14,244 per horse — 40% higher than sire Metallic Cat. 56 NCHA Futurity earners from 31 different dam sires confirms he crosses on everything. 365 foals born in 2023 alone — full book with waiting list into 2027. HERDA negative (6 Panel N/N) — can cross safely on all High Brow Cat and Metallic Cat mares. Offspring consistently described as: "incredibly gentle and effortless to handle," "requires minimal riding and training," "so accurate and never takes a foot out of place," "incredibly smart." Trainer Beau Galyean: "The other thing that Stevie offers that's so huge is that he's negative for HERDA. So all the mares out there that have HERDA, they now have a Cat-related stud to go to that doesn't carry the HERDA gene." Top offspring: Janie Wood (2021 NCHA Futurity Open Champion, $311K), Swing Wide ($353K, standing at Valley Equine), Tornado Jonez (2022 NRCHA SBF Open + Intermediate Open Champion, $266K), Hottie Rey Von (2024 BI Derby Open Champion, $224K), Smart Von Lena ($454K). Now owned by Jason and Kisha Itkin of Theorem Ranch. Stud fee $10,000. Beau Galyean Stallion Services, Weatherford TX.
