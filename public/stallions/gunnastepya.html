Gunnastepya -- 2016 Gray Stallion. By Gunnatrashya (Colonels Smoking Gun x Natrasha/Trashadeous -- LTE $236,457; 2009 NRHA Open Futurity Champion; 2009 Congress Open Futurity Champion; 2010 NRHA Open Derby Champion; NRHA Hall of Fame; sire earnings $18M+; standing at Oswood Stallion Station as osw001 in this database -- Gunnastepya's sire stands at the same station, a father-son pair within the Oswood program) out of SDP A Lasting Step (Wimpys Little Step x -- Wimpys Little Step is a leading NRHA sire and NRHA Hall of Fame inductee with PE $15M+; standing at Silver Spurs Equine as ss007 in this database; SDP A Lasting Step's dam line confirms elite NRHA producing female family). Owned by DAG Ventures LLC. Oswood Stallion Station, Weatherford TX. LTE $283,898 confirmed Oswood roster. Transported cooled and frozen semen available. Source: https://www.oswood.com/stallions/gunnastepya/ Confirmed show record from Oswood records and NRHA/NRBC results: 2023 NRBC L4 Non-Pro Champion; 2023 Cactus Classic L4 Open Derby Champion; 2023 Cactus Classic TRFAM Qualifier Champion; 2023 The Run For A Million 3rd; 2022 NRBC L4 Non-Pro Reserve Champion. LTE $283,898 confirmed. Gunnastepya's 2023 season was exceptional across multiple major reining events simultaneously. The NRBC L4 Non-Pro Championship demonstrated elite trainability and rideability for non-professional riders -- one of the most commercially valuable traits a breeding stallion can possess. The Cactus Classic L4 Open Derby Championship in the same year confirmed that the same horse could compete at the top of the open division against professional riders, a rare combination of non-pro rideability and open-level athletic talent. The Cactus Classic TRFAM Qualifier Championship and 3rd-place finish at The Run For A Million -- one of the richest equestrian events in history -- in 2023 completed a season that placed Gunnastepya among the top reining horses in the world at his peak. The pedigree carries exceptional depth. Gunnatrashya as sire (NRHA Hall of Fame; 2009 NRHA Futurity and Derby Champion; $18M+ sire earnings; osw001) makes Gunnastepya a direct continuation of the Colonels Smoking Gun/Colonel Freckles sire line into the next generation. Wimpys Little Step as dam sire (NRHA Hall of Fame; PE $15M+; ss007 at Silver Spurs Equine in this database) adds one of the sport's most celebrated trainability and stop-power sire lines. The Gunnatrashya x Wimpys Little Step cross pairs two Hall of Fame sire lines -- father and dam's sire both Hall of Fame inductees -- creating a pedigree capable of producing both the non-pro rideability and the open-level athletic performance that Gunnastepya has demonstrated in competition.
