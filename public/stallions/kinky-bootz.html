Performance Summary

Kinky Bootz -- 2016 Sorrel Stallion. By Dual Rey (QData and NCHA #3 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, PE $57.9M+, AQHA Hall of Fame -- full profile bvss001) out of Sheza Stylish Cougar (High Brow Cat -- LTE $57,040; Kinky Bootz confirmed as her first money-earner per NCHA Southern Futurity announcement October 2020; by High Brow Cat, QData/NCHA #2 All-Time Leading Cutting Sire, PE $97M+ -- full profile bvss002). The cross of Dual Rey on a High Brow Cat mare is one of the most proven pedigree combinations in modern cutting: Dont Stopp Believin (Dual Rey x High Brow Cat mare, LTE $463,962, PE $5.9M+, NCHA Hall of Fame, 2025 QData Top 12 Leading Sire) and Reyzin The Cash (Dual Rey x Spookys Cash, PE $11M+) are notable recent examples of this cross. Bred and owned by Glenn and Debbie Drake, Weatherford TX. Shown by Austin Shepard, NCHA Hall of Fame earner of over $8.5 million -- one of the most decorated riders in cutting history with five Southern Futurity 4-Year-Old Open titles. Entered stud 2024; first foals are two-year-olds in 2026. Stud fee $2,500. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Semen availability: cooled/fresh (GREEN designation on Valley Equine Breeding Calendar). LTE $182,221. Compiled three major event championships in 2020 alone. Confirmed full show record: 2019 The Ike Open Derby Champion (scored 216 and 222 in go-rounds with Austin Shepard -- confirmed Austin Shepard Cutting Horses, described as 'back-to-back triumphs' by Shepard following Countess Catrina victory); 2020 Southern Futurity 4-Year-Old Open Champion (scored 225 with Austin Shepard, October 27, 2020, in Weatherford TX; NCHA reported 'this was Austin Shepard's fifth Southern Futurity 4-Year-Old championship win,' and described the win as 'the third major 2020 win for Kinky Bootz'; five of 19 finalists scored 220+ points, Hit Tha Flo marked 224 for reserve, and Jae Zee scored 223.5 for third; NCHA confirmed Kinky Bootz 'is the first money earner out of the High Brow Cat daughter Sheza Stylish Cougar LTE $57,040'); 2020 Arbuckle Mountain Open Classic split 2nd/3rd (scored 220.5 with Austin Shepard, tied with Bama Jelly also ridden by Shepard -- confirmed NCHA February 2021; KR Isadorable won the class with Lloyd Cox). At least two additional major wins in 2020 preceded the Southern Futurity title per NCHA description of 'third major 2020 win.' Valley Equine 2026: 'His own record was built in the aged events, showing consistency and competitiveness against strong fields, and he comes from a family known for producing cutting earners generation after generation. With cow on the top side and a proven female line underneath, Kinky Bootz offers a young sire option grounded in ability and backed by depth on paper.' No offspring yet showing -- first foals are two-year-olds in 2026, and the Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive program is in place to support early believers. Eligible for Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive, NCHA Super Stakes, Breeders Invitational, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive, and PCCHA.
