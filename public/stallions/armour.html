Performance Summary

Armour — 2021 Red Roan. By Metallic Rebel (Metallic Cat x Sweet Abra/Abrakadabracre — LTE $428,271+, 2017 NCHA Horse of the Year, NCHA Hall of Fame, NCHA World Champion Stallion, PE $16.6M+, QData #2 Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire 2025, NRCHA Million Dollar Sire 2024; management and handlers describe him as "such an athlete, amazing mind and is so cowy") out of Coureygous (Dual Rey x Highbrow Supercat/High Brow Cat). Metallic Rebel's sire Metallic Cat carries High Brow Cat athleticism and Peptoboonsmal mental toughness — the combination that defined the modern era of western performance horses. Metallic Rebel himself first competed in NRCHA Snaffle Bit Futurity with offspring in 2022, and by 2023 had 6 making the Open finals — an exceptional debut for the discipline. Coureygous: LTE $250,000+, NCHA Non-Pro Horse of the Year, Co-Champion NCHA Non-Pro Futurity 2019 (shown by Kristen Galyean in her own right as a top-20 NCHA Non-Pro Hall of Fame earner at $840,721), Bonanza Non-Pro Derby Champion. Coureygous is a full sibling to Lil Rattler (dam of Third Edge — multi-NCHA Triple Crown winner and $169K+ earner now owned by Teton Ridge, purchased at major sale — and Nineteen 42), Button Down Supercat ($289,596 by Dual Rey), and Cina De Maya (dam of Metallic Curveball). This Galyean-bred female family is one of the most successful in the cutting industry. Coureygous's dam Highbrow Supercat is by High Brow Cat out of Holly Dolly Too (Colonel Freckles): LTE $364,690, 2005 NCHA Futurity Open Champion (one of only 8 in event history), dam of producers earning $1.4M+. Armour thus has Metallic Cat through his sire, High Brow Cat through his dam's sire (Dual Rey) and through his granddam Highbrow Supercat — three separate layers of elite performance genetics. LTE $379,775. Still competing in 2026. Bred and owned by the Galyean family — Wesley Galyean (2023 NCHA Futurity Champion) and Kristen Galyean trained and showed both Armour and his dam Coureygous for owner Joel Colgrove Sr. Full confirmed show record: Breeders Invitational Open Derby Champion, Split 5th NCHA Open Futurity, 3rd NCHA Non-Pro Derby, 8th American Performance Horseman Cutting Classic, Top 10 Brazos Bash Open Derby. Valley Equine: "His pedigree ties directly to stallions that have shaped this program, and his performance record speaks for itself." First breeding season at Valley Equine 2026 — no offspring of showing age. NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive and Chrome Cash enrolled. Stud fee $4,000. Standing at Valley Equine, Stephenville TX.
