Performance Summary

Swing Wide -- 2019 Blue Roan Stallion. By Stevie Rey Von (Metallic Cat x Miss Ella Rey/Dual Rey -- LTE $420,559; 2015 NCHA Open Futurity Champion; NCHA Hall of Fame; PE $20M+; 6-panel N/N including HERDA Negative; 2026 breeding fee $10,000 at Beau Galyean Stallion Services -- full profile add003) out of Miss Woody Two Shoes (High Brow Cat x -- producer of $1.1M+ in earning offspring confirmed Valley Equine 2026; Swing Wide is her most accomplished offspring). Owned by Nancy and Steve Martin. Shown by Cullen Chartier. Stud fee $3,000 confirmed Valley Equine 2026 lineup. First foals born 2025. Foals eligible: Valley Equine Super Stallion Incentive, NCHA Super Stakes, NRCHA Cow Horse Incentive, PCCHA, Breeders Invitational, Chrome Cash. Valley Equine, Stephenville TX. Swing Wide is Stevie Rey Von's highest-earning offspring overall -- confirmed Western Life Today: 'Stevie's highest money-earner, Swing Wide (out of Miss Woody Two Shoes, by High Brow Cat), came from his second foal crop.' He is also listed first and prominently on the official steviereyvon.com offspring page. This distinction gives breeders confidence that Swing Wide concentrates the best of Stevie Rey Von's genetics. Miss Woody Two Shoes by High Brow Cat creates a Metallic Cat / High Brow Cat pedigree cross through Stevie Rey Von (Metallic Cat son) and the dam (High Brow Cat daughter) -- a cross that has consistently produced elite performers across cutting and cow horse disciplines. LTE $353,399 (Valley Equine 2026 confirmed). Confirmed show record: Tied 3rd 2022 NCHA Open Futurity with Cullen Chartier -- earned $138,237 confirmed QHN Stevie Rey Von leading sire article: 'His leading earner at the NCHA Futurity was Swing Wide, who tied for third with trainer Cullen Chartier and earned $138,237.' Western Life Today: 'The stallion tied for third in the Open at the 2022 NCHA Futurity.' 2026 NCHA Futurity Finalist -- confirmed Stallion Register Directory. Stevie Rey Von Incentive Champion -- confirmed Stallion Register Directory and steviereyvon.com. Consistent aged event success through 5YO and 6YO seasons. Valley Equine 2026: 'Swing Wide brings a performance record built through consistent aged event success, earning over $353,000 before beginning his breeding career.
